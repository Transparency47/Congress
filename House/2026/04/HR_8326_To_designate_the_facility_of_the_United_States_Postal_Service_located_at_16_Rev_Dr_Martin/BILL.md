A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 16 Rev Dr Martin Luther King Jr Drive in Willingboro, New Jersey, as the James A. Cotten Post Office .
#### 1. James A. Cotten Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 16 Rev Dr Martin Luther King Jr Drive in Willingboro, New Jersey, shall be known and designated as the James A. Cotten Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the James A. Cotten Post Office .
