To amend the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 to extend the time period for which certain regulations concerning the North Atlantic right whale are effective.
#### 1. Extension of effectiveness of certain regulations concerning North Atlantic right whale
Section 101 of division JJ of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 ( 16 U.S.C. 1387 note) is amended by striking 2028 each place it appears and inserting 2035 .
