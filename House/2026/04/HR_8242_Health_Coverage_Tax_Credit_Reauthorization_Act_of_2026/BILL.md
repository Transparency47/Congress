April 9, 2026 Mr. Turner of Ohio (for himself, Ms. McDonald Rivet , Ms. Tenney , and Ms. Stevens ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means
A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the health coverage tax credit.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Health Coverage Tax Credit Reauthorization Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Extension of health coverage tax credit
##### (a) In general
Section 35(b)(1)(B) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking before January 1, 2022 and inserting before January 1, 2030 .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to months beginning after December 31, 2021.
