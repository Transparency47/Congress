IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 21, 2026 Mr. Deluzio (for himself and Mr. Nehls ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To amend the Railroad Retirement Act of 1974 to eliminate certain deductions for annuities under the Act.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Railroad Retirement Fairness Act .
#### 2. Eliminating certain deductions for annuities under the Railroad Retirement Act of 1974
Section 2(f) of the Railroad Retirement Act of 1974 ( 45 U.S.C. 231a(f) ) is amended by striking subdivision (6).
