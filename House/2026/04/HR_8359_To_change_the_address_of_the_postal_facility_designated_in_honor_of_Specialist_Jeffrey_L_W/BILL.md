A BILL
To change the address of the postal facility designated in honor of Specialist Jeffrey L. White, Jr.
#### 1. Specialist Jeffrey L. White, Jr. Post Office Redesignation
##### (a) Redesignation
Section 1(a) of Public Law 115–134 (132 Stat. 340) is amended by striking 324 and inserting 211 .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in section 1 of Public Law 115–134 , as amended by subsection (a), shall be deemed to be a reference to the Specialist Jeffrey L. White, Jr. Post Office .
