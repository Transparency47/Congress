IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1225
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 29, 2026 Mr. Green of Texas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Commending the Islamic Republic of Pakistan for its efforts to facilitate peace between the United States and Israel with Iran.
#### 1. Short Title
This resolution may be cited as the Original Resolution Commending the Islamic Republic of Pakistan for its efforts to facilitate peace between the United States and Israel with Iran .
#### 2. Commending the Islamic Republic of Pakistan
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
acknowledges and commends the Islamic Republic of Pakistan for its efforts to facilitate a lasting peace between the United States and Israel with Iran.
