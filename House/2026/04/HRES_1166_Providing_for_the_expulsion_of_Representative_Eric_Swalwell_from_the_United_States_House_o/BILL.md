IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1166
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 13, 2026 Mrs. Luna submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Providing for the expulsion of Representative Eric Swalwell from the United States House of Representatives.
That pursuant to article I, section 5, clause 2 of the Constitution of the United States, Representative Eric Swalwell, be, and he hereby is, expelled from the United States House of Representatives.
