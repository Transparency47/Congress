A BILL
To amend title 49, United States Code, to allow certain funds to be used for incremental costs of incorporating art into facilities, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Saving Transit Art Resources Act or the STAR Act .
#### 2. Allowing art
##### (a) In general
Section 5323(h) of title 49, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (1) by inserting or after the semicolon;
**(2)**
by striking paragraph (2); and
**(3)**
by redesignating paragraph (3) as paragraph (2).
##### (b) No special rule
Section 5309 of such title is amended by—
**(1)**
striking subsection (p); and
**(2)**
redesignating subsection (q) as subsection (p).
