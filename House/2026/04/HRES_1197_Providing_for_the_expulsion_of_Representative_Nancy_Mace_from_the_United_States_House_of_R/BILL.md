IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1197
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 20, 2026 Mr. Mills submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Providing for the expulsion of Representative Nancy Mace from the United States House of Representatives.
That pursuant to Article I, Section 5, Clause 2 of the Constitution of the United States, Representative Nancy Mace, be, and she hereby is, expelled from the United States House of Representatives.
