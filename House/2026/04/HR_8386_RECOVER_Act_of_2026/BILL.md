A BILL
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to eliminate the payment reduction for certain multiple therapy services under the Medicare program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Removing Excessive Cuts to Outpatient and Vital Essential Rehabilitation Act of 2026 or the RECOVER Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Eliminating the payment reduction for certain multiple therapy services under the Medicare program
##### (a) Physician fee schedule
Section 1848(b)(7) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395w–4(b)(7) ) is amended, in the second sentence, by inserting (or, in the case of such services furnished on or after January 1, 2027, 0 percent) after 50 percent .
##### (b) Outpatient therapy services and comprehensive outpatient rehabilitation services
Section 1834(k)(7) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395m(k)(7) ) is amended by inserting (or, in the case of such services furnished on or after January 1, 2027, 0 percent) after 50 percent .
