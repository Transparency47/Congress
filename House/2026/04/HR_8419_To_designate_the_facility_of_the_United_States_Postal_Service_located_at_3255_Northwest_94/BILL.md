A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3255 Northwest 94th Avenue in Coral Springs, Florida, as the Nancy Metayer Post Office Building .
#### 1. Nancy Metayer Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3255 Northwest 94th Avenue in Coral Springs, Florida, shall be known and designated as the Nancy Metayer Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Nancy Metayer Post Office Building .
