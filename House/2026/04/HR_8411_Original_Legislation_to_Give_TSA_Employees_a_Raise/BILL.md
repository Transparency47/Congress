A BILL
To increase the annual rate of basic pay for career employees of the Transportation Security Administration, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Original Legislation to Give TSA Employees a Raise .
#### 2. TSA employee pay increase
##### (a) In general
Beginning on the first day of the applicable pay period—
**(1)**
the annual rate of basic pay for each career employee shall be increased by 15 percent; and
**(2)**
the annual rate of basic pay for each pay band under the Transportation Security Administration Core Compensation System shall be increased by 15 percent.
##### (b) Definitions
In this section:
**(1) Applicable pay period**
The term applicable pay period means the pay period that—
**(A)**
is the first pay period beginning on or after the date of the enactment of this Act; and
**(B)**
occurs prior to the beginning of the second pay period beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
**(2) Career employee**
The term career employee means any career agent or officer of the Transportation Security Administration who is not a senior executive, a career appointee, or a senior career employee (as such terms are defined in section 114(n)(2) of title 49, United States Code) of the Administration.
