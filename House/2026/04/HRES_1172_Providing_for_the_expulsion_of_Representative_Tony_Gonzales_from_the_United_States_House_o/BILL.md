119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1172
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 14, 2026 Ms. Leger Fernandez submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Providing for the expulsion of Representative Tony Gonzales from the United States House of Representatives.
That pursuant to article I, section 5, clause 2 of the Constitution of the United States, Representative Tony Gonzales, be, and he hereby is, expelled from the United States House of Representatives.
