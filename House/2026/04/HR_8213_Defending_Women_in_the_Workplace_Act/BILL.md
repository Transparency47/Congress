April 9, 2026 Mrs. Biggs of South Carolina (for herself, Mr. Harrigan , Mr. Ogles , Mr. Perry , Ms. Mace , Mr. Gosar , Ms. Letlow , Ms. Boebert , Mrs. Luna , and Mr. Norman ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To clarify that sex discrimination under title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 does not include discrimination on the basis of gender identity.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Defending Women in the Workplace Act .
#### 2. Rule of construction
For purposes of title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 ( 42 U.S.C. 2000e et seq. ), the terms because of sex and on the basis of sex do not include because of or on the basis of gender identity.
