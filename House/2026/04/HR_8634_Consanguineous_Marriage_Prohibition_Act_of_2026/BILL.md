119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. R. 8634
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 30, 2026 Mr. Self introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To prohibit marriages between individuals who are closely related by blood.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Consanguineous Marriage Prohibition Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Federal prohibition on consanguineous marriage
Section 7 of title 1, United States Code, is amended by striking 2 individuals and inserting 2 individuals who are not related by affinity or consanguinity within the degree of first cousin each place it appears.
