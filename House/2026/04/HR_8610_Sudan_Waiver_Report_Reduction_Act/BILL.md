April 30, 2026 Mr. Fallon (for himself and Mr. Walkinshaw ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To amend the Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of 2007 to require agency reports on any procurement waiver made by the President.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Sudan Waiver Report Reduction Act .
#### 2. Reporting requirement with respect to waivers granted under the Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of 2007
Section 6(c)(2) of the Sudan Accountability and Divestment Act of 2007 ( Public Law 110–174 ; 50 U.S.C. 1701 note) is amended to read as follows:
(2) Reporting requirement Not later than 30 days after the President grants a waiver under paragraph (1), the head of the executive agency concerned shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report on such waiver. .
