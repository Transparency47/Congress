119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1193
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 20, 2026 Ms. Mace submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Providing for the expulsion of Representative Cory Mills from the United States House of Representatives.
That pursuant to article I, section 5, clause 2 of the Constitution of the United States, Representative Cory Mills, be, and he hereby is, expelled from the United States House of Representatives.
