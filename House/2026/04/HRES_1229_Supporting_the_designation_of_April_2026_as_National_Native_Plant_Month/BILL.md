IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1229
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 29, 2026 Ms. Goodlander (for herself, Mr. Joyce of Ohio , Mr. Carter of Louisiana , Mr. Moylan , and Mr. Neguse ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of April 2026 as National Native Plant Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Native Plant Month ; and
**(2)**
recognizes the benefits of native plants to the environment and economy of the United States.
