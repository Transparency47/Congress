IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 7, 2026 Mr. Foster submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to the withdrawal of the rule relating to Fair Credit Reporting; Name-Only Matching Procedures .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to the withdrawal of the rule relating to Fair Credit Reporting; Name-Only Matching Procedures, 86 Fed. Reg. 62468 (November 10, 2021) (90 Fed. Reg. 20084 (May 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
