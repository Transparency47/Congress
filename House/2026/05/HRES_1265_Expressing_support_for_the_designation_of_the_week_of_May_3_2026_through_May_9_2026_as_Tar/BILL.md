IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1265
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 7, 2026 Mr. Peters (for himself, Mr. Bilirakis , Mr. Mullin , and Mr. Bean of Florida ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of the week of May 3, 2026, through May 9, 2026, as Tardive Dyskinesia Awareness Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of Tardive Dyskinesia Awareness Week ; and
**(2)**
encourages each individual in the United States to become better informed about and aware of tardive dyskinesia.
