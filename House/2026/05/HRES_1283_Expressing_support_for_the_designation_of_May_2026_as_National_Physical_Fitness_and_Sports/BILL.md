IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1283
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 13, 2026 Mr. Veasey (for himself, Ms. Barragán , Mr. Johnson of Georgia , Ms. Norton , Ms. Sewell , and Mr. Bacon ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of May 2026 as National Physical Fitness and Sports Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Physical Fitness and Sports Month ; and
**(2)**
supports efforts to educate the people of the United States on the importance of a healthy and nutritious lifestyle.
