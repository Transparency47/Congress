May 12, 2026 Mr. Self introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 to wind down the Filipino Veterans Equity Compensation Fund.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Compensation Fund Recrediting Act .
#### 2. Sunset of the Filipino Veterans Equity Compensation Fund
Section 1002 of the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 ( Public Law 111–5 ; 38 U.S.C. 107 note) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (l), by striking until expended and inserting until the date specified in subsection (m) ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(m) Sunset On January 1, 2027— (1) this section shall cease to have any effect; and (2) any amounts in the compensation fund shall be deposited into the general fund of the Treasury. .
