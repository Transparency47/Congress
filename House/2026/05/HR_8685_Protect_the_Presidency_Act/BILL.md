IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 7, 2026 Mr. Gooden (for himself and Mr. Steube ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend section 1751 of title 18, United States Code, to include the death penalty as punishment for an attempt to assassinate certain individuals.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protect the Presidency Act .
#### 2. Punishment for assassination of certain individuals
Section 1751(c) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting death or before imprisonment .
