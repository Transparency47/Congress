119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1326
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 29, 2026 Mr. Crow (for himself, Ms. Ocasio-Cortez , and Mr. Levin ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Denouncing corruption in all its forms.
That the House of Representatives denounces corruption in all its forms and opposes the implementation of policies that benefit special interests and corrupt politicians at the expense of the American people.
