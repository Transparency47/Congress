119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1294
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 15, 2026 Mrs. Dingell (for herself, Mr. Beyer , and Mr. Buchanan ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of May 15, 2026, as Endangered Species Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of Endangered Species Day ;
**(2)**
recognizes the intrinsic value of plants and wildlife as part of the United States environmental heritage and the invaluable role that the Endangered Species Act of 1973 ( 16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq. ) has played in safeguarding at-risk species; and
**(3)**
acknowledges the continued importance of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 ( 16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq. ) and other conservation measures to protect threatened species and endangered species and address the biodiversity crisis.
