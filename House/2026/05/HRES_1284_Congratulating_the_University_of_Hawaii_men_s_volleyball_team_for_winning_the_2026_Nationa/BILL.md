IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1284
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 14, 2026 Mr. Case (for himself and Ms. Tokuda ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the University of Hawaii men’s volleyball team for winning the 2026 National Collegiate Athletic Association Men’s Volleyball Championship.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the 2026 University of Hawaii men’s volleyball team for their hard work and dedication to the sport and for the excitement they bring to the State of Hawaii; and
**(2)**
congratulates the 2026 University of Hawaii men’s volleyball team on a successful season.
