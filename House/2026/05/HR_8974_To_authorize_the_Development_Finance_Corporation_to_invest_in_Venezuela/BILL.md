May 21, 2026 Mr. Issa (for himself and Ms. Salazar ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To authorize the Development Finance Corporation to invest in Venezuela.
#### 1. Authorization to invest in Venezuela
##### (a) Removal as country of concern
Section 1402(3) of the BUILD Act of 2018 ( 22 U.S.C. 9601(3) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking subparagraph (A); and
**(2)**
by redesignating subparagraphs (B) through (G) as subparagraphs (A) through (F), respectively.
##### (b) Investment authority
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Development Finance Corporation may invest in the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela.
