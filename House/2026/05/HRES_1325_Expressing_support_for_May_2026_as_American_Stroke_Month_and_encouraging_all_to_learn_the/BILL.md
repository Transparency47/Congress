119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1325
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 29, 2026 Mrs. Beatty (for herself and Mr. Cohen ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for May 2026 as American Stroke Month and encouraging all to learn the warning signs of stroke, understand their personal risk factors, and take action to improve stroke prevention, response, and recovery in our communities.
That the House of Representatives expresses support for American Stroke Month and encourages all to learn the warning signs of stroke, understand their personal risk factors, and take action to improve stroke prevention, response, and recovery in our communities.
