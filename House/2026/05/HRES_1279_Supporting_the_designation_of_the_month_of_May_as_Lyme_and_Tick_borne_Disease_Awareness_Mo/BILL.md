119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1279
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 13, 2026 Mr. Smith of New Jersey (for himself, Mr. Doggett , Mr. Fitzpatrick , Mr. Gottheimer , and Mr. Mackenzie ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of the month of May as Lyme and Tick-borne Disease Awareness Month .
That the House of Representatives supports the designation of Lyme and Tick-borne Disease Awareness Month and continues to champion efforts to spread awareness of and develop better clinical responses to Lyme disease and other tick-borne diseases.
