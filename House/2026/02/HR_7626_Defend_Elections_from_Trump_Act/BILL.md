A BILL
To prohibit the use of funds to order Federal law enforcement or military personnel to polling places or other domestic election locations, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Defend Elections from Trump Act .
#### 2. Definitions
##### (a) In general
In this Act:
**(1) Election**
The term election has the meaning given the term in section 301(1) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 ( 52 U.S.C. 30101(1) )
#### 3. Prohibition on Federal law enforcement, intelligence, or military personnel at domestic election locations
##### (a) In general
No funds appropriated or otherwise made available for the Federal Government may be obligated or expended during the period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act and ending on December 31, 2026, for ordering, bringing, or keeping Federal law enforcement, intelligence, or military personnel acting under color of law at polling places, election offices, or locations where vote canvassing, counting, or certification occurs, or other locations where an election is held, unless Congress has enacted specific statutory authorization for such a presence at a domestic location.
##### (b) Exception
The prohibition under subsection (a) shall not apply to any individual exercising the right of suffrage in any district to which he or she may belong, if otherwise qualified according to the laws of the State of such district.
