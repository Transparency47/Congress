A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1225 Kempsville Road in Virginia Beach, Virginia, as the Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building .
#### 1. Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1225 Kempsville Road in Virginia Beach, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Colonel Edward Shames Post Office Building .
