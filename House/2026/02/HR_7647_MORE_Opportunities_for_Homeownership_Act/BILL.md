A BILL
To amend the Federal Home Loan Bank Act to expand homeownership access, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Minimizing Outdated Restrictions that Exclude Opportunities for Homeownership Act or the MORE Opportunities for Homeownership Act .
#### 2. Expanding homeownership access through community financial institutions
