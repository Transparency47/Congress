A BILL
To amend the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act to include Guam.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Parity for Pacific Radiation Survivors Act .
#### 2. Atmospheric testing in the pacific
Section 4(b)(1) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act ( Public Law 101–426 ; 42 U.S.C. 2210 note) is amended—
**(1)**
in subparagraph (A)—
**(A)**
in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking subparagraph (B) and inserting subparagraphs (B) and (C) ;
**(B)**
in clause (ii), by striking and at the end; and
**(C)**
by adding at the end the following:
(iv) the territory of Guam; ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following:
(C) with respect to a claim by an individual under subsection (a)(1)(A)(i)(IV) or (a)(2)(D), only Guam. .
#### 3. Leukemia claims relating to tests in the pacific
Section 4(a)(1)(A) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act ( Public Law 101–426 ; 42 U.S.C. 2210 note) is amended—
**(1)**
in clause (i)—

**(B)**
by redesignating subclause (IV) as subclause (VI); and
**(C)**
