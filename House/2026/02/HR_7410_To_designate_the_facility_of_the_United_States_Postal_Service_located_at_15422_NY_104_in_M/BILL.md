A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 15422 NY 104 in Martville, New York, as the Sergeant Staret J. Engleston Memorial Post Office Building .
#### 1. Sergeant Staret J. Engleston Memorial Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 15422 NY 104 in Martville, New York, as the Sergeant Staret J. Engleston Memorial Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Sergeant Staret J. Engleston Memorial Post Office Building .
