IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1036
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 4, 2026 Mr. Bera (for himself and Mr. Flood ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the annual designation of the first Saturday after the Spring Equinox as National Day of Play .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
designates a ‘‘National Day of Play’’;
**(2)**
expresses support for the annual designation of a ‘‘National Day of Play’’;
**(3)**
recognizes the importance of social connection for mental, physical, and social development; and
**(4)**
encourages people to put their electronics down and play.
