A BILL
To amend the Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act and Dingell-Johnson Sport Fish Restoration Act to treat the District of Columbia as a State for the purposes of such Acts, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act and Dingell-Johnson Sport Fish Restoration Act District of Columbia Equality Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Treatment of District of Columbia under Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act and Dingell-Johnson Sport Fish Restoration Act
##### (a) Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act
The Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act ( 16 U.S.C. 669 et seq. ) is amended—
**(1)**
in section 2 ( 16 U.S.C. 669a )—
**(A)**
by redesignating paragraphs (6) through (11) as paragraphs (7) through (12), respectively; and
**(B)**
by inserting after paragraph (5) the following:
(6) the term State means each of the several States and the District of Columbia; ; and
**(2)**
in section 4 ( 16 U.S.C. 669c )—
**(A)**
in subsection (b), by striking several ;
**(B)**
in subsection (d)(1)(A), by striking District of Columbia and to the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, each and inserting Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, ; and
**(C)**
in subsection (e)(5), by striking the District of Columbia, .
##### (b) Dingell-Johnson Sport Fish Restoration Act
The Dingell-Johnson Sport Fish Restoration Act ( 16 U.S.C. 777 et seq. ) is amended—
**(1)**
in section 2 ( 16 U.S.C. 777a )—
**(A)**
by striking and at the end of paragraph (2);
**(B)**
by striking the period at the end of paragraph (3) and inserting ; and ; and
**(C)**
by adding at the end the following:
(4) the term State means each of the several States and the District of Columbia. ;
**(2)**
in section 4(c)(1) ( 16 U.S.C. 777c(c)(1) ), by striking several ; and
**(3)**
in section 12 ( 16 U.S.C. 777k )—
**(A)**
in the first sentence—
**(i)**
by striking the Mayor of the District of Columbia, ;
**(ii)**
by striking the District of Columbia, ; and
**(iii)**
by striking for the District of Columbia one-third of 1 per centum, ; and
**(B)**
in the second sentence, by striking the District of Columbia, .
