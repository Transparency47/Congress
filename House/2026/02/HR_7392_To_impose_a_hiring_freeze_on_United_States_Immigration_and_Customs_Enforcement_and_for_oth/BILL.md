A BILL
To impose a hiring freeze on United States Immigration and Customs Enforcement, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Hiring freeze for U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement
##### (a) In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, beginning on the date of the enactment of this section, no Federal funds may be obligated or expended to—
**(1)**
appoint any individual to a position within United States Immigration and Customs Enforcement; or
**(2)**
detail or transfer any Federal employee to United States Immigration and Customs Enforcement.
##### (b) Application
Subsection (a) shall cease to apply on the date following the date of the enactment of this section that Congress enacts into law a provision that provides, by specific reference to subsection (a) of this section, that such subsection is superseded, overridden, or otherwise modified.
