119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1041
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 5, 2026 Mr. Thompson of Pennsylvania (for himself, Mr. Bishop , Mr. Sessions , Mr. Bacon , Mr. Ellzey , Mr. Walkinshaw , and Mr. Kennedy of Utah ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of Scouting America Day in celebration of its 116th anniversary.
That the House of Representatives supports the redesignation of a Scouting America Day in celebration of the 116th anniversary of its incorporation.
