119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1066
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 13, 2026 Mr. Foster (for himself, Mr. Jackson of Illinois , Ms. Kelly of Illinois , Mrs. Ramirez , Mr. García of Illinois , Mr. Quigley , Mr. Casten , Mr. Davis of Illinois , Mr. Krishnamoorthi , Ms. Schakowsky , Mr. Schneider , Ms. Budzinski , Ms. Underwood , and Mr. Sorensen ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing the condolences of the House of Representatives and honoring the memory of the victims of the mass shooting in Aurora, Illinois, on February 15, 2019.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses its sincere condolences to the families, friends, and loved ones of those who were killed in the tragic shooting on February 15, 2019, in Aurora, Illinois: Russell Beyer, Vicente Juarez, Clayton Parks, Josh Pinkard, and Trevor Wehner;
**(2)**
extends its support and prayers to those who were wounded or injured and wishes them a speedy recovery;
**(3)**
commends the law enforcement officers, emergency responders, and medical personnel who responded to the shooting with professionalism, dedication, and bravery;
**(4)**
expresses its support for the Aurora community in this difficult time; and
**(5)**
stands in solidarity with the victims of senseless gun violence in communities across the United States.
