February 25, 2026 Mr. Alford introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary , and in addition to the Committee on Education and Workforce , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To amend the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996 to require that certain nonprofits comply with E-Verify.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protect our Ballots Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Certain nonprofits required to comply with E-Verify
Section 402(e) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996 is amended by adding at the end the following:
(4) Certain nonprofits Any entity described under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 and exempt from taxation under section 501(a) of such Code, if that entity pays any individual for the provision of personal services for any political purpose or to take an active part in political management or a political campaign, shall elect to participate in the E-Verify program under section 403(a), and shall comply with the terms and conditions of such an election. .
