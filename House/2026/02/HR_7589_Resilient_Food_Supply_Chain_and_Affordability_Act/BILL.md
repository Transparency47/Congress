A BILL
To direct the Secretary of Agriculture to continue to carry out the Resilient Food Systems Infrastructure program, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Resilient Food Supply Chain and Affordability Act .
#### 2. Resilient Food Systems Infrastructure program
The Secretary of Agriculture shall continue to carry out the Resilient Food Systems Infrastructure program established pursuant to section 1001(b)(4) of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 ( Public Law 117–2 ), in the same manner as such program is carried out as of the date of the enactment of this Act, except that beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, recipients of assistance under such program may use funding received through such program for activities relating to meat and poultry products.
