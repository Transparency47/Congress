IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 4, 2026 Ms. Scholten (for herself and Mr. Mast ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to reauthorize certain programs relating to nonpoint source management, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Local Water Protection Act .
#### 2. Nonpoint source management programs
Section 319(j) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1329(j) ) is amended by striking for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2027 and inserting for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2031 .
