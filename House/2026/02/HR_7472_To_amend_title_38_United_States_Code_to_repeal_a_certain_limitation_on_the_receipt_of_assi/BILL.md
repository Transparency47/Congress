A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to repeal a certain limitation on the receipt of assistance under both the Department of Veterans Affairs Veteran Readiness and Employment program and Department of Veterans Affairs educational assistance programs.
#### 1. Repeal of certain limitation on receipt of assistance under both Department of Veterans Affairs Veteran Readiness and Employment program and Department of Veterans Affairs educational assistance programs
Section 3695 of title 38, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
by striking subsection (b); and
**(2)**
by redesignating subsection (c) as subsection (b).
