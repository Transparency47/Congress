A BILL
To amend Public Law 87–788 (commonly known as the McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act; 16 U.S.C. 582a–7 ) to provide for equal treatment of the District of Columbia with respect to funds made available under that Act.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the McIntire-Stennis Act District of Columbia Equality Act .
#### 2. Inclusion of District of Columbia for purposes of funding under McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act
Section 8 of Public Law 87–788 (commonly known as the McIntire-Stennis Cooperative Forestry Act; 16 U.S.C. 582a–7 ) is amended by inserting the District of Columbia, after include .
