IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1098
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 3, 2026 Mr. Morelle (for himself, Mr. Bacon , Mr. Tonko , Mr. Gottheimer , and Ms. Norton ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of March 3, 2026, as National Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Day .
That the House of Representatives supports the designation of National Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Day to create awareness of the devastating effect of these cancers on families in the United States and the need for strong action to eradicate triple-negative breast cancer.
