IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1133
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 24, 2026 Mr. Kean (for himself and Mr. Gottheimer ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Designating November 7 of each year as Shwachman Diamond Syndrome Awareness Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
Supports the designation of Shwachman Diamond Syndrom Awareness Day to increase awareness of this heritable rare genetic disorder and its impace on the residents of this nation, and to promote further research into effective treatments for this disease.
**(2)**
This act shall take effect immediately.
