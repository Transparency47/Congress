March 4, 2026 Mr. Taylor (for himself, Mr. Landsman , Mr. Jordan , Mr. Latta , Mr. Rulli , Mr. Miller of Ohio , Mr. Davidson , Mr. Turner of Ohio , Ms. Brown , Mr. Balderson , Mr. Joyce of Ohio , and Mr. Carey ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 East Grant Avenue in Georgetown, Ohio, as the Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building .
#### 1. Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 East Grant Avenue in Georgetown, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building .
