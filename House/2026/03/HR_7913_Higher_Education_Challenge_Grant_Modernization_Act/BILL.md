A BILL
To amend the Food and Agriculture Act of 1977 to reauthorize grants and fellowships for food and agricultural sciences education, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Higher Education Challenge Grant Modernization Act .
#### 2. Grants and fellowships for food and agricultural sciences education
##### (a) Priorities
Section 1417(c) of the Food and Agriculture Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3152(c) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (1), by striking and at the end;
**(2)**
in paragraph (2), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following:
(3) applications for teaching enhancement projects, including paid work-based learning, that address a need for additional trained professionals in— (A) food and agricultural sciences; or (B) rural economic, community, and business development. .
##### (b) Authorization of appropriations
Section 1417(m) of the Food and Agriculture Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3152(m) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (1), by striking and at the end;
**(2)**
in paragraph (2), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following:
(3) $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2035. .
