IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1101
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 4, 2026 Mrs. Luna submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Tony Gonzales of Texas.
That—
**(1)**
the House of Representatives hereby censures Representative Tony Gonzales of Texas for conduct that has brought discredit upon the House;
**(2)**
Representative Tony Gonzales shall present himself in the well of the House for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
this resolution be entered into the Journal of the House as an expression of the House’s condemnation of the conduct described herein.
