119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1091
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 2, 2026 Ms. Houlahan (for herself and Mr. Obernolte ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for declaring 2026 the Year of Math in the United States.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the declaration of a Year of Math in the United States;
**(2)**
celebrates the United States being selected as host of the International Congress of Mathematicians 2026 and using this platform to increase the visibility of mathematical sciences as fields of study and to communicate the myriad benefits of education in mathematics and statistics;
**(3)**
recognizes that mathematics underpins all of the STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) disciplines, which are critical to the national security and economic prosperity of the United States; and
**(4)**
celebrates the role that mathematics and statistics play in the everyday lives of all people of the United States, including in technology, news, games, literature, and music.
