IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 24, 2026 Mr. Schmidt introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend title 28, United States Code, to restore authority to the executive branch to oversee operations of the Offices of the United States Attorneys, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring Executive Branch Authorities to Oversee Offices of the United States Attorneys Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Restoring constitutional limitations on the United States courts
Section 546 of title 28, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (c)(2), by inserting after after appointment the following: of that person ; and
**(2)**
by striking subsection (d).
