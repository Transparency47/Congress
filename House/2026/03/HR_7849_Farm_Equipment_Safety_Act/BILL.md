## Summary

**Farm Equipment Safety Act**

This bill provides that emissions standards under the Clean Air Act for nonroad engines and nonroad vehicles do not apply to nonroad engines and nonroad vehicles that are used for agricultural purposes.

A BILL
To amend the Clean Air Act to exempt nonroad engines and vehicles that are used for agricultural purposes from the emission standards of that Act, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Farm Equipment Safety Act .
#### 2. Exemption of nonroad farm engines and vehicles from Clean Air Act emission standards
Section 213 of the Clean Air Act ( 42 U.S.C. 7547 ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e) Exemption The standards under this section do not apply to emissions from nonroad engines and nonroad vehicles that are used for agricultural purposes. .
