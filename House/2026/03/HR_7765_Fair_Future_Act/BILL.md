H. R. 7765
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 3, 2026 Mr. Frost (for himself and Mr. Mackenzie ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend the Fair Housing Act to repeal the Thurmond amendment.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Fair Future Act .
#### 2. Amendment
Section 807(b) of the Fair Housing Act ( 42 U.S.C. 3607(b) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking paragraph (4); and
**(2)**
by redesignating paragraph (5) as paragraph (4).
