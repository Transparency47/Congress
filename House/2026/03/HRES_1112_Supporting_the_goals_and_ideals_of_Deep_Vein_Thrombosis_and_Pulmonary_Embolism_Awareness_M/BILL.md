119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1112
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 12, 2026 Mr. Murphy (for himself and Mr. Tonko ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of Deep Vein Thrombosis and Pulmonary Embolism Awareness Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of Deep Vein Thrombosis and Pulmonary Embolism Awareness Month ; and
**(2)**
recognizes the importance of raising awareness of deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolisms.
