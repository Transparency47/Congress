March 9, 2026 Ms. Budzinski (for herself, Mr. Bost , Mr. Bell , and Mrs. Wagner ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To amend the Act of May 17, 1954, to revise the boundary of Gateway Arch National Park.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Gateway Arch National Park Boundary Revision Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Boundary revision
Section 4(a) of the Act of May 17, 1954 ( 16 U.S.C. 450jj–3(a) ; 68 Stat. 99) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking one hundred acres and inserting 102.18 acres ; and
**(2)**
by striking map entitled and all that follows through January 1992 and inserting map titled Gateway Arch National Park Proposed Boundary Revision , numbered 366/189,627, dated August 2023 .
