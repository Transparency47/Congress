## Summary

This bill designates the Palm Beach International Airport in Florida as the Donald J. Trump International Airport.

Further, the Federal Aviation Administration must work with the International Air Transport Association, the International Civil Aviation Organization, and the World Meteorological Organization to change the identifying letters of the airport from PBI to DJT.

A BILL
To designate the Palm Beach International Airport in Florida as the Donald J. Trump International Airport , and for other purposes.
#### 1. Designation of Donald J. Trump International Airport
##### (a) Designation
The Palm Beach International Airport in Florida shall after the date of enactment of this Act be known and designated as the Donald J. Trump International Airport .
##### (b) Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the airport referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Donald J. Trump International Airport.
##### (c) Identifying letters
The Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration shall work with the International Air Transport Association, the International Civil Aviation Organization, the Location Identifier of the Federal Aviation Administration, the World Meteorological Organization, and any other organization or entity that assigns numeric or alphabetical identifiers to geographically specific locations to change the identifying letters of the airport referred to in subsection (a) as DJT .
