March 27, 2026 Mr. Ogles introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend title 4, United States Code, to require the flag to be flown at half-staff on the first Friday and Saturday after the full Moon that occurs on or after the spring equinox, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Good Friday Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Position and manner of flag display
Section 7(m) of title 4, Unites States Code, is amended by inserting The flag shall be flown at half-staff on the first Friday and Saturday after the full Moon that occurs on or after the spring equinox. after Armed Forced Day. .
