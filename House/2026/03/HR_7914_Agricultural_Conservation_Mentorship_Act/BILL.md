March 12, 2026 Mrs. McClain Delaney (for herself and Mr. Vindman ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Agriculture
A BILL
To amend the Food Security Act of 1985 with respect to the experienced services program, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Agricultural Conservation Mentorship Act .
#### 2. Experienced services program
Section 1252 of the Food Security Act of 1985 ( 16 U.S.C. 3851(a)(2) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (a)(2)—
**(A)**
in subparagraph (D), by striking ; and and inserting a semicolon;
**(B)**
in subparagraph (E), by striking (as defined in section 1404 of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3103 )). and inserting ; and ; and
**(C)**
by adding at the end the following:
(F) assisting cooperative initiatives under section 1417(b)(3) of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3152(b)(3) ) to improve higher education teaching programs, including paid work-based learning, at institutions of higher education. ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following:
(e) Definitions In this section: (1) Institution of higher education The term institution of higher education has the meaning given that term in section 101 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1001 ). (2) Land-grant colleges and universities The term land-grant colleges and universities has the meaning given that term in section 1404 of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3103 ). (3) Work-based learning The term work-based learning has the meaning given that term in section 3 of the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006 ( 20 U.S.C. 2302 ). .
