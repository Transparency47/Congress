119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1137
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 25, 2026 Mr. Harrigan (for himself, Mr. Edwards , Mr. DesJarlais , Ms. Tenney , Mr. Gooden , Mr. McDowell , Mr. Timmons , Mr. Mann , Mr. Miller of Ohio , Mrs. Kim , Mr. Rose , Mr. Moore of Alabama , Mr. Collins , Mr. McCormick , Mr. Rulli , Mr. Knott , Mr. Carter of Georgia , Mr. Mills , Mr. Bost , and Mr. Stauber ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 100th anniversary of the creation of the Sporting Arms and Ammunition Manufacturers’ Institute, Inc., and commending its work establishing industry standards that ensure the safe interoperability of firearms and ammunition.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the 100th anniversary of the creation of the Sporting Arms and Ammunition Manufacturers’ Institute, Inc.;
**(2)**
commends the Sporting Arms and Ammunition Manufacturers’ Institute, Inc., for a century of leadership in establishing standards that ensure the safe interoperability of firearms and ammunition;
**(3)**
acknowledges the Sporting Arms and Ammunition Manufacturers’ Institute, Inc.’s vital role in promoting public safety, scientific rigor, and responsible industry practices;
**(4)**
encourages continued collaboration among industry, government, and scientific organizations to advance safety and innovation in sporting arms and ammunition; and
**(5)**
honors all those who have contributed to the mission of the Sporting Arms and Ammunition Manufacturers’ Institute, Inc., over the past century.
