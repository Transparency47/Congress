119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1134
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 24, 2026 Mr. Rouzer (for himself, Mr. Rutherford , Mr. Pallone , and Mr. Min ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
RESOLUTION
Celebrating the 100th anniversary of the American Shore and Beach Preservation Association.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes and celebrates the 100th anniversary of the ASBPA;
**(2)**
commemorates the centennial of the ASBPA, honoring a century of shore and beach preservation and progress; and
**(3)**
congratulates the leaders and members of the ASBPA on a century of dedication to our Nation’s shores and beaches and recognizes the enduring importance of its mission.
