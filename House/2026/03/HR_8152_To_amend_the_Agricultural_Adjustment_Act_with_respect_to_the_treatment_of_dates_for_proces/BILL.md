A BILL
To amend the Agricultural Adjustment Act with respect to the treatment of dates for processing under certain marketing orders.
#### 1. Treatment of dates for processing under marketing orders
Section 8e(a) of the Agricultural Adjustment Act ( 7 U.S.C. 608e–1(a) ), reenacted with amendments by the Agricultural Marketing Agreement Act of 1937, is amended—
**(1)**
by inserting including dates for processing, after dates, ; and
**(2)**
by striking , other than dates for processing, each place it appears.
