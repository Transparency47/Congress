## Summary

This resolution recognizes and thanks Mae Krier on her 100 th birthday for her contributions to the United States as a trailblazer and a Rosie the Riveter (a term used to describe women who joined the workforce during World War II).

119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1126
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 19, 2026 Mr. Fitzpatrick submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the role of Mae Krier and her contributions as she celebrates her 100th birthday.
That the House of Representatives recognizes and thanks Mae Krier on her 100th birthday for her contributions to the Nation as a Rosie the Riveter and trailblazer.
