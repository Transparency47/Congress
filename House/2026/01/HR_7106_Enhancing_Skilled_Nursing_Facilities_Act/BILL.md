A BILL
To amend titles XVIII and XIX of the Social Security Act to streamline care delivery in skilled nursing facilities and nursing facilities under the Medicare and Medicaid programs.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Enhancing Skilled Nursing Facilities Act .
