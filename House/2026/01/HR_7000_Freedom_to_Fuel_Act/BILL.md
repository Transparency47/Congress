January 9, 2026 Mr. Moore of Alabama (for himself, Ms. Fedorchak , Mr. Rulli , Mr. Hurd of Colorado , and Mr. Rose ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend the Clean Air Act to exclude a portable fuel container from the definition of a consumer or commercial product, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Freedom to Fuel Act .
#### 2. Portable fuel container exclusion
Section 183(e)(1)(B) of the Clean Air Act ( 42 U.S.C. 7511b(e)(1)(B) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in the first sentence, by striking The term and inserting the following:
(i) In general The term ; and
**(2)**
by striking the second sentence and inserting the following:
(ii) Exclusions The term consumer or commercial product does not include the following: (I) A portable fuel container. (II) A fuel or fuel additive regulated under section 211. (III) A motor vehicle, non-road vehicle, or non-road engine as defined under section 216. .
