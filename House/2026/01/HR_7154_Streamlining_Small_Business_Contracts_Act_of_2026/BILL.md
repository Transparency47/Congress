A BILL
To amend the Small Business Act to modify the dollar threshold for certain sole source contracts, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Streamlining Small Business Contracts Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Amendments to sole source contracting authority for certain small business concerns
The Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 631 et seq. ) is amended—
**(1)**
in section 8(a)(1)(D)(i)(II), by striking $7,000,000 and all that follows and inserting $10,000,000. ;
**(2)**
in section 8(m)—
**(A)**
in paragraph (7)(B), by striking will not exceed and all that follows and inserting will not exceed $10,000,000; and ; and
**(B)**
in paragraph (8)(B), by striking will not exceed and all that follows and inserting will not exceed $10,000,000; and ;
**(3)**
in section 31(c)(2)(A)(ii) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 657a(c)(2)(A)(ii) ), by striking will not exceed and all that follows and inserting will not exceed $10,000,000; and ; and
**(4)**
in section 36(c)(2) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 657f(c)(2) ), by striking will not exceed and all that follows and inserting will not exceed $10,000,000; and .
