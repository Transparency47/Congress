A BILL
To amend the Small Business Act to provide for loan guarantees to certain small businesses, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Territorial SBA Loan Guaranty Adjustment Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Loan guarantees for covered territory businesses
##### (a) In general
Section 7(a)(2) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 636(a)(2) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(G) Participation in loan to covered territory business (i) In general Except as provided in clause (ii), in an agreement to participate in a loan to a covered territory business on a deferred basis under this subsection, the participation by the Administration shall be 90 percent. (ii) Exceptions Clause (i) shall not apply with respect to a loan under paragraph (9), (14)(A), (16), (31), or (34), or under a pilot program carried out pursuant to paragraph (25). .
##### (b) Conforming amendment
Section 7(a)(2)(A) of such Act is amended by striking and (F) and inserting (F), and (G) .
