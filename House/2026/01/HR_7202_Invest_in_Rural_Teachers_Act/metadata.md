(3) Use of funds (A) In general An eligible entity that receives a subgrant under paragraph (1) shall use the subgrant funds to award a signing and retention bonus to each teacher hired by the agency who agrees to teach in a rural school for a period of 3 school years. (B) Bonus amount and disbursement The amount of the signing and retention bonus paid to a teacher under subparagraph (A) shall be $5,000 annually for each year of the 3 school-year period described in such subparagraph. (4) Prioritization of homegrown teachers An eligible entity that receives a subgrant under this subsection shall seek to prioritize the recruitment, hiring, and award of bonuses to teachers who previously resided in the community served by the entity, including teachers who were born, raised, and educated in such community.
