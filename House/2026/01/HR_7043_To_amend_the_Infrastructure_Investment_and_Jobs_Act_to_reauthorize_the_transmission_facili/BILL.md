A BILL
To amend the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act to reauthorize the transmission facilitation program.
#### 1. Transmission facilitation program
Section 40106(d)(3) of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act ( 42 U.S.C. 18713(d)(3) ) is amended by striking 2022 through 2026 and inserting 2026 through 2031 .
