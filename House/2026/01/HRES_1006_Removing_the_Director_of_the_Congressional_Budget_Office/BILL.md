## Summary

This resolution removes Congressional Budget Office (CBO) Director Phillip Swagel from his position. (Under current law, either chamber of Congress may remove the CBO director with a resolution.)

119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1006
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 15, 2026 Mr. Mills submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Budget
RESOLUTION
Removing the Director of the Congressional Budget Office.
That effective immediately on the date of the adoption of this resolution, the Director of the Congressional Budget Office is hereby removed pursuant to section 201(a)(4) of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974 ( 2 U.S.C. 601(a)(4) ).
