IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 13, 2026 Mr. Ruiz introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to expand eligibility for, and make permanent, certain programs for homeless veterans.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Health Care for Homeless Veterans Act .
#### 2. Health Care for Homeless Veterans program: expansion of eligibility; permanent authorization
##### (a) Expansion of eligibility
Section 2002(b)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking sections 2011, 2012, 2013, and inserting sections 2011, 2012, 2013, 2031, .
##### (b) Extension of authorization
Section 2031 of such title is amended—
**(1)**
by striking subsection (b); and
**(2)**
in subsection (a), in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by striking the enumerator.
#### 3. Permanent authorization of program of additional services for homeless veterans
Section 2033 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking subsection (d).
