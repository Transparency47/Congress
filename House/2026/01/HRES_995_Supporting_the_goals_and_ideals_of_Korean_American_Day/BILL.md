RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of Korean American Day.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of Korean American Day;
**(2)**
urges all Americans to observe Korean American Day so as to have a greater appreciation of the invaluable contributions Korean Americans have made to the United States; and
**(3)**
honors and recognizes the 123rd anniversary of the arrival of the first Korean immigrants to the United States.
