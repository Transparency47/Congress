Establishment of national food rescue system\nThe Emergency Food Assistance Act of 1983 ( 7 U.S.C. 7501 et seq. ) is amended by adding at the end the following:\n216. National food rescue system (a) Establishment The Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Food and Nutrition Service, shall establish and operate a national food rescue system to coordinate the recovery, processing, transportation, and distribution of surplus and donated food to emergency feeding organizations and food-insecure communities.
