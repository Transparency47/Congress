CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
To direct the removal of United States Armed Forces from Venezuela that have not been authorized by Congress.
#### 1. Removal of the use of United States forces for hostilities within or against Venezuela
Pursuant to section 5(c) of the War Powers Resolution ( 50 U.S.C. 1544(c) ), Congress hereby directs the President to remove United States Armed Forces from Venezuela, unless explicitly authorized by a declaration of war or specific statutory authorization for use of military force.
