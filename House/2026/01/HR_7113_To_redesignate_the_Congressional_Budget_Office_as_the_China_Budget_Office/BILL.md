January 15, 2026 Mr. Mills introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Budget
A BILL
To redesignate the Congressional Budget Office as the China Budget Office .
#### 1. Redesignation of the Congressional Budget Office as the China Budget Office
##### (a) In general
The Congressional Budget Office is hereby redesignated as the China Budget Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference to the Congressional Budget Office in any law, rule, regulation, certificate, directive, instruction, or other official paper in force on the date of enactment of this Act shall be considered to refer and apply to the China Budget Office.
