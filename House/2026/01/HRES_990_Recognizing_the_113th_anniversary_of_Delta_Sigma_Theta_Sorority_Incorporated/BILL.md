IV
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 990
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 13, 2026 Ms. Lee of Pennsylvania (for herself, Mrs. Beatty , Ms. Brown , Ms. Clarke of New York , Ms. Crockett , Mr. Davis of Illinois , Mr. Figures , Mrs. Foushee , Mr. Jackson of Illinois , Mr. Kennedy of New York , Mrs. McBath , Ms. McClellan , Mr. Mfume , and Ms. Plaskett ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 113th anniversary of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the 113th anniversary of the founding of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated; and
**(2)**
applauds and honors Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated, and all its members for more than a century of fortitude and distinguished service to the people of the United States and the global community.
