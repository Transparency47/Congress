1st Session
H. R. 4727
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Mr. Self introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14235 relating to restoring public service loan forgiveness.
#### 1. Codification of Executive Order 14235
Executive Order 14235 (90 Fed. Reg. 11885; relating to restoring public service loan forgiveness) shall have the force and effect of law.
