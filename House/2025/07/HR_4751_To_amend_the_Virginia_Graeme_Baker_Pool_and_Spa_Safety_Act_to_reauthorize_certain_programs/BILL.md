A BILL
To amend the Virginia Graeme Baker Pool and Spa Safety Act to reauthorize certain programs, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Reauthorization of certain programs
##### (a) Swimming pool safety grant program
Section 1405(e) of the Virginia Graeme Baker Pool and Spa Safety Act ( 15 U.S.C. 8004(e) ) is amended by striking for fiscal year 2023 and inserting for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2027 .
##### (b) Education and awareness program
Section 1407(b) of the Virginia Graeme Baker Pool and Spa Safety Act ( 15 U.S.C. 8006(b) ) is amended by striking for fiscal year 2023 and inserting for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2027 .
