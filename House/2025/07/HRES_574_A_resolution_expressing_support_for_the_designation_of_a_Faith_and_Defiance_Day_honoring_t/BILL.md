IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 574
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 10, 2025 Mr. Rulli submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
A resolution expressing support for the designation of a Faith and Defiance Day, honoring the victims of the July 13th, 2024 shooting, condemning political violence, and acknowledging the positive impact President Donald J. Trump has had on America.
That the House of Representatives:
**(1)**
Supports the designation of a “Faith and Defiance Day”.
**(2)**
Honors the life and heroism of Corey D. Comperatore.
**(3)**
Recognizes James Copenhaver and David Dutch for their strength and courage in recovery.
**(4)**
Condemns all acts of political violence, regardless of target or motive.
**(5)**
Acknowledges the positive impact our 45th and 47th President Donald J. Trump’s leadership has had on national policy and public sentiment.
