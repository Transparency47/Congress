## Summary

**ACO Assignment Improvement Act of 2025**

This bill establishes additional requirements for assigning Medicare fee-for-service beneficiaries to accountable care organizations (ACOs) under the Medicare shared savings program. Under current law, the program enables ACOs to receive payments for savings stemming from care coordination and management.

The bill requires the basis for assignment to reflect beneficiaries' utilization of not only primary care services provided by ACO physicians, but also those provided by other ACO practitioners—specifically, physician assistants, nurse practitioners, and clinical nurse specialists.

To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to establish a SNF-at-home program under the Medicare program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the ACO Assignment Improvement Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Improvements to the assignment of beneficiaries under the medicare shared savings program
Section 1899(c)(1) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395jjj(c)(1) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subparagraph (A), by striking and at the end;
**(2)**
in subparagraph (B), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**

(C) in the case of performance years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, primary care services provided under this title by an ACO professional described in subsection (h)(1)(B) .
