A BILL
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to eliminate the 190-day lifetime limit on inpatient psychiatric hospital services under the Medicare Program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Medicare Mental Health Inpatient Equity Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Elimination of 190-day lifetime limit on inpatient psychiatric hospital services
##### (a) In general
Section 1812 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395d ) is amended—

##### (b) Effective Date
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply to items and services furnished on or after January 1, 2027.
