119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 4707
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Mrs. Miller-Meeks (for herself, Mr. Feenstra , and Mrs. Hinson ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1019 Avenue H in Fort Madison, Iowa, as the Martin L. Graber Post Office .
#### 1. Martin L. Graber Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1019 Avenue H in Fort Madison, Iowa, shall be known and designated as the Martin L. Graber Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Martin L. Graber Post Office .
