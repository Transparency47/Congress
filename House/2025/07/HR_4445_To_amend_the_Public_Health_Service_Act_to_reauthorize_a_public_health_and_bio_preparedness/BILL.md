A BILL
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize a public health and bio-preparedness workforce loan repayment program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Public Health and Bio-Preparedness Workforce Loan Repayment Reauthorization Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Reauthorization of Public Health and Bio-Preparedness Workforce Loan Repayment Program
Section 776(i) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 295f–1(i) ) is amended by striking fiscal years 2023 through 2025 and inserting fiscal years 2026 through 2030 .
