July 17, 2025 Mr. Tony Gonzales of Texas (for himself, Ms. De La Cruz , Mr. Cuellar , Mr. Vicente Gonzalez of Texas , and Ms. Escobar ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
A BILL
To amend the Cranston-Gonzalez National Affordable Housing Act to update the eligibility requirements related to metropolitan statistical area population limits for the Community Development Block Grant Colonia Set-Aside Program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Ensuring Continued Access to Funding for Colonias Act .
#### 2. Amendment to the Colonia Set-Aside Program
Section 916(e)(4) of the Cranston-Gonzalez National Affordable Housing Act ( 42 U.S.C. 5306 note) is amended by striking 1,000,000 and inserting 2,000,000 .
