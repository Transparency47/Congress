119th CONGRESS

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 17, 2025 Ms. Wasserman Schultz (for herself, Mrs. Miller-Meeks , Ms. Castor of Florida , Mr. Fitzpatrick , Mrs. Dingell , and Mrs. Harshbarger ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To reauthorize the Young Women’s Breast Health Education and Awareness Requires Learning Young Act of 2009.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the EARLY Act Reauthorization of 2025 .
#### 2. Reauthorization of the Young Women’s Breast Health Education and Awareness Requires Learning Young Act of 2009
Section 399NN(h) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 280m(h) ) is amended by striking 2026 and inserting 2031 .
