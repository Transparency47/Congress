IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Mr. Bell introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act to include additional ZIP Codes with respect to claims relating to Manhattan Project waste.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the St. Louis RECA Readjustment Act .
#### 2. Radiation exposure compensation claims relating to Manhattan Project waste
##### (a) Inclusion of additional ZIP Codes
Section 5A(d)(1) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act ( Public Law 101–426 ; 42 U.S.C. 2210 note), as added by section 100204 of Public Law 119–21 , is amended by inserting 63106, 63107 after 63074, .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the enactment of Public Law 119–21 .
