1st Session
H. R. 4783
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 29, 2025 Mrs. Cammack introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To prohibit Federal funds from being made available to any law enforcement agency that employs an alien as a law enforcement officer, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Citizen-Only Police Act of 2025 or the COP Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Prohibition on availability of Federal funds
No Federal funds may be made available to any law enforcement agency that employs an alien as a law enforcement officer.
