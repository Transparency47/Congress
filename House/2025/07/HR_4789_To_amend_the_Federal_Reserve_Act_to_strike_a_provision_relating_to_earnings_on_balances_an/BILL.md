IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 29, 2025 Mr. Davidson introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
A BILL
To amend the Federal Reserve Act to strike a provision relating to earnings on balances, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Fiscal Accountability for Interest on Reserves Act or the FAIR Act .
#### 2. Earnings on balances
##### (a) In general
Section 19(b) of the Federal Reserve Act ( 12 U.S.C. 461(b) ) is amended by striking paragraph (12).
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
