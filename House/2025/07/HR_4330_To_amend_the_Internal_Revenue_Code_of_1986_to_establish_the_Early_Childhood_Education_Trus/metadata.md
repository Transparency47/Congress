(b) Transfer to trust fund of amounts equivalent to certain taxes There are hereby appropriated to the Early Childhood Education Trust Fund 15 percent of the amount equivalent to the taxes received in the Treasury under section 2001 in each calendar year, of which not less than 25 percent shall be made available to carry out subsection (e).
