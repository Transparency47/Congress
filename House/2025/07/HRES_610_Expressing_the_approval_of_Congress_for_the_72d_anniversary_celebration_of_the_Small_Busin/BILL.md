IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 610
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Mr. Williams of Texas (for himself and Mr. Olszewski ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Small Business
RESOLUTION
Expressing the approval of Congress for the 72d anniversary celebration of the Small Business Administration and in recognition of America’s entrepreneurs and job creators for their important contributions to the United States economy.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses approval of the 72d anniversary celebration of the Small Business Administration; and
**(2)**
recognizes American entrepreneurs and job creators for their important contributions to the United States economy.
