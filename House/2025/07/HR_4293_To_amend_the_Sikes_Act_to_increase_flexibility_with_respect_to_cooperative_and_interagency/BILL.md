A BILL
To amend the Sikes Act to increase flexibility with respect to cooperative and interagency agreements for land management off of installations.
#### 1. Cooperative and interagency agreements for land management off of installations
Section 103A(a)(2) of the Sikes Act ( 16 U.S.C. 670c–1(a)(2) ) is amended by inserting or the operations of the military installation or State-owned National Guard installation after current or anticipated military activities .
