July 23, 2025 Mr. Onder introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To designate the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts as the Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts .
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Make Entertainment Great Again Act of 2025 or the MEGA Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Designation of Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts
##### (a) Designation
The John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts shall after the date of enactment of this Act be known and designated as the Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts .
##### (b) Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts shall be considered to be a reference to the Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts.
