July 17, 2025 Mr. Kelly of Pennsylvania introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
A BILL
To advance Thomas B. Hagen on the retired list of the Navy.
#### 1. Advancement of Captain Thomas B. Hagen, United States Navy, on the retired list
##### (a) Advancement
Captain Thomas B. Hagen, United States Navy (retired), is entitled to hold the rank of rear admiral (lower half) while on the retired list of the Navy.
##### (b) Additional benefits not To accrue
The advancement of Thomas B. Hagen on the retired list of the Navy under subsection (a) shall not affect—
**(1)**
the retired pay or other benefits from the United States to which Thomas B. Hagen would have been entitled based upon his military service; or
**(2)**
any benefits to which any other person may become entitled based on such military service.
