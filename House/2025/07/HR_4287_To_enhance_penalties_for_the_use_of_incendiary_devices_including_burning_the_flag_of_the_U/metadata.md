(b) Enhanced penalty Whoever, in the course of committing a Federal offense involving property damage, obstruction of government operations, or public endangerment, knowingly uses or causes the use of an open flame or incendiary device, including burning the flag of the United States, shall be subject to an enhanced term of imprisonment of not less than 1 year, in addition to any other penalty authorized by law.
