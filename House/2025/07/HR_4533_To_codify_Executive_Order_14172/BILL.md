119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 4533
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 17, 2025 Mr. Steube introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14172.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring Names of American Greatness Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14172
Section 2 of Executive Order 14172 (90 Fed. Reg. 8629; titled Restoring Names That Honor American Greatness ) shall have the force and effect of law.
