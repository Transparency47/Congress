IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 576
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 10, 2025 Mr. Torres of New York (for himself, Ms. Clarke of New York , Mr. Goldman of New York , and Mr. Espaillat ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Andy Ogles.
That—
**(1)**
Representative Andy Ogles be censured;
**(2)**
Representative Andy Ogles forthwith present himself in the well of the House of Representatives for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
Representative Andy Ogles be censured with the public reading of this resolution by the Speaker.
