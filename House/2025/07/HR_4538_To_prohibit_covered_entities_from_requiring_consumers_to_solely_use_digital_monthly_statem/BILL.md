1st Session
H. R. 4538
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 17, 2025 Mr. Turner of Ohio introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
A BILL
To prohibit covered entities from requiring consumers to solely use digital monthly statements, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protecting Against Paperless and Electronic Requirements Act or the PAPER Act .
#### 2. Requirement
##### (a) In general
Each covered entity shall offer each consumer of such entity the option of receiving paper copies of their monthly statements.
##### (b) Prohibition
A covered entity may not condition the use of any services offered by such entity on the sole use of digital monthly statements by a consumer.
##### (c) Definitions
**(1) Depository institution**
The term depository institution has the meaning given the term in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Act.
**(2) Credit union**
The term credit union has the meaning given the term in section 101 of the Federal Credit Union Act.
**(3) Covered entity**
The term covered entity means a depository institution or a credit union.
