119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 4728
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Mr. Self introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce , and in addition to the Committees on the Judiciary , and Armed Services , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14280 relating to reinstating commonsense school discipline policies.
#### 1. Codification of Executive Order 14280
Executive Order 14280 (90 Fed. Reg. 17533; relating to reinstating commonsense school discipline policies) shall have the force and effect of law.
