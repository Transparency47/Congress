119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 4421
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 15, 2025 Ms. Tenney introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14208 relating to ending procurement and forced use of paper straws.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Better Straws Act .
#### 2. Codification of Executive order 14208
Executive Order 14208 (90 Fed. Reg. 9585; relating to ending procurement and forced use of paper straws) shall have the force and effect of law.
