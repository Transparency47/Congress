119th CONGRESS

H. R. 4743
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Mr. Tiffany (for himself and Mr. Clyde ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To repeal section 14(g)(5)(A) of the Immigration and Nationality Act.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Colleges for the American People Act of 2025 or the CAP Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Numerical limitations related to employees of institutions of higher education
Section 214(g)(5)(A) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1184(g)(5)(A) ) is repealed.
