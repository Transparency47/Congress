IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 620
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 29, 2025 Mr. Evans of Colorado (for himself, Mrs. Miller-Meeks , and Mr. Kean ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Celebrating the 60th anniversary of Medicaid.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
celebrates the 60th anniversary of Medicaid; and
**(2)**
will continue to protect Medicaid from waste, fraud, and abuse to ensure its longevity for the most vulnerable Americans such as children, single mothers, the disabled, and individuals living below the Federal poverty line.
