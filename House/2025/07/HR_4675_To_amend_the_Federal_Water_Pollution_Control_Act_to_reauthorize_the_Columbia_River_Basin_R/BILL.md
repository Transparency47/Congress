IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 23, 2025 Ms. Hoyle of Oregon introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to reauthorize the Columbia River Basin Restoration program, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Columbia River Clean-Up Act .
#### 2. Columbia River Basin Restoration
Section 123(d)(6) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1275(d)(6) ) is amended by striking 2020 and 2021 and inserting 2026 through 2030 .
