119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5135
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 4, 2025 Mr. Burlison introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To nullify a final rule on train crew size safety requirements published by the Federal Railroad Administration.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Train Crew Choice Act .
#### 2. Nullification of final rule on train crew size safety requirements
The final rule published in the Federal Register on April 9, 2024 by the Federal Railroad Administration titled Train Crew Size Safety Requirements (89 Fed. Reg. 25052) shall have no force or effect.
