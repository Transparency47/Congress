119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 765
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 26, 2025 Mr. Arrington submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 10-year anniversary of the TORQE 62 crash in Jalalabad, Afghanistan.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
commemorates the lives, courage, and service of the 6 United States Airmen and 5 civilian contractors killed in the TORQE 62 crash on October 2, 2015, in Jalalabad, Afghanistan;
**(2)**
extends heartfelt condolences to the families, loved ones and friends of the crew; and
**(3)**
expresses gratitude for the courage, duty and selfless service demonstrated by the crew of TORQE 62, and all those who served in Operation Freedom’s Sentinel.
