September 3, 2025 Mr. Torres of New York (for himself, Ms. Ocasio-Cortez , and Mr. Carter of Louisiana ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To amend the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 to include certain safety requirements, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as River’s Law .
#### 2. Safety requirements for child care providers
Section 658E(c)(2)(I) of the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 ( 42 U.S.C. 9858c(c)(2)(I) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in clause (i)(XI), by striking ; and and inserting a semicolon;
**(2)**
by redesignating clause (ii) as clause (iii); and
**(3)**
by inserting after clause (i) the following:
(ii) shall include— (I) a prohibition on constructing, maintaining, or otherwise operating a pool on the premises of any facility where such services are provided; and (II) a requirement to install door and window alarms in any facility where such services are provided; and .
