IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 710
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 15, 2025 Ms. Chu (for herself, Ms. Bonamici , Ms. Meng , and Ms. Pingree ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of Community School Coordinators Appreciation Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of Community School Coordinators Appreciation Week ;
**(2)**
thanks community school coordinators for the work they do to serve students, families, and communities; and
**(3)**
encourages students, parents, school administrators, and public officials to participate in events that celebrate Community School Coordinators Appreciation Week.
