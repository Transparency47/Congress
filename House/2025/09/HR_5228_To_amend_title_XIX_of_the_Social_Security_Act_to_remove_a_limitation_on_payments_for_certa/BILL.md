September 9, 2025 Mrs. Dingell (for herself, Ms. Lofgren , Ms. Jayapal , Ms. Moore of Wisconsin , Ms. Tlaib , Ms. Tokuda , Mrs. McBath , Mrs. Cherfilus-McCormick , Mr. Evans of Pennsylvania , Ms. Budzinski , Mr. Mullin , Ms. McClellan , Ms. Simon , Mr. Soto , Mr. McGovern , Ms. Pressley , Mr. Peters , Ms. Scanlon , Ms. Omar , Mr. Thanedar , Ms. Brownley , Ms. Norton , Ms. Barragán , Ms. McBride , Ms. Craig , and Ms. Ansari ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to remove a limitation on payments for certain home or community-based services waivers under the Medicaid program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the HCBS Worker Protection Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Removing limitation on payments for certain HCBS waivers under Medicaid
Section 1915(c)(11) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1396n(c)(11) ) is amended by striking subparagraph (C).
