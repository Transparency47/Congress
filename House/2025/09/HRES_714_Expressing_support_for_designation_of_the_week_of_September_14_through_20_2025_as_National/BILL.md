119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 714
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 15, 2025 Mrs. McBath (for herself, Mr. Fitzpatrick , Mr. Mrvan , Mrs. Houchin , Mr. Fields , Mr. Mannion , Mr. Evans of Pennsylvania , Ms. Norton , Mr. Johnson of Georgia , Mr. Davis of Illinois , Mr. Swalwell , and Mr. Bishop ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for designation of the week of September 14 through 20, 2025, as National Adult Education and Family Literacy Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Adult Education and Family Literacy Week , to raise public awareness about the importance of adult education, workforce skills, and family literacy;
**(2)**
encourages people across the United States to support programs that assist individuals in need of adult education, workforce skills, and family literacy programs;
**(3)**
recognizes the importance of adult education, workforce skills, and family literacy programs; and
**(4)**
calls on public, private, and nonprofit entities to support increased access to adult education and family literacy programs to ensure a fully literate society.
