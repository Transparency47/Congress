IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 676
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 3, 2025 Ms. Clarke of New York submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Cory Mills.
That—
**(1)**
Representative Cory Mills be censured;
**(2)**
Representative Cory Mills forthwith present himself in the well of the House of Representatives for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
Representative Cory Mills be censured with the public reading of this resolution by the Speaker.
