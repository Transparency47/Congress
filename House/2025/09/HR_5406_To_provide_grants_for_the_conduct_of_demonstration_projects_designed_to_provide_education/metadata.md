(5) Definitions In this subsection: (A) Eligible entity The term eligible entity means any of the following entities that demonstrates in an application submitted under this subsection that the entity has the capacity to fully develop and administer the demonstration project described in the application: (i) A local workforce development board established under section 107 of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act. (ii) A State or territory, a political subdivision of a State or territory, or an agency of a State, territory, or such a political subdivision.
