IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 761
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 23, 2025 Mr. Menendez (for himself, Mr. Pallone , Mrs. Watson Coleman , Mr. Casten , Ms. Escobar , Mr. Gottheimer , and Ms. Pou ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of September 23, 2025, as national Bruce Springsteen Day , and honoring his contributions to music, New Jersey, and the Nation.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of Bruce Springsteen Day ; and
**(2)**
honors Bruce Springsteen’s contributions to music, New Jersey, and the Nation.
