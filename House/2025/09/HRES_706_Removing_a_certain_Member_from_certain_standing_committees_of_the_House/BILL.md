IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 15, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia (for himself, Mr. Crenshaw , Mrs. Bice , Mr. McDowell , Mr. Moore of Alabama , and Mr. Van Orden ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Removing a certain Member from certain standing committees of the House.
That the following named Members be, and are hereby, removed from the following standing committees of the House of Representatives:
Committee on the Budget:
Ms. Omar.
Committee on Education and Workforce:
Ms. Omar.
