119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 726
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 16, 2025 Mr. Morelle (for himself, Ms. Sewell , Mrs. Torres of California , and Ms. Johnson of Texas ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on House Administration
RESOLUTION
Supporting the recognition of September 16, 2025, as National Voter Registration Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Voter Registration Day ; and
**(2)**
encourages each voting-eligible citizen of the United States to—
**(A)**
register to vote in his or her respective jurisdiction;
**(B)**
confirm with their appropriate election official that his or her name, address, and other personal identifying information on record is current; and
**(C)**
fulfill his or her civic duty by voting on election day if he or she would like to do so.
