IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 696
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 10, 2025 Mr. Schweikert (for himself and Mr. Larsen of Washington ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 60th anniversary of independence of the Republic of Singapore and supporting a strong United States-Singapore relationship.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the 60th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Singapore; and
**(2)**
reaffirms its support for a strong United States-Singapore bilateral relationship with a focus on increasing economic, security, and people-to-people ties.
