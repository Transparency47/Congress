119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 740
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 18, 2025 Ms. Jayapal (for herself, Mr. Larsen of Washington , Ms. DelBene , Mr. Smith of Washington , and Ms. Strickland ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Congratulating Seattle Sounders FC on becoming the first Major League Soccer team to win all 5 major North American soccer trophies.
That the House of Representatives recognizes—
**(1)**
the Seattle Sounders FC for becoming the first Major League Soccer team to win all 5 major North American soccer trophies;
**(2)**
the people of the State of Washington and the city of Seattle for their enthusiastic support of Seattle Sounders FC; and
**(3)**
the continuing progress toward ensuring equity in men’s and women’s professional sports.
