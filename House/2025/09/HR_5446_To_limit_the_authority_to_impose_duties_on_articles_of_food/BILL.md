A BILL
To limit the authority to impose duties on articles of food.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No Tariffs on Groceries Act .
#### 2. Limitation on authority to impose duties on articles of food
##### (a) In general
The President may not impose duties proposed by the Presidential Administration on articles of food unless—
**(1)**
the President transmits to Congress a request to impose such duties; and
**(2)**
such request is subsequently and specifically approved by an Act of Congress.
##### (b) Exception
The limitation in subsection (a) shall not apply with respect to existing tariff-rate quotas on articles of food.
##### (c) Articles of food defined
In this section, the term articles of food means—
**(1)**
articles used for food or drink for man or other animals;
**(2)**
articles used for components of any articles described in paragraph (1); and
**(3)**
seeds, fertilizers, manures, and agro-chemicals.
