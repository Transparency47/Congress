119th CONGRESS

H. R. 5525
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 19, 2025 Mr. Perry introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To repeal the authority of the District of Columbia to use automated traffic enforcement systems and to erect signage prohibiting right turns at a red traffic signal.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Stop DC Capital Authoritarian Motorvehicle Enforcement and Restoration of Autonomy Act or the Stop DC CAMERA Act .
#### 2. Repeal of authority to use automated traffic enforcement systems and to erect signage prohibiting right turns at red traffic signal
##### (a) Repeal of authority To use automated traffic enforcement systems
Section 9e of the District of Columbia Traffic Act, 1925 (sec. 50–2201.04e, D.C. Official Code) is repealed.
##### (b) Repeal of authority to erect signage prohibiting certain right turns at red traffic signal
Title IX of the Fiscal Year 1997 Budget Support Act of 1996 (sec. 50–2209.01, D.C. Official Code) is repealed.
