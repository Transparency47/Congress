September 4, 2025 Mr. Sherman (for himself, Ms. Brownley , Ms. Budzinski , Mrs. Cherfilus-McCormick , Mr. Cohen , Mr. Garamendi , Mr. Gomez , Mr. Lynch , Mr. Magaziner , Mr. McGarvey , Mr. McGovern , Mr. Moulton , Mr. Nadler , Ms. Norton , Mr. Panetta , and Mr. Peters ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To repeal the authority under the National Labor Relations Act for States to enact laws prohibiting agreements requiring membership in a labor organization as a condition of employment, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Nationwide Right to Unionize Act .
#### 2. Preempting State right-to-work laws
Subsection (b) of section 14 of the National Labor Relations Act ( 29 U.S.C. 164 ) is repealed.
