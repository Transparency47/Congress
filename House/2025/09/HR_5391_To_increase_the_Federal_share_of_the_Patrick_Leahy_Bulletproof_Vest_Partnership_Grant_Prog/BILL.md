119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5391
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 16, 2025 Mr. Deluzio (for himself and Mr. Garbarino ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To increase the Federal share of the Patrick Leahy Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Bipartisan Bulletproof Vest Partnership Program Expansion Act .
#### 2. Increase in grant share
Section 2501 of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 ( 34 U.S.C. 10531 ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (f)(1)—
**(A)**
in subparagraph (A), by striking 50 percent and inserting 60 percent ; and
**(B)**
in subparagraph (B), by striking 50 percent and inserting 60 percent ; and
**(2)**
by striking subsection (h) and inserting the following:
(h) Authorization of appropriations There is authorized to be appropriated $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030 to carry out this part. .
