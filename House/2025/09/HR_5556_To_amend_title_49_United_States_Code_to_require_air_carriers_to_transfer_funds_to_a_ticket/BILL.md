A BILL
To amend title 49, United States Code, to require air carriers to transfer funds to a ticket agent for cancelled or significantly delayed or changed flights within the time required for air carriers and ticket agents to provide refunds to passengers, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Flight Refund Fairness Act .
#### 2. Refunds for cancelled or significantly delayed or changed flights
Section 42305(e) of title 49, United States Code, is amended—
