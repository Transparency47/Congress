119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5218
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 9, 2025 Mr. McGuire introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Homeland Security
A BILL
To direct the Secretary of Homeland Security establish a publicly avaiable web page on the website of the Department of Homeland Security to permit individuals to view active border wall construction and progress, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Border Wall Status Act .
#### 2. Website to view active border wall construction and progress
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall take such actions as may be necessary to establish a publicly available web page on the website of the Department of Homeland Security to permit individuals to view active border wall construction and progress.
