IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 769
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 26, 2025 Mr. Green of Texas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Affirming the State of Palestine’s right to exist.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
affirms Palestine’s right to exist and at a future time to become a nation State;
**(2)**
recognizes the two-state solution as the only solution that will secure a lasting peace in the region; and
**(3)**
rejects calls for Palestine’s destruction.
