IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 772
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 26, 2025 Ms. Titus (for herself, Mr. Vasquez , Mr. Ciscomani , Ms. Ansari , Ms. Norton , Mr. Cleaver , Ms. Brownley , Mr. Smith of Washington , Mr. Carter of Louisiana , Mr. Espaillat , Mr. Suozzi , Ms. Elfreth , Mr. Case , Mr. Torres of New York , Mr. Neguse , Mr. Quigley , Mrs. Watson Coleman , Mr. Horsford , Mr. Vargas , Ms. Meng , Ms. Escobar , Mrs. Kim , Mr. LaLota , Ms. Hoyle of Oregon , Mr. Evans of Colorado , Ms. Bonamici , Mr. Lawler , Mr. Fitzpatrick , Ms. Lee of Nevada , Mr. Krishnamoorthi , Ms. DeGette , Mr. Stanton , and Ms. Pettersen ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for National Public Lands Day and encouraging the people of the United States to visit public lands on this fee-free day and recognize their spiritual and cultural value, as well as their contribution to the economy of the United States.
That the House of Representatives supports National Public Lands Day and encourages the people of the United States to visit public lands on this fee-free day and recognize the historic, cultural, and economic impact of public lands.
