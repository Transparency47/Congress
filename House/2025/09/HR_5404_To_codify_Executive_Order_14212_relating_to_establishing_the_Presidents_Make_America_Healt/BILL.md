119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5404
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 16, 2025 Mr. Lawler introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14212, relating to establishing the President’s Make America Healthy Again Commission.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Make America Healthy Again Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14212
Executive Order 14212 (90 Fed. Reg. 9833; relating to establishing the President’s Make America Healthy Again Commission) shall have the force and effect of law.
