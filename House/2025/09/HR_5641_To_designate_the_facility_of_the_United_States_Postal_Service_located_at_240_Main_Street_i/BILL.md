A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 240 Main Street in Northport, New York, as the Coast Guard Petty Officer 3rd Class Nate Bruckenthal Post Office Building .
#### 1. Coast Guard Petty Officer 3rd Class Nate Bruckenthal Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 240 Main Street in Northport, New York, shall be known and designated as the Coast Guard Petty Officer 3rd Class Nate Bruckenthal Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Coast Guard Petty Officer 3rd Class Nate Bruckenthal Post Office Building .
