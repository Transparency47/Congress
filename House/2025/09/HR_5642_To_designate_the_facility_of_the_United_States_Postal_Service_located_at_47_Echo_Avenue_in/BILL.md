A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 47 Echo Avenue in Miller Place, New York, as the Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building .
#### 1. Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 47 Echo Avenue in Miller Place, New York, shall be known and designated as the Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building .
