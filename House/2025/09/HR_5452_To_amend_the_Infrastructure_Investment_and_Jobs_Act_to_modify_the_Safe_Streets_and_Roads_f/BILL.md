September 18, 2025 Mr. Cohen (for himself, Mr. Fitzpatrick , and Mr. Garcia of California ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To amend the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act to modify the Safe Streets and Roads for All program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Safe Streets for All Reauthorization and Improvement Act .
#### 2. Safe streets and roads for all program modification
Section 24112 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act ( 23 U.S.C. 402 note) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (c)(2) by striking subparagraph (B) and inserting the following:
(B) Planning grants Of the total amounts made available to carry out the program for fiscal year 2024 and each fiscal year thereafter, not less than 20 percent shall be awarded to eligible projects described in subsection (a)(3)(A). ; and
**(2)**
in subsection (f)(1) by inserting and $5,000,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2027 through 2031 after 2026 .
