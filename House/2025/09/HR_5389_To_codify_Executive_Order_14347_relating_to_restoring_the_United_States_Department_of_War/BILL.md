119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5389
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 16, 2025 Mr. Burchett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14347 (relating to restoring the United States Department of War).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring the United States Department of War Act .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14347
Executive Order 14347 (90 Fed. Reg. 17508; relating to restoring the United States Department of War) shall have the force and effect of law.
