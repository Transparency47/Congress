September 26, 2025 Mr. Ezell (for himself, Mr. Carter of Louisiana , Mrs. Fletcher , Mr. Higgins of Louisiana , and Mr. Moore of Alabama ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
A BILL
To extend the National Flood Insurance Program through November 21, 2025.
#### 1. Extension of National Flood Insurance Program
##### (a) Financing
Section 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 ( 42 U.S.C. 4016(a) ) is amended, in the first sentence, by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting November 21, 2025 .
##### (b) Program expiration
Section 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 ( 42 U.S.C. 4026 ) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting November 21, 2025 .
