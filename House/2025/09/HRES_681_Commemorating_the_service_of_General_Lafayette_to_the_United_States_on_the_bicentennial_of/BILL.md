119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 681
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 8, 2025 Mr. Wittman (for himself, Mr. Keating , Mr. Wilson of South Carolina , Ms. Titus , and Mr. Latta ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Commemorating the service of General Lafayette to the United States on the bicentennial of his farewell tour.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the invaluable service of Lafayette to the independence and ideals of the United States;
**(2)**
expresses gratitude for Lafayette’s sacrifice in fighting during the American Revolution; and
**(3)**
acknowledges Lafayette on the bicentennial of his farewell tour to our Nation.
