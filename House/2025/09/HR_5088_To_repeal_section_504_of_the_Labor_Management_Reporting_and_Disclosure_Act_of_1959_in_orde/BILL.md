September 2, 2025 Ms. Lee of Pennsylvania (for herself, Mr. McGovern , Mrs. McIver , Ms. Ocasio-Cortez , Mr. Thanedar , Ms. Tlaib , and Ms. Wilson of Florida ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To repeal section 504 of the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959 in order to permit certain persons to hold offices in labor organizations.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Union Participation for All Act .
#### 2. Repeal of prohibition against certain persons holding offices in labor organizations
##### (a) In general
Section 504 of the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959 ( 29 U.S.C. 504 ) is repealed.
##### (b) Conforming amendment
Section 401(e) of the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959 ( 29 U.S.C. 481(e) ) is amended by striking to section 504 and .
