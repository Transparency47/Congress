1st Session

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 26, 2025 Mr. Garbarino introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
A BILL
To amend the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 to reauthorize the National Flood Insurance Program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the NFIP Extension Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Extension of the National Flood Insurance Program
##### (a) Financing
Section 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 ( 42 U.S.C. 4016(a) ) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2026 .
##### (b) Program expiration
Program expiration.—Section 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 ( 42 U.S.C. 4026 ) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2026 .
##### (c) Retroactive effective date
If this Act is enacted after September 30, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on September 30, 2025.
