August 5, 2025 Mr. Patronis (for himself, Mr. Collins , Ms. Tenney , and Mrs. Houchin ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14319 (relating to preventing woke AI in the Federal Government).
#### 1. Codification of Executive Order 14319
Executive Order 14319 (90 Fed. Reg. 35389; relating to preventing woke AI in the Federal Government) shall have the force and effect of law.
