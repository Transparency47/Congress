119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 4869
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 1, 2025 Mr. Williams of Texas introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14267 (relating to reducing anti-competitive regulatory barriers).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Freedom to Compete Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14267
Executive Order 14267 (90 Fed. Reg. 15629; relating to reducing anti-competitive regulatory barriers) shall have the force and effect of law.
