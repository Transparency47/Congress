(b) Requirements for online portals accepting challenges A State or local election official who operates an online portal through which a person may submit a formal challenge to the voter registration status of an individual who is registered to vote in elections for Federal office held in the State\u2014 (1) shall reject a challenge submitted on the portal by a person who does not include the person\u2019s name on the material the person submits for the challenge;
