IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 654
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Ms. Clarke of New York submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives with respect to Marcus Garvey.
That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that—
**(1)**
Marcus Garvey was innocent of the charges brought against him by the United States Government;
**(2)**
Marcus Garvey was and should be recognized internationally as a leader and thinker in the struggle for human rights; and
**(3)**
the President should take appropriate measures within his power to exonerate Marcus Garvey and clear his good name.
