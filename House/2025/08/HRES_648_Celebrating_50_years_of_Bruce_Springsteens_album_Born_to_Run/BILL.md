IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 648
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 12, 2025 Mr. Pallone submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Celebrating 50 years of Bruce Springsteen’s album, Born to Run .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
congratulates Bruce Springsteen, E Street Band Members Roy Bittan, Clarence Clemons, Garry Tallent, Max Weinberg, Ernest Carter, Danny Federici, David Sancious, and all others who contributed 50 years ago to the musical masterpiece that is Born to Run ;
**(2)**
celebrates Bruce Springsteen’s mark on American music, culture, and the great State of New Jersey; and
**(3)**
recognizes the importance of Federal investments in the arts and encourages continued support in nurturing American music and culture.
