A BILL
To authorize use of amounts from emergency solutions grants under the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act to provide safe overnight parking facilities for homeless persons, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Naomi Schwartz and Susan Rose Safe Parking Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Safe parking as eligible activity under Emergency Solutions Grant Program
##### (a) In general
Section 415(a) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ( 42 U.S.C. 11374(a) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(6) Maintenance, operation, insurance, and provision of utilities related to safe parking. .
##### (b) Definition
Section 411 of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act ( 42 U.S.C. 11371 ) is amended—
**(1)**
by redesignating paragraphs (7) through (9) as paragraphs (8) through (10), respectively; and
**(2)**
by inserting after paragraph (6) the following new paragraph:
(7) The term safe parking means an activity that— (A) provides homeless persons living in vehicles, including motor homes, with a safe place to park their vehicles overnight to facilitate a transition to stable housing; and (B) provides re-housing and supportive services to such homeless persons. .
