IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 655
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Ms. Clarke of New York submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of August 17, 2025, as Marcus Garvey Recognition Day .
That the House of Representatives supports the designation of Marcus Garvey Recognition Day and the President issuing a proclamation calling upon the people of the United States to observe that day with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
