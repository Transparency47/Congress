## Summary

This resolution recognizes that the national debt is a threat to national security and that deficits are unsustainable, irresponsible, and dangerous. It also commits to (1) restoring regular order to the appropriations process, and (2) addressing the fiscal crisis faced by the United States.

119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 631
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 5, 2025 Mr. Biggs of Arizona (for himself, Mr. Donalds , Mr. Gosar , Mr. Webster of Florida , and Mr. Newhouse ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Budget , and in addition to the Committee on Ways and Means , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the national debt as a threat to national security.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes that the national debt is a threat to the national security of the United States;
**(2)**
realizes that deficits are unsustainable, irresponsible, and dangerous;
**(3)**
commits to restoring regular order in the appropriations process; and
**(4)**
commits to addressing the fiscal crisis faced by the United States.
