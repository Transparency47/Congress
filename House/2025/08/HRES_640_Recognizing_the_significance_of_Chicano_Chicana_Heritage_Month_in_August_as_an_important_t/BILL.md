119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 640
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 8, 2025 Mr. Correa submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the significance of Chicano/Chicana Heritage Month in August as an important time to celebrate the significant contributions of Mexican Americans to the history of the United States.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the significance of Chicano/Chicana Heritage Month as an important time to celebrate the significant contributions of Mexican Americans to the history of the United States;
**(2)**
recognizes that Mexican Americans enhance the rich diversity of and strengthen the United States; and
**(3)**
acknowledges Chicano/Chicana Heritage Month and encourages its observation with appropriate events and activities.
