August 8, 2025 Ms. Scholten introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To provide that the Commissioner of Labor Statistics may be removed only with proof of inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Facts First Act .
#### 2. Limitation on removal of Commissioner of Labor Statistics
Section 2 of the Act of June 13, 1888, entitled An act to establish a department of Labor (25 Stat. 182; 29 U.S.C. 3 ) is amended by adding at the end the following: The Commissioner may be removed only with proof of inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office. .
