119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5076
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 29, 2025 Mr. Steube (for himself and Mrs. Bice ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce , and in addition to the Committee on the Judiciary , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14322 (relating to college sports revenue and athlete compensation).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protection of College Sports Act .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14322
Executive Order 14322 (90 Fed. Reg. 35821; relating to college sports revenue and athlete compensation) shall have the force and effect of law.
