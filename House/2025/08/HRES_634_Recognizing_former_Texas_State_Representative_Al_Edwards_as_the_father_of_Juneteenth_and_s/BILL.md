IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 634
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 5, 2025 Mr. Green of Texas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Recognizing former Texas State Representative Al Edwards as the father of Juneteenth and showing appreciation for successfully passing Texas House Bill 1016, making Texas the first State to recognize Juneteenth (June 19) as an official State holiday.
#### 1. Short title
This resolution may be cited as The Original Resolution Recognizing Al Edwards as the Father of Juneteenth .
#### 2. Recognition and appreciation
The House of Representatives recognizes former Texas State Representative Al Edwards as the father of Juneteenth and shows appreciation for his extraordinary work to garner recognition for the day.
