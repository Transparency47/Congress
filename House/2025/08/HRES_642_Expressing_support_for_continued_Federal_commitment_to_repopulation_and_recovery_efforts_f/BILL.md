IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 642
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 8, 2025 Mrs. Foushee (for herself and Mr. Murphy ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for continued Federal commitment to repopulation and recovery efforts for the red wolf in North Carolina and across the country.
That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that continued support for red wolf repopulation and recovery efforts, with combined partnership between Federal, State, local, educational, and nonprofit institutions across the country, is needed.
