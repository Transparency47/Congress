119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5036
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 26, 2025 Mr. Estes introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To remove the limitation imposed as a result of receiving funding under the Land and Water Conservation Fund on the conversion of Northeast Sedgwick County Park in Sedgwick County, Kansas, to a use other than public outdoor recreation.
#### 1. Nonapplication of limitation on conversion to use other than public outdoor recreation
Section 200305(f)(3) of title 54, United States Code, shall not apply to Northeast Sedgwick County Park in Sedgwick County, Kansas.
