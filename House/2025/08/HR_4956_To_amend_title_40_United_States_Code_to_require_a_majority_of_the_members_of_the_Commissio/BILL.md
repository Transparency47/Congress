A BILL
To amend title 40, United States Code, to require a majority of the members of the Commission of Fine Arts to be residents of the District of Columbia.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Commission of Fine Arts District of Columbia Residency Act .
#### 2. Requiring majority of members of Commission of Fine Arts to be District of Columbia residents
##### (a) Requirement
Section 9101(b) of title 40, United States Code, is amended by striking appointed by the President, who serve and inserting a majority of whom are residents of the District of Columbia, who shall be appointed by the President to serve .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect upon the expiration of the 1-year period which begins on the date of the enactment of this Act.
