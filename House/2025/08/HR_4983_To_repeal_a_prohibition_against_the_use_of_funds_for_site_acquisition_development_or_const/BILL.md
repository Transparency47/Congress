119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 4983
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Mr. Lawler introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To repeal a prohibition against the use of funds for site acquisition, development, or construction of any facility in Israel, Jerusalem, or the West Bank.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Keeping Official Territories Eligible for Land-use Act or the KOTEL Act .
#### 2. Repeal of prohibition
Section 414 of the Omnibus Diplomatic Security and Antiterrorism Act of 1986 ( 22 U.S.C. 4862 ) is repealed.
