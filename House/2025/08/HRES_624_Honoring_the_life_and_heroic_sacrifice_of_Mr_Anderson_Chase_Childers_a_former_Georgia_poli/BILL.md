119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 624
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 1, 2025 Mr. Fry (for himself, Mr. Carter of Georgia , and Mr. Loudermilk ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Honoring the life and heroic sacrifice of Mr. Anderson Chase Childers, a former Georgia police officer who selflessly sacrificed his life rescuing swimmers from a deadly rip current off the coast of South Carolina.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
honors the life and heroic sacrifice of Mr. Anderson Chase Childers, a former Georgia police officer who gave selflessly sacrificed his life rescuing swimmers from a deadly rip current off the coast of South Carolina;
**(2)**
offers its deepest condolences to the family, friends, and loved ones of Mr. Childers;
**(3)**
commends the first responders who assisted in the recovery efforts;
**(4)**
recognizes the danger posed by rip currents and supports public education efforts regarding rip currents to reduce future loss of life;
**(5)**
celebrates the legacy of service and heroism that Mr. Childers leaves behind; and
**(6)**
expresses gratitude for all those who act selflessly in service to others, even at great personal risk.
