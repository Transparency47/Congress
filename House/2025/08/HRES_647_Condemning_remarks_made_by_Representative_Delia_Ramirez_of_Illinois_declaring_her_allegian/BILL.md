IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 647
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 12, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Condemning remarks made by Representative Delia Ramirez of Illinois declaring her allegiance to the Republic of Guatemala before the United States of America.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
condemns the anti-American comments made by Representative Delia Ramirez of Illinois;
**(2)**
affirms its allegiance to always put the interests of American citizens before those of other nations;
**(3)**
condemns comments that put the interests of foreign nations above those of the United States; and
**(4)**
affirms its commitment to always put the safety, prosperity, and overall well-being of the American people first before those of other nations.
