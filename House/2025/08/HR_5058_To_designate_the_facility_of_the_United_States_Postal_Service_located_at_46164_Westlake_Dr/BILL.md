119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5058
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 26, 2025 Mr. Subramanyam (for himself, Mr. Wittman , Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia , Mr. Scott of Virginia , Ms. McClellan , Mr. McGuire , Mr. Cline , Mr. Vindman , and Mr. Beyer ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 46164 Westlake Drive in Sterling, Virginia, as the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building .
#### 1. Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 46164 Westlake Drive in Sterling, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building .
