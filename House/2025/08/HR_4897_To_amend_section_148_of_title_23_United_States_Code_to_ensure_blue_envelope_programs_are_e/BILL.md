August 5, 2025 Ms. Norton introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To amend section 148 of title 23, United States Code, to ensure blue envelope programs are eligible for certain safety project funding, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Blue Envelope Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Highway safety improvement program
Section 148(a)(11)(B)(ii) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by striking laws; and inserting laws, including a project to improve interactions between a motor vehicle operator who has difficulty communicating through speech and a law enforcement officer during a motor vehicle stop, commonly referred to as a blue envelope program ; .
