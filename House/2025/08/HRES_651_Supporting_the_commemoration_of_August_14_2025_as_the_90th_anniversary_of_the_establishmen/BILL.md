119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 651
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Mr. Bresnahan (for himself, Ms. Tenney , Mr. Lawler , Mr. Nunn of Iowa , Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia , Mr. Kean , and Mr. Fitzpatrick ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means
RESOLUTION
Supporting the commemoration of August 14, 2025, as the 90th anniversary of the establishment of Social Security.
That the House of Representatives supports the commemoration of the 90th anniversary of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 301 et seq. ).
