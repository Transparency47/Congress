IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 652
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia (for himself, Mr. Bishop , Mr. McCormick , Mrs. McBath , Mr. Collins , Mr. Loudermilk , and Mr. Allen ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
RESOLUTION
To recognize and honor the heroes of the Fort Stewart, Georgia, shooting on August 6, 2025.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes and honors the brave soldiers of Fort Stewart for their courage and sacrifice to stop the shooter and protect their fellow brothers and sisters in arms—
**(A)**
First Sgt. Joshua Arnold;
**(B)**
Master Sgt. Justin Thomas;
**(C)**
Staff Sgt. Melissa Taylor;
**(D)**
Staff Sgt. Robert Pacheco;
**(E)**
Sgt. Aaron Turner; and
**(F)**
Sgt. Eve Rodarte; and
**(2)**
extends its heartfelt wishes for a swift and full recovery to all of those who were injured in the attack.
