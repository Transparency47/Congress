IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Ms. Mace introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend title 28, United States Code, related to vacancies in offices of United States Attorneys, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No Appointments by Rogue Judges Act .
#### 2. Vacancies of United States Attorneys
Section 546 of title 28, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
by amending paragraph (2) of subsection (c) to read as follows:
(2) the expiration of the time limitations in section 3346. ; and
**(2)**
by striking subsection (d).
