119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5044
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 26, 2025 Mr. Lawler introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To amend the Diplomatic Security Act to require that congressional notifications relating to decisions to re-open high risk, high threat diplomatic posts include an explanation of the national security value of such decision.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Safe Embassies Act .
#### 2. Modification to congressional notifications
Paragraph (1) of section 105(b) of the Diplomatic Security Act ( 22 U.S.C. 4804(b) ) is amended by inserting , including a detailed explanation of the national security value of re-opening such a post before the period at the end.
