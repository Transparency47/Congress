August 22, 2025 Mr. Barr introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services , and in addition to the Committee on Small Business , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14331 on fair banking.
#### 1. Codification of Executive Order on fair banking
Executive Order 14331, titled Guaranteeing Fair Banking for All Americans (90 Fed. Reg. 38925; published August 12, 2025), shall have the force and effect of law.
