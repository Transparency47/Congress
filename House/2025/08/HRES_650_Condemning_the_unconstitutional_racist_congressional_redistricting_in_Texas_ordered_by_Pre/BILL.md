IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
August 15, 2025 Mr. Green of Texas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Condemning the unconstitutional, racist congressional redistricting in Texas, ordered by President Donald J. Trump.
That the House of Representatives unequivocally condemns the unconstitutional, racist congressional redistricting in Texas, ordered by President Donald J. Trump.
