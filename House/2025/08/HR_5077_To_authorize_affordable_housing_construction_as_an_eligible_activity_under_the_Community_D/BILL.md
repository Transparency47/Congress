A BILL
To authorize affordable housing construction as an eligible activity under the Community Development Block Grant Program, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Strengthening Housing Supply Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Affordable housing construction as eligible activity under Community Development Block Grant Program
##### (a) Eligible activity
Subsection (a) of section 105 of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974 ( 42 U.S.C. 5305(a) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (25)(D), by striking and at the end;
**(2)**
in paragraph (26), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(27) the new construction of affordable housing, within the meaning given such term under section 215 of the Cranston-Gonzalez National Affordable Housing Act ( 42 U.S.C. 12745 ). .
##### (b) Low and moderate income requirement
Paragraph (3) of section 105(c) of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974 ( 42 U.S.C. 5305(c)(3) ) is amended by striking or rehabilitation and inserting , rehabilitation, or new construction .
##### (c) Applicability
The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect only to amounts appropriated after the date of the enactment of this Act.
