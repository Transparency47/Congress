## Summary

This bill permits the Stenotomus chrysops species of fish (also known as *scup* ) to be labeled or marketed using the name *golden sea bream* .

A BILL
To establish Golden Sea Bream as an acceptable market name for Stenotomus chrysops.
#### 1. Golden Sea Bream as acceptable market name
A food shall not be treated as misbranded, adulterated, or otherwise in violation of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ( 21 U.S.C. 301 et seq. ) because the food is labeled or marketed using the name Golden Sea Bream for the species Stenotomus chrysops.
