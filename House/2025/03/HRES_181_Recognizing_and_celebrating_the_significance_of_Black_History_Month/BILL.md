RESOLUTION
Recognizing and celebrating the significance of Black History Month.
#### 1. Short title
This resolution may be cited as the Original Black History Month Resolution of 2025 .
#### 2. Supporting black history month
The House of Representatives recognizes the importance of commemorating Black History Month as it acknowledges the achievements of Black Americans throughout the Nation’s history and encourages the continuation of its celebration to raise the awareness of this community’s accomplishments for all Americans.
