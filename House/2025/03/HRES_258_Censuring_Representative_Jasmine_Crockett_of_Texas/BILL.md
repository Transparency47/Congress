IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 258
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 26, 2025 Mr. Weber of Texas (for himself and Mr. Carter of Texas ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Jasmine Crockett of Texas.
That—
**(1)**
Representative Jasmine Crockett of Texas be censured;
**(2)**
Representative Jasmine Crockett forthwith present herself in the well of the House of Representatives for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
Representative Jasmine Crockett be censured with the public reading of this resolution by the Speaker.
