119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 275
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 31, 2025 Ms. Garcia of Texas (for herself, Ms. Scholten , Ms. Norton , Mr. Tonko , Mr. Landsman , and Mr. Davis of Illinois ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of Social Work Month and World Social Work Day on March 18, 2025.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of Social Work Month and World Social Work Day;
**(2)**
recognizes with gratitude the contributions of the millions of social workers who have advanced individual, family, community, and the Nation’s health and well-being since the dawn of the social work profession;
**(3)**
acknowledges the diligent efforts of individuals and groups who promote the importance of social work and observe Social Work Month and World Social Work Day; and
**(4)**
encourages individuals to engage in appropriate ceremonies and activities to promote further awareness of the integral role that social workers play in building a more perfect Union.
