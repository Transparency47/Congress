March 27, 2025 Mr. Smith of Nebraska (for himself and Mr. Moran ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means
A BILL
To amend part A of title IV of the Social Security Act to target funds to families in need.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Targeting TANF to Families in Need Act .
#### 2. Targeting funds to families in need
##### (a) In general
Section 404 of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 604 ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(l) Establishing a threshold for families in need A State to which a grant is made under section 403(a)(1) shall use the grant only to provide assistance or services to a family whose income is less than twice the poverty guidelines updated periodically in the Federal Register under section 673(2) of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1981 ( 42 U.S.C. 9902(2) ). .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on October 1, 2026.
