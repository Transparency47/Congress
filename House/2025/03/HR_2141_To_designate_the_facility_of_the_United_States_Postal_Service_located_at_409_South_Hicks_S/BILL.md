A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 409 South Hicks Street in Lawrenceville, Virginia, as the James Solomon Russell Post Office .
#### 1. James Solomon Russell Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 409 South Hicks Street in Lawrenceville, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the James Solomon Russell Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the James Solomon Russell Post Office .
