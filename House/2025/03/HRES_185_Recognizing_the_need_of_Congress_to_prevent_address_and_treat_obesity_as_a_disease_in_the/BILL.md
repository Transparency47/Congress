119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 185
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 4, 2025 Mrs. Cherfilus-McCormick (for herself and Ms. Moore of Wisconsin ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the need of Congress to prevent, address, and treat obesity as a disease in the United States on this World Obesity Day, March 4, 2025.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the urgent need to prevent, treat, and address obesity as a disease on this World Obesity Day;
**(2)**
recognizes the need to categorize obesity as a disease to reduce the risk of other diseases and related comorbidities.
**(3)**
understands that genetic, environmental, behavioral factors, and the social determinants of health contribute to obesity; and
**(4)**
encourages health care providers and researchers to develop evidence-based strategies for the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of obesity.
