IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 190
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 5, 2025 Mr. Steil (for himself and Mr. Morelle ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on House Administration
RESOLUTION
Electing Members to the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library and the Joint Committee on Printing.
#### 1. Election of Members to Joint Committee of Congress on the Library and Joint Committee on Printing
##### (a) Joint Committee of Congress on the Library
The following Members are hereby elected to the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library, to serve with the chair of the Committee on House Administration and the chair of the Subcommittee on the Legislative Branch of the Committee on Appropriations:
**(1)**
Mr. Carey.
**(2)**
Mr. Morelle.
**(3)**
Ms. Johnson of Texas.
##### (b) Joint Committee on Printing
The following Members are hereby elected to the Joint Committee on Printing, to serve with the chair of the Committee on House Administration:
**(1)**
Mr. Morelle.
**(2)**
Mr. Murphy.
**(3)**
Ms. Sewell.
**(4)**
Mrs. Miller of Illinois.
