IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 202
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 10, 2025 Ms. Houlahan submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Lauren Boebert of Colorado for her recent disparaging and derogatory comments about Representative Al Green of Texas.
That—
**(1)**
Representative Lauren Boebert be censured;
**(2)**
Representative Lauren Boebert forthwith present herself in the well of the House of Representatives for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
) Representative Lauren Bobert be censured with the public reading of this resolution by the Speaker.
