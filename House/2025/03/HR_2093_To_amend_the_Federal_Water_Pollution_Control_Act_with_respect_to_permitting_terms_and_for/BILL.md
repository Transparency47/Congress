A BILL
To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act with respect to permitting terms, and for other purposes.
#### 1. National pollutant discharge elimination system (NPDES) terms
##### (a) In general
Section 402(b)(1)(B) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1342(b)(1)(B) ) is amended to read as follows:
(B) are for fixed terms— (i) not exceeding 10 years, for a permit issued to a State or municipality; and (ii) not exceeding 5 years, for a permit issued to any person not described in clause (i); and .
