March 27, 2025 Mr. Gill of Texas introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Northlake, Texas.
#### 1. Single, unique ZIP Code for Northlake, Texas
Not later than 270 days after the enactment of this section, the United States Postal service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Northlake, Texas.
