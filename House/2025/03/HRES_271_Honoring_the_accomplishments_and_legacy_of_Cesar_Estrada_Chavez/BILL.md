119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 271
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 31, 2025 Mr. Carbajal (for himself, Ms. Rivas , Ms. Barragán , Mr. Cisneros , Mr. Gomez , and Mr. García of Illinois ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Honoring the accomplishments and legacy of César Estrada Chávez.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the accomplishments and example of a great hero of the United States, César Estrada Chávez;
**(2)**
pledges to promote the legacy of César Estrada Chávez; and
**(3)**
encourages the people of the United States to commemorate the legacy of César Estrada Chávez and to always remember his great rallying cry: ¡Sí se puede! , which is Spanish for Yes, we can! .
