A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3817 Marysville Boulevard in Sacramento, California, as the Grantland Johnson Post Office .
#### 1. Grantland Johnson Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3817 Marysville Boulevard in Sacramento, California, shall be known and designated as the Grantland Johnson Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Grantland Johnson Post Office .
