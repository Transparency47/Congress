IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 197
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 5, 2025 Mr. Nehls (for himself, Mr. Jackson of Texas , Mr. Weber of Texas , Mr. Fallon , Mr. Fitzgerald , Mr. Moore of Alabama , Mr. Fry , Mr. Harris of North Carolina , Mr. Gill of Texas , Mrs. Miller of Illinois , Mr. Yakym , Mrs. Harshbarger , Mr. Barr , Mr. Collins , Mr. Steube , Mr. Green of Tennessee , Mr. Cline , Mr. Van Orden , Mr. Van Drew , Ms. Van Duyne , Mr. Rulli , Mr. Ellzey , Mr. Williams of Texas , Mr. Pfluger , Ms. Greene of Georgia , Mr. Kiley of California , Mrs. Houchin , Mr. Luttrell , Mr. Gooden , Mr. Timmons , Mr. Self , Mr. Langworthy , Mr. Crenshaw , Mr. Hamadeh of Arizona , Mr. Onder , Mr. Arrington , and Mr. Carter of Texas ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Al Green of Texas.
That—
**(1)**
Representative Al Green of Texas be censured;
**(2)**
Representative Al Green forthwith present himself in the well of the House of Representatives for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
Representative Al Green be censured with the public reading of this resolution by the Speaker.
