119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 2175
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 18, 2025 Mr. Carbajal introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 130 South Patterson Avenue in Santa Barbara, California, as the Brigadier General Frederick R. Lopez Post Office Building .
#### 1. Brigadier General Frederick R. Lopez Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 130 South Patterson Avenue in Santa Barbara, California, shall be known and designated as the Brigadier General Frederick R. Lopez Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Brigadier General Frederick R. Lopez Post Office Building .
