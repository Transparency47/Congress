## Summary

**Harmful Algal Bloom Disaster Relief Act**

This bill explicitly authorizes the President to declare that a major disaster exists when algal blooms cause damage sufficient to warrant federal major disaster assistance.

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 3, 2025 Mr. Donalds (for himself and Ms. Wasserman Schultz ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To amend the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act to include algal blooms in the definition of a major disaster, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Harmful Algal Bloom Disaster Relief Act .
#### 2. Algal blooms
Section 102(2) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act ( 42 U.S.C. 5122 ) is amended by striking or drought and inserting drought, or algal blooms .
