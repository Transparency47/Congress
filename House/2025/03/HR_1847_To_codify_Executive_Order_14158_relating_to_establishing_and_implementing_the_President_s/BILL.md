A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14158 relating to establishing and implementing the President’s Department of Government Efficiency.
#### 1. Codifying Executive Order 14158 relating to establishing and implementing the President’s Department of Government Efficiency
Executive Order 14158 (90 Fed. Reg. 8441; relating to establishing and implementing the President’s Department of Government Efficiency) shall have the force and effect of law.
