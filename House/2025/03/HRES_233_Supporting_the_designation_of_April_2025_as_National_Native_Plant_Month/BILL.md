IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 233
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 21, 2025 Mr. Case (for himself, Mr. Neguse , Mr. Joyce of Ohio , Mr. Soto , Mr. Moylan , and Ms. Tokuda ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of April 2025 as National Native Plant Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Native Plant Month ; and
**(2)**
recognizes the benefits of native plants to the environment and the economy of the United States.
