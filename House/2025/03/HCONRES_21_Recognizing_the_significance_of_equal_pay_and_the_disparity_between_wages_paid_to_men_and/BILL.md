CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Recognizing the significance of equal pay and the disparity between wages paid to men and women.
That Congress—
**(1)**
recognizes the disparity between wages paid to women and men and its impact on women, families, and the Nation; and
**(2)**
reaffirms its commitment to supporting equal pay and to narrowing the gender wage gap.
