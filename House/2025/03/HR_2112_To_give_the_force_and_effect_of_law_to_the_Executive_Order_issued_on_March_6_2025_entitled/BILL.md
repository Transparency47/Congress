A BILL
To give the force and effect of law to the Executive Order issued on March 6, 2025 entitled Establishment of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and United States Digital Asset Stockpile .
#### 1. Establishment of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and United States Digital Asset Stockpile
The Executive order issued on March 6, 2025 entitled Establishment of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and United States Digital Asset Stockpile shall have the force and effect on law.
