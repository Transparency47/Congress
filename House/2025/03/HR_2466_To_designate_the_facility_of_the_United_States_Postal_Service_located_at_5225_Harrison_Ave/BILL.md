119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 2466
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
March 27, 2025 Mr. Sorensen (for himself and Mr. LaHood ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5225 Harrison Avenue in Rockford, Illinois, as the Jay P. Larson Post Office Building .
#### 1. Jay P. Larson Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5225 Harrison Avenue in Rockford, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the Jay P. Larson Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Jay P. Larson Post Office Building .
