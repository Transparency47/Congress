## Summary

**Bureau of Prisons Pay Protection Act**

This bill provides appropriations for the salaries of Bureau of Prisons (BOP) personnel during any lapse in discretionary appropriations for any fiscal year (i.e., a government shutdown). During a lapse in appropriations, the bill provides appropriations for the salaries of correctional and non-correctional personnel at BOP facilities.

A BILL
To provide funding for personnel of Bureau of Prisons facilities.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Bureau of Prisons Pay Protection Act .
#### 2. Funding for personnel of Bureau of Prison facilities
Out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, there is appropriated such sums as are necessary to pay, during any period of a lapse in discretionary appropriations during any fiscal year, the salaries of the personnel, both correctional and non-correctional, of a facility of the Bureau of Prisons during such lapse.
