November 19, 2025 Mr. Fine (for himself and Mr. Harris of North Carolina ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To amend the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959 to require secret ballot elections of members for certain union officers.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protecting Union Representation and Elections Act or the PURE Act .
#### 2. Secret ballot for certain elections
##### (a) In general
Section 401 of the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959 ( 29 U.S.C. 481 ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (a)—
**(A)**
by striking either ; and
**(B)**
by striking or at a convention of delegates chosen by secret ballot ; and
**(2)**
in subsection (d), by striking or by labor organization officers representative of such members who have been elected by secret ballot .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the date that is 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act.
