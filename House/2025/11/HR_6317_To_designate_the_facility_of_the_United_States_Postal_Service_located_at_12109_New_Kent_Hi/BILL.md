119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6317
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 25, 2025 Mr. Wittman (for himself, Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia , Mr. Scott of Virginia , Ms. McClellan , Mr. McGuire , Mr. Cline , Mr. Vindman , Mr. Beyer , Mr. Griffith , Mr. Subramanyam , and Mr. Walkinshaw ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12109 New Kent Highway in New Kent, Virginia, as the Patricia Paige Post Office .
#### 1. Patricia Paige Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12109 New Kent Highway in New Kent, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Patricia Paige Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Patricia Paige Post Office .
