IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 7, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform , and in addition to the Committee on Appropriations , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To limit the provision of Federal funding to New York City during any period Zohran Mamdani is mayor, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Moving American Money Distant from Anti-National Interests Act or the MAMDANI Act .
#### 2. Limitation on Federal funds for New York City if Zohran Mamdani is mayor
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, during any period in which Zohran Mamdani is mayor of New York, New York—
**(1)**
any unobligated Federal funds available for New York, New York, for any purpose are hereby rescinded; and
**(2)**
no Federal funds may be obligated or expended for any purpose to New York, New York.
