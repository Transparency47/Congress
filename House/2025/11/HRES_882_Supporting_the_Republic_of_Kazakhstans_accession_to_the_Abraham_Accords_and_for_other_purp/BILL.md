119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 882
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 17, 2025 Mr. Goldman of Texas (for himself, Mrs. Wagner , Mr. Lawler , Mr. Huizenga , Mr. Wilson of South Carolina , and Mr. Gottheimer ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Supporting the Republic of Kazakhstan’s accession to the Abraham Accords, and for other purposes.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
welcomes the November 6, 2025, announcement regarding the Republic of Kazakhstan’s anticipated accession to the Abraham Accords, enhancing ties between Israel and Kazakhstan;
**(2)**
calls on the governments of the State of Israel and the Republic of Kazakhstan to encourage greater people-to-people ties in order to facilitate sustainability of the agreement;
**(3)**
supports the efforts of the United States to expand and strengthen the Abraham Accords;
**(4)**
urges other countries to normalize or deepen relations with Israel to bring peace and prosperity to the Middle East and neighboring regions, including by joining the Abraham Accords; and
**(5)**
supports Israel’s standing in the international community and its bilateral relationship with the United States, which serves to strengthen shared values and commitment to peace and prosperity.
