November 19, 2025 Mr. Whitesides (for himself, Ms. Matsui , Ms. Lofgren , Mr. Panetta , Mr. Costa , Ms. Brownley , Mr. Lieu , Mr. Garcia of California , and Mr. Peters ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 28201 Franklin Parkway in Santa Clarita, California, as the Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building .
#### 1. Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 28201 Franklin Parkway in Santa Clarita, California, shall be known and designated as the Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building .
