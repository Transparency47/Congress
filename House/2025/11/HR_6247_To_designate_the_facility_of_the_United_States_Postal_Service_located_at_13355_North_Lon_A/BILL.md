119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6247
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 21, 2025 Mr. Ciscomani (for himself, Mr. Stanton , Ms. Ansari , Mr. Biggs of Arizona , Mr. Crane , Mr. Gosar , Mrs. Grijalva , Mr. Hamadeh of Arizona , and Mr. Schweikert ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, as the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office .
#### 1. Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office .
