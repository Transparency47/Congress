A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1201 Calvert Street in Lincoln, Nebraska, as the Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building .
#### 1. Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1201 Calvert Street in Lincoln, Nebraska, shall be known and designated as the Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Commodore Dixie Kiefer Memorial Post Office Building .
