November 17, 2025 Mr. Kennedy of Utah introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 50 East 100 North in Moab, Utah, as the 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office .
#### 1. 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 50 East 100 North in Moab, Utah, as the 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the 2nd Lieutenant Mitchell Williams Post Office .
