IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 901
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 20, 2025 Mr. Steube submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Providing for the expulsion of Representative Sheila Cherfilus-McCormick from the United States House of Representatives.
That pursuant to article I, section 5, clause 2 of the Constitution of the United States, Representative Cherfilus-McCormick, be, and she hereby is, expelled from the United States House of Representatives.
