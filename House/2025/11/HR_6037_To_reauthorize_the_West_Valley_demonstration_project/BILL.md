A BILL
To reauthorize the West Valley demonstration project.
#### 1. West valley demonstration project
Section 3(a) of the West Valley Demonstration Project Act ( Public Law 96–368 ; 42 U.S.C. 2021a note) is amended by striking $75,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2020 through 2026 and inserting $150,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2037 .
