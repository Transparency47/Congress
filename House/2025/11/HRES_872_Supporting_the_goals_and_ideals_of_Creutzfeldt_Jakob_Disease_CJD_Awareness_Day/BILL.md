IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 872
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 10, 2025 Mr. Valadao (for himself, Mr. Joyce of Ohio , Mr. Fong , Mr. Bean of Florida , Mr. Nadler , Ms. Brown , Ms. Brownley , Mr. Buchanan , Mr. Swalwell , Mr. Tonko , and Mr. Costa ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD) Awareness Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD) Awareness Day ; and
**(2)**
recognizes the importance of raising awareness of this rare brain disorder.
