November 18, 2025 Mr. Tiffany (for himself, Mr. Steil , Mr. Van Orden , Ms. Moore of Wisconsin , Mr. Fitzgerald , Mr. Grothman , and Mr. Wied ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 202 South Chestnut Avenue in Marshfield, Wisconsin, as the Army Corporal Gordon Gordy Richard Schmoll Post Office Building .
#### 1. Army Corporal Gordon Gordy Richard Schmoll Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 202 South Chestnut Avenue in Marshfield, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the Army Corporal Gordon Gordy Richard Schmoll Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Army Corporal Gordon Gordy Richard Schmoll Post Office Building .
