A BILL
To amend the Coastal Zone Management Act of 1972 to allow the District of Columbia to receive Federal funding under such Act, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the District of Columbia Flood Prevention Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Eligibility of District of Columbia for Federal funding under Coastal Zone Management Act of 1972
Section 304(4) of the Coastal Zone Management Act of 1972 ( 16 U.S.C. 1453(4) ) is amended by inserting the District of Columbia, after the term also includes .
