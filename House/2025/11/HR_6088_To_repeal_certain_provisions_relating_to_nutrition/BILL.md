A BILL
To repeal certain provisions relating to nutrition.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring Food Security for American Families and Farmers Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Repeal
Sections 10101 through 10108 of the Act entitled An Act to provide for reconciliation pursuant to title II of H. Con. Res. 14 (119th Congress) are repealed, and any provisions of law amended by those sections are restored or revived as if those sections had not been enacted into law.
