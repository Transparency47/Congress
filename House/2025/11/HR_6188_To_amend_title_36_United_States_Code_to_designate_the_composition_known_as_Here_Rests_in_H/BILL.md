A BILL
To amend title 36, United States Code, to designate the composition known as Here Rests in Honored Glory by Donald B. Miller as the national hymn of the United States.
#### 1. National hymn
##### (a) In general
Chapter 3 of title 36, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
307. National hymn The composition consisting of the words and music known as Here Rests in Honored Glory by Donald B. Miller is designated as the national hymn of the United States. .
##### (b) Clerical amendment
The table of sections for chapter 3 of title 36, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
307. National hymn .
