119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6204
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 20, 2025 Ms. Lee of Nevada (for herself and Mr. Ciscomani ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To extend the authorization for a large-scale water recycling and reuse grant program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Large-Scale Water Recycling Reauthorization Act .
#### 2. Authorization extension
Section 40905(k) of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act ( Public Law 117–58 ) is amended by striking 5 years and inserting 10 years .
