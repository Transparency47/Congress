and\n**(D)**\nconsult National Laboratories during periodic updates to the verification process, in coordination with any relevant stakeholder advisory group convened under section 5.\n##### (d) Independent audits\n**(1) In general**\nThe Commission, in consultation with the Secretary, shall designate National Laboratories with necessary expertise, or other qualified institutions, to conduct independent audits of scorecards published under subsection (a) on a periodic or as-needed basis to ensure the accuracy, completeness, and integrity of reported data, methodologies, and performance metrics.\n**(2) Initiation**\nAn audit under this subsection may be initiated\u2014\n**(A)**\nat the discretion of the Secretary;\n**(B)**\nupon identification of material discrepancies in reported metrics;\n**(C)**\nin response to concerns raised by a stakeholder advisory group convened under section 5; or\n**(D)**\nas part of a randomized, rotating sample of reporting entities to support continuous oversight.\n**(3) Results**\nThe results of an audit conducted under this subsection shall be made publicly available not later than 2 months after completion of the audit.\n##### (e) Rulemaking\n**(1) In general**\nNot later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Commission shall issue a final rule to carry out this section.\n**(2) Department of Energy support**\nThe Secretary shall provide technical assistance, subject-matter expertise, and access to relevant data and tools to the Commission in developing the rule required to be published under this subsection.\n**(3) Inclusions**\nThe Commission shall include in the rule issued under this section\u2014\n**(A)**\nrequirements to ensure timely and consistent reporting, which may include requirements for data-sharing agreements, protocols for data access, and other mechanisms as necessary to facilitate the completion of scorecards;\n**(B)**\nallowance for the use of proxies, estimates, or approximations only\u2014\n**(i)**\nwhere direct data are unavailable; and\n**(ii)**\nif the proxies, estimates, or approximations are based on the best available data, transparently documented, subject to Commission review and approval, and updated as improved data become available;
