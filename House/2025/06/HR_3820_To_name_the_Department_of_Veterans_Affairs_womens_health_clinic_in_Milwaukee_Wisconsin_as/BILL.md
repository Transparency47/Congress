A BILL
To name the Department of Veterans Affairs women’s health clinic in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, as the Anna Mae Robertson VA Well Woman Clinic.
#### 1. Name of Department of Veterans Affairs women’s health clinic, Milwaukee, Wisconsin
The women’s health clinic (Red Team clinic) of the Clement J. Zablocki Veterans' Administration Medical Center in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, shall, after the date of the enactment of this Act, be known and designated as the Anna Mae Robertson VA Well Woman Clinic . Any reference to such women’s health clinic in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Anna Mae Robertson VA Well Woman Clinic.
