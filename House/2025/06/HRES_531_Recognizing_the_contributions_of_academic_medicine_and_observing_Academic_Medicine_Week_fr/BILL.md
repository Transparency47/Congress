119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 531
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 23, 2025 Ms. Castor of Florida (for herself and Mr. Fitzpatrick ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the contributions of academic medicine and observing Academic Medicine Week from June 23 through 27, 2025.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of “Academic Medicine Week”;
**(2)**
affirms the unique and critical impact of academic medicine on patients and communities nationwide;
**(3)**
recognizes the importance of ensuring strong Federal support for programs that support academic medicine’s leadership in medical research, cutting-edge patient care, innovative medical education, and critical community collaborations; and
**(4)**
encourages the people of the United States to recognize the critical role of the Nation’s medical schools and academic health systems and teaching hospitals in improving the health of all.
