119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3799
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 6, 2025 Mr. Burchett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To codify the executive order relating to Enforcing Commonsense Rules of the Road for America’s Truck Drivers.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Executive Order 14286 Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of executive order
Executive Order 14286 (relating to Enforcing Commonsense Rules of the Road for America's Truck Drivers (signed on April 28, 2025)) shall have the force and effect of law.
