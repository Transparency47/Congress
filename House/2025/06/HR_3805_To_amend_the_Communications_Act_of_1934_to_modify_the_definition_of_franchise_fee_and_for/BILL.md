IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 6, 2025 Mr. Carter of Louisiana introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend the Communications Act of 1934 to modify the definition of franchise fee, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protecting Community Television Act .
#### 2. Modifying the definition of franchise fee
Section 622(g)(1) of the Communications Act of 1934 ( 47 U.S.C. 542(g)(1) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking includes and inserting means ; and
**(2)**
by inserting other monetary before assessment .
