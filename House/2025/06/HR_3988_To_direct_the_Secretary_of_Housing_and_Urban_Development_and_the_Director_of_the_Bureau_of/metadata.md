Study on housing loss\n##### (a) In General\nThe Secretary of Housing and Urban Development and the Director of the Bureau of the Census shall jointly conduct a study about how Federal agencies identify and record cases of housing loss.\n##### (b) Consultation required\nWhen conducting the study required under subsection (a) the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development and the Director of the Bureau of the Census shall consult with\u2014\n**(1)**\nthe Director of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection;\n**(2)**\nthe Director of the Federal Housing Finance Agency; and\n**(E)**\nan assessment of the accuracy and reliability of the sources of the data in the data set;\n**(5)**\nrecommends ways in which Federal agencies can improve the identification and recording of cases of housing loss in the United States, including by collecting additional data, creating additional data sets, and consolidating data sets, if appropriate;
