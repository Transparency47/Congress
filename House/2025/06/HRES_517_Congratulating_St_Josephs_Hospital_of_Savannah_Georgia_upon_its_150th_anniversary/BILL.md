IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 517
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 17, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Congratulating St. Joseph’s Hospital of Savannah, Georgia, upon its 150th anniversary.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
congratulates St. Joseph's Hospital, the Sisters of Mercy, the hospital's past and present leadership team, and all its coworkers on the grand occasion of its 150th anniversary; and
**(2)**
extends best wishes for its continued service to the citizens of the City of Savannah and the surrounding regions.
