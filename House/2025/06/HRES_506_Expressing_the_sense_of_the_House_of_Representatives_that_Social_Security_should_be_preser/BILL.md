June 11, 2025 Ms. Tenney (for herself, Mr. Flood , Mr. Wittman , Mr. Ciscomani , Mr. Kelly of Pennsylvania , Mr. Higgins of Louisiana , Mr. Hamadeh of Arizona , and Ms. Malliotakis ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means
