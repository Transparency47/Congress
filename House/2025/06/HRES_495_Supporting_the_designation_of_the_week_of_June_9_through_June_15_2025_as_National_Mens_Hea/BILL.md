IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 495
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 10, 2025 Mr. Carter of Louisiana (for himself, Mr. Lieu , Mr. Menendez , Mr. McCormick , and Mr. Dunn of Florida ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of the week of June 9 through June 15, 2025, as National Men’s Health Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the annual National Men’s Health Week ; and
**(2)**
requests that the President issue a proclamation calling upon the people of the United States and interested groups to observe National Men’s Health Week with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
