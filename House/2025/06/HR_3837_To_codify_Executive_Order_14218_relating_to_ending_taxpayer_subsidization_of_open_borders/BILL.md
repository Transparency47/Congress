119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3837
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 9, 2025 Mr. Burchett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14218 (relating to ending taxpayer subsidization of open borders).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the EO 14218 Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order EO 14218, ending taxpayer subsidization of open borders
Executive Order 14218 (90 Fed. Reg. 10581; relating to ending taxpayer subsidization of open borders) shall have the force and effect of law.
