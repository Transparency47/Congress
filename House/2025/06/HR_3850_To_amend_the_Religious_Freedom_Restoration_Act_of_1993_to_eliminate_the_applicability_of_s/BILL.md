June 9, 2025 Ms. Norton introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend the Religious Freedom Restoration Act of 1993 to eliminate the applicability of such Act to the District of Columbia.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the District of Columbia Non-Discrimination Home Rule Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Elimination of applicability of Religious Freedom Restoration Act to the District of Columbia
Paragraph (2) of section 5 of the Religious Freedom Restoration Act of 1993 ( 42 U.S.C. 2000bb–2(2) ) is amended by striking the District of Columbia, and the comma after Puerto Rico .
