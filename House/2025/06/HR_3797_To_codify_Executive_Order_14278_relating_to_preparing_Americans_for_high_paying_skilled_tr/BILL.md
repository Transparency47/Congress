119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3797
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 6, 2025 Mr. Burchett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14278 (relating to preparing Americans for high-paying skilled trade jobs of the future).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the EO 14278 Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14278
Executive Order 14278 (90 Fed. Reg. 17525); (relating to preparing Americans for high-paying skilled trade jobs of the future) shall have the force and effect of law.
