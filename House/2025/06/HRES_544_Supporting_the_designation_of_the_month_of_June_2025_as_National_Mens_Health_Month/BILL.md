IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 544
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 25, 2025 Mr. Carter of Louisiana (for himself, Mr. McCormick , Mr. Dunn of Florida , Mr. Menendez , and Mr. Lieu ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of the month of June 2025, as National Men’s Health Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the annual National Men’s Health Month ; and
**(2)**
requests that the President issue a proclamation calling upon the people of the United States and interested groups to observe National Men’s Health Month with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
