A BILL
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to allow Medicare beneficiaries to choose their physical and occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, audiologists, and chiropractors.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Medicare Patient Choice Act .
#### 2. Free choice of therapists for Medicare beneficiaries
