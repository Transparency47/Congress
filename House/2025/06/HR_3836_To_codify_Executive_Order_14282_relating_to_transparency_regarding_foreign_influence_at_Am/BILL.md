119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3836
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 9, 2025 Mr. Burchett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14282 (relating to transparency regarding foreign influence at American universities).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the EO 14282 Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14282
Executive Order 14282 (90 Fed. Reg. 17541; relating to transparency regarding foreign influence at American universities) shall have the force and effect of law.
