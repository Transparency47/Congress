119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 460
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 3, 2025 Ms. Davids of Kansas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on House Administration
RESOLUTION
Commemorating and celebrating the extraordinary contributions of the late Honorable Raúl Grijalva, honoring his legacy of service, dedication, and unwavering advocacy for the people of Arizona and indigenous communities across the country.
That—
**(1)**
the House of Representatives commemorates and celebrates the extraordinary contributions of Raúl M. Grijalva, honoring his legacy of service, dedication, and unwavering advocacy for the people of Arizona and indigenous communities across the country; and
**(2)**
this resolution serves as a testament to his work for the people of Arizona’s 7th Congressional district and lifetime of dedication to issues impacting Indian Country.
