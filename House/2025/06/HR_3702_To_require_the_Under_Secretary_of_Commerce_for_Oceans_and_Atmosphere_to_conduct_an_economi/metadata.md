Economic study on financial costs of extreme heat\n##### (a) Study\nThe Under Secretary, acting through the Climate Program Office of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration and in coordination with the member agencies of the National Integrated Heat Health Information System, shall conduct an economic impact study of the financial costs of extreme heat, including by quantifying the dollar value of loss of life and property specific to extreme heat.\n##### (b) Elements\nThe study required under subsection (a) shall\u2014\n**(1)**\nevaluate broad health impacts of extreme heat, including mortality and morbidity, in coordination with State and national public health departments, following standard methodologies for valuing health and life;\n**(2)**\nevaluate property damage or property loss from extreme heat, to the extent practicable;
