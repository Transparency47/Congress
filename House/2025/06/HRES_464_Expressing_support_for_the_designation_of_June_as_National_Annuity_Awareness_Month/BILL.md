IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 464
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 3, 2025 Mr. Nunn of Iowa (for himself and Ms. Pettersen ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of June as National Annuity Awareness Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of National Annuity Awareness Month ; and
**(2)**
calls on the United States Government, the States, localities, schools, nonprofit organizations, businesses, and the people of the United States to observe National Annuity Awareness Month with appropriate programs and activities.
