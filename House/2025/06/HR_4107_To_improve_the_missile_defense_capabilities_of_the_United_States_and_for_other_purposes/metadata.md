Government will deter and defend the citizens and critical infrastructure of the United States against any foreign attack on the United States homeland; and\n**(C)**\nthe Federal Government will including from both missiles and unmanned systems.\n**(2) All-domain awareness**\nThe strategy developed pursuant to paragraph (1) shall include plans for a system of layered sensors from the seafloor
