H. R. 3883
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 10, 2025 Ms. Mace introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To require the Secretary of State to designate the Muslim Brotherhood as a foreign terrorist organization.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Muslim Brotherhood Is a Terrorist Organization Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Designation of the Muslim Brotherhood as a Foreign Terrorist Organization
The Secretary of State shall designate the Muslim Brotherhood as a foreign terrorist organization pursuant to section 219 of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1189 ).
