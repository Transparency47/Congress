H. R. 3675
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 3, 2025 Mr. Burchett (for himself and Mrs. Luna ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14290 (relating to ending taxpayer subsidization of biased media).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the EO 14290 Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of executive order 14290
Executive Order 14290 (90 Fed. Reg. 19415; relating to ending taxpayer subsidization of biased media) shall have the force and effect of law.
