IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 511
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 12, 2025 Mrs. Foushee submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
RESOLUTION
Honoring the unbreakable spirit, bravery, and legacy of women veterans and supporting recognition of Women Veterans Recognition Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes, honors, and celebrates the contributions, sacrifices, and achievements of women veterans, past and present; and
**(2)**
expresses support for the designation of Women Veterans Recognition Day .
