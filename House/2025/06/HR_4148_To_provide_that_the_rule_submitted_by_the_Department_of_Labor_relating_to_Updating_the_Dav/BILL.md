June 25, 2025 Mr. Smucker (for himself, Ms. Foxx , Ms. Van Duyne , Mr. Thompson of Pennsylvania , Mr. Hudson , Mr. Moolenaar , Mrs. Miller of Illinois , Mr. Mann , Mr. Self , Ms. Letlow , Mr. Rose , Mr. Williams of Texas , Mr. Weber of Texas , Mr. Sessions , Mrs. Houchin , and Mr. Baird ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To provide that the rule submitted by the Department of Labor relating to Updating the Davis-Bacon and Related Acts Regulation shall have no force or effect.
#### 1. Disapproval of regulation
The final rule submitted by the Department of Labor relating to Updating the Davis-Bacon and Related Acts Regulation (88 Fed. Reg. 57526 (August 23, 2023)) shall have no force or effect.
