A BILL
To amend section 235 of the Immigration and Nationality Act to remove certain exceptions with respect to the inspection of applicants for admission.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Expedited Removal Expansion Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Inspection of Applicants for Admission
