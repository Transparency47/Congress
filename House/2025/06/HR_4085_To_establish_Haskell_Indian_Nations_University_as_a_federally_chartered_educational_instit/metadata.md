and\n**(B)**\nserve for a term of 2 years.\n**(3) Vacancies**\nIn the case of a vacancy in the office of Chairman or Vice Chairman of the Board\u2014\n**(A)**\nthe vacancy shall be filled by the members of the Board appointed pursuant to subsection (b)(1)(A)(i); Powers of the Board\n##### (a) In general\nIn carrying out this Act, the Board shall have the power, in accordance with this Act\u2014\n**(1)**\nto adopt, use, and alter a corporate seal;\n**(2)**\nto make agreements and contracts with persons, Indian Tribes, and private or governmental entities;\n**(3)**\nto sue and be sued in its corporate name and to complain and defend in any court of competent jurisdiction;\n**(4)**\nto represent itself, or to contract for representation, in all judicial, legal, and other proceedings;\n**(5)**\n**(A)**\nwith the approval of the Federal agency concerned, to make use of services, facilities, and property of any board, commission, independent establishment, or executive agency or department of the Executive Branch in carrying out this Act;
