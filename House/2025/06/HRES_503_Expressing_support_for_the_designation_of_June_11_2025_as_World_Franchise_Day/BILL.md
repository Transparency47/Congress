1st Session
H. RES. 503
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 11, 2025 Mr. Hern of Oklahoma (for himself, Ms. Van Duyne , Ms. Scholten , Mr. Carter of Louisiana , Mr. Wied , Mr. Gray , Mr. McGarvey , Mr. Mullin , Mr. Costa , Mr. Panetta , Mr. Gooden , and Ms. Bynum ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform

Expressing support for the designation of June 11, 2025, as World Franchise Day .
That the House of Representatives expresses support for the designation of World Franchise Day .
