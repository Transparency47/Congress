## Summary

**EO 14285 Act of 2025**

This bill provides statutory authority for Executive Order 14285, which expedites the process for reviewing and issuing permits and licenses for seabed mineral resource development.

119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3803
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
June 6, 2025 Mr. Burchett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources , and in addition to the Committees on Armed Services , Foreign Affairs , and Financial Services , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14285, titled Unleashing America’s Offshore Critical Minerals and Resources .
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the EO 14285 Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14285
Executive Order 14285 (90 Fed. Reg. 17735, relating to seabed mineral resource development) shall have the force and effect of law.
