April 8, 2025 Mrs. Torres of California introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To require approval from Congress for a certain reduction of Federal Aviation Administration workforce, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Air Traffic Controller Protection Act or the ATC Protection Act .
#### 2. Reduction, replacement, or outsource of FAA staffing
##### (a) Reduction in staffing
**(1) In general**
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, in order for the Secretary of Transportation to reduce, replace, or outsource 1 percent or more of the workforce of the Federal Aviation Administration, the Secretary shall obtain explicit approval from Congress.
**(2) Report to Congress**
Before seeking approval from Congress under paragraph (1), the Secretary of Transportation shall submit to Congress a report detailing the rationale for any decision to reduce, replace, or outsource 1 percent or more of the workforce of the Federal Aviation Administration as well as any analysis on how such decision will impact the aviation and transportation system at large.
##### (b) DOGE control
The Administrator of the Department of Governmental Efficiency may not exercise control over the Federal Aviation Administration.
##### (c) ATC privatization
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the air traffic control system of the Federal Aviation Administration may not be privatized or outsourced from the Federal Aviation Administration.
