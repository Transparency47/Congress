119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 322
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 9, 2025 Mrs. Torres of California (for herself and Mr. Fitzpatrick ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of National Public Safety Telecommunicators Week.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of National Public Safety Telecommunicators Week;
**(2)**
honors and recognizes the important and lifesaving contributions of public safety telecommunications professionals in the United States; and
**(3)**
encourages the people of the United States to remember the value of the work performed by public safety telecommunications professionals.
