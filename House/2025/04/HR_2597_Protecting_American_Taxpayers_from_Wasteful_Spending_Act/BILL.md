119th CONGRESS

H. R. 2597
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 2, 2025 Mrs. Hinson introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14249 (relating to protecting America’s bank account against fraud, waste, and abuse).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protecting American Taxpayers from Wasteful Spending Act .
#### 2. Codification of Executive Order 14249, relating to protecting America’s bank account against fraud, waste, and abuse
Executive Order 14249 (90 Fed. Reg. 14011; relating to protecting America’s bank account against fraud, waste, and abuse, signed March 25, 2025), shall have the force and effect of law.
