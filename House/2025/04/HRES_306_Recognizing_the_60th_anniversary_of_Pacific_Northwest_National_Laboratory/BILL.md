IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 306
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 8, 2025 Mr. Newhouse (for himself, Ms. Schrier , and Ms. DelBene ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 60th anniversary of Pacific Northwest National Laboratory.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
congratulates Pacific Northwest National Laboratory on six decades of scientific brilliance;
**(2)**
recognizes the contributions of Pacific Northwest National Laboratory working on some of the United States most complex and urgent challenges; and
**(3)**
looks forward to continued innovation and scientific discovery that will make the Nation and the world a better and safer place.
