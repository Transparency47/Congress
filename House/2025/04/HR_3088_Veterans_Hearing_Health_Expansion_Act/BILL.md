119th CONGRESS

H. R. 3088
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 30, 2025 Mr. Barrett introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to include licensed hearing aid specialists under the Veterans Community Care Program of the Department of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Veterans Hearing Health Expansion Act .
#### 2. Inclusion of licensed hearing aid specialists in Veterans Community Care Program of the Department of Veterans Affairs
Section 1703(c) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by—
**(1)**
redesignating paragraphs (2) through (5) as paragraphs (3) through (6), respectively; and
**(2)**
by inserting after paragraph (1) the following new paragraph:
(2) A hearing aid specialist licensed to furnish medical services in the State in which such hearing aid specialist is located. .
