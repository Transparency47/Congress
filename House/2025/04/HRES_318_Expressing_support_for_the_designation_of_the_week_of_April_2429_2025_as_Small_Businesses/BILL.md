IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 318
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 9, 2025 Mrs. Miller of West Virginia submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Small Business
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of the week of April 24–29, 2025, as Small Businesses in For-Hire Transportation Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of Small Businesses in For-Hire Transportation Week in honor of the commitment and essential services provided by the for-hire transportation industry; and
**(2)**
recognizes the efforts of small business in the for-hire transportation industry to promote safety and support for our communities.
