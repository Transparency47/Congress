1st Session
H. R. 2955
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 17, 2025 Mr. Peters (for himself and Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
A BILL
To amend title 10, United States Code, to modify the definition of short-term work for purposes of Navy construction of combatant and escort vessels and assignment of vessel projects.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Smart Ship Repair Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Definition of short-term work for purposes of Navy construction of combatant and escort vessels and assignment of vessel projects
Section 8669a(c)(4) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking 12 months and inserting 18 months .
