IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 10, 2025 Mr. Rose (for himself and Ms. De La Cruz ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services
A BILL
To require the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection to issue a rule before deleting or modifying certain small business loan data, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Bank Loan Privacy Act .
#### 2. Rulemaking requirement
Section 704B(e)(4) of the Equal Credit Opportunity Act ( 15 U.S.C. 1691c–2(e)(4) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking The Bureau may, and inserting:
(A) In general The Bureau may, ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following:
(B) Rulemaking requirement The Bureau shall, before deleting or modifying data under this paragraph issue, through advance notice and comment, a rule that includes a description of what modifications and deletions the Bureau intends to make to the data and how such modifications and deletions will advance a privacy interest. .
