April 17, 2025 Mr. Kennedy of New York introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Wheatfield, New York.
#### 1. Single, unique ZIP Code for Wheatfield, New York
Not later than 270 days after the enactment of this section, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Wheatfield, New York.
