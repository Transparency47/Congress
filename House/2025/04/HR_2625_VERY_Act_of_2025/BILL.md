## Summary

**Veterans Employment Readiness Yield Act of 2025 or the VERY Act of 2025**

This bill revises terminology used under the Veteran Readiness and Employment program, specifically by replacing the use of *employment handicap* with *employment barrier* .

119th CONGRESS
1st Session

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 3, 2025 Mr. Davis of North Carolina (for himself and Mr. Ciscomani ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to update certain terminology regarding veteran employment.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Veterans Employment Readiness Yield Act of 2025 or the VERY Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Update of certain terminology regarding veteran employment
Title 38, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
by striking employment handicap each place it occurs and inserting employment barrier ; and
**(2)**
by striking employment handicaps each place it occurs and inserting employment barriers .
