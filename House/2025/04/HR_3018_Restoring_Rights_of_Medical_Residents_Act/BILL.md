119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3018
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 24, 2025 Mrs. Spartz introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend the Pension Funding Equity Act of 2004 to repeal the antitrust exemption applicable to graduate medical resident matching programs.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring Rights of Medical Residents Act .
#### 2. Repealer
Section 207 of the Pension Funding Equity Act of 2004 ( 15 U.S.C. 37B ) is repealed.
#### 3. Effective date
This Act shall take effect on the March 18 that 1st occurs after the date of the enactment of this Act.
