A BILL
To amend the Advancing Research to Prevent Suicide Act to expand the areas of focus regarding childhood suicide, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Youth Suicide Prevention Research Act .
#### 2. NSF research regarding childhood suicide
Subsection (a) of section 3 of the Advancing Research to Prevent Suicide Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1862v ) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (4), by striking and after the semicolon;
**(2)**
by redesignating paragraph (5) as paragraph (7); and
**(3)**
by inserting after paragraph (4) the following new paragraphs:
(5) basic understanding of the role of adverse childhood experiences; (6) basic understanding of the role of toxic stress in childhood; and .
