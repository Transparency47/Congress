119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 304
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 8, 2025 Mr. Kennedy of New York submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Rules
RESOLUTION
Amending House Resolution 211 to ensure that days occurring during the first session of the One Hundred Nineteenth Congress constitute calendar days for purposes of section 202 of the National Emergencies Act ( 50 U.S.C. 1622 ) with respect to a joint resolution terminating a national emergency declared by the President on February 1, 2025.
#### 1. Ensuring days occurring during first session constitute calendar days for purposes of joint resolution terminating a national emergency declared by the President on February 1, 2025
##### (a) Treatment of days as calendar days
House Resolution 211, agreed to March 11, 2025, is amended by striking section 4.
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the adoption of House Resolution 211.
