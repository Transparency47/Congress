April 29, 2025 Ms. Titus (for herself and Ms. Omar ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform , and in addition to the Committees on Energy and Commerce , and the Judiciary , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To repeal section 704(b)(12) of the Office of National Drug Control Policy Reauthorization Act of 1998, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Evidence-Based Drug Policy Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Repeal of section 704(b)(12) of the Office of National Drug Control Policy Reauthorization Act of 1998
Section 704(b)(12) of the Office of National Drug Control Policy Reauthorization Act of 1998 ( 21 U.S.C. 1703(b)(12) ) is repealed.
