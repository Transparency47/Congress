A BILL
To amend title 18, United States Code, to increase the scope of the penalty for crimes against children.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Paula Bohovesky and Joan D’Alessandro Act .
#### 2. Death or Imprisonment for Crimes Against Children
Section 3559(d)(1)(A) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after 14 years the following: or if the victim has not attained the age of 18 years if the conviction involved a sexual offense .
