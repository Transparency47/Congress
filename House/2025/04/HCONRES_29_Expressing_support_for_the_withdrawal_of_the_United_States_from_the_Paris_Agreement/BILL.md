IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. CON. RES. 29
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 21, 2025 Mr. Barr (for himself and Mr. Moore of West Virginia ) submitted the following concurrent resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the withdrawal of the United States from the Paris Agreement.
That Congress supports the action of the President to withdraw the United States from the Paris Agreement. In the preceding sentence, the term Paris Agreement means the decision by the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change’s 21st Conference of Parties in Paris, France, adopted December 12, 2015.
