119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 334
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 17, 2025 Mr. Kennedy of New York submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Rules
RESOLUTION
Amending House Resolution 211 with respect to a national emergency declared by the President on February 1, 2025, and House Resolution 313 with respect to a national emergency declared by the President on April 2, 2025.
#### 1. Ensuring days occurring during first session constitute calendar days for purposes of joint resolution terminating a national emergency declared by the President on February 1, 2025
##### (a) Treatment of days as calendar days
House Resolution 211, agreed to March 11, 2025, is amended by striking section 4.
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the adoption of House Resolution 211.
#### 2. Ensuring days occurring during first session constitute calendar days for purposes of joint resolution terminating a national emergency declared by the President on April 2, 2025
##### (a) Treatment of days as calendar days
House Resolution 313, agreed to April 9, 2025, is amended by striking section 2.
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the adoption of House Resolution 313.
