This joint resolution withdraws congressional approval of the World Trade Organization Agreement, which established the World Trade Organization.

119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. J. RES. 93
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 10, 2025 Mr. Tiffany submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means
JOINT RESOLUTION
Withdrawing approval of the Agreement Establishing the World Trade Organization.
That the Congress withdraws its approval, provided under section 101(a) of the Uruguay Round Agreements Act, of the WTO Agreement as defined in section 2(9) of that Act.
