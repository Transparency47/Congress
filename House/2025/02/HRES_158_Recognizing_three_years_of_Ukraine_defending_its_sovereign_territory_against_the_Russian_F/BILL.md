IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 158
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 24, 2025 Mr. Panetta (for himself, Mr. Wilson of South Carolina , Mr. Keating , and Mr. Bacon ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Recognizing three years of Ukraine defending its sovereign territory against the Russian Federation’s second unprovoked assault and full-scale invasion.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
stands unequivocally with the people of Ukraine as they continue to defend their people and territory in the third year of the Russian Federation’s unprovoked full-scale invasion against Ukraine;
**(2)**
condemns the Russian Federation’s full-scale invasion into Ukraine and its subsequent collaboration with the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, the Islamic Republic of Iran, and the People’s Republic of China to conduct crimes against humanity including by providing soldiers and weapons to use against the Ukrainian people;
**(3)**
supports the continued collaboration among key allies and partners of the United States to strengthen Ukraine’s military, governance, and economy;
**(4)**
supports using Russian frozen assets to support Ukraine’s wartime needs and the continued application of sanctions against the Russian Federation and adversaries of the United States who have provided material and personnel support; and
**(5)**
reaffirms the United States commitment to the Ukrainian people as they fight to preserve their territory, culture, and history from the Russian Federation, the Islamic Republic of Iran, and the People’s Republic of China.
