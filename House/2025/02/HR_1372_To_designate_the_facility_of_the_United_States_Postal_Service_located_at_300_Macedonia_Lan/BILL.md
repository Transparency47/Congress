I
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 1372
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 14, 2025 Mr. Burchett (for himself, Mr. Green of Tennessee , Mr. Fleischmann , Mr. Rose , Mr. Cohen , Mrs. Harshbarger , Mr. Ogles , Mr. DesJarlais , and Mr. Kustoff ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 300 Macedonia Lane in Knoxville, Tennessee, as the Reverend Harold Middlebrook Post Office Building .
#### 1. Reverend Harold Middlebrook Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 300 Macedonia Lane in Knoxville, Tennessee, shall be known and designated as the Reverend Harold Middlebrook Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Reverend Harold Middlebrook Post Office Building .
