IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 165
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 25, 2025 Ms. Velázquez (for herself and Mr. Jackson of Illinois ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of February 28, 2025, as Community Arts Education Day.
That the House of Representatives supports Community Arts Education Day and recognizes Community Arts Education Day is more crucial than ever as a vehicle for activism, individual and collective self-expression, and healing.
