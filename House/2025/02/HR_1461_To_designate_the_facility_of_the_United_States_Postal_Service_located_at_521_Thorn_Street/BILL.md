119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 1461
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 21, 2025 Mr. Deluzio (for himself, Mr. Fitzpatrick , Mr. Boyle of Pennsylvania , Ms. Scanlon , Ms. Dean of Pennsylvania , Mr. Smucker , Ms. Lee of Pennsylvania , and Mr. Joyce of Pennsylvania ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 521 Thorn Street in Sewickley, Pennsylvania, as the Mary Elizabeth Bettie Cole Post Office Building .
#### 1. Mary Elizabeth Bettie Cole Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 521 Thorn Street in Sewickley, Pennsylvania, shall be known and designated as the Mary Elizabeth Bettie Cole Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Mary Elizabeth Bettie Cole Post Office Building .
