# HR 1044: To amend Public Law 99-338 with respect to Kaweah Project permits.

- Congress: 119
- Origin chamber: House
- Introduced: 2025-02-06
- Latest action: 2025-02-06 - Introduced in House
- Policy area: Water Resources Development
- Sponsor: Rep. Valadao, David G. [R-CA-22]
- Congress.gov: https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/house-bill/1044
- Date accessed: 2026-06-01T04:21:25.748028+00:00

## Summary

This bill authorizes the Department of the Interior to issue seven (currently, three) renewals for portions of the Kaweah Project, which is an existing hydroelectric project in Tulare County, California.

A BILL
To amend Public Law 99–338 with respect to Kaweah Project permits.
#### 1. Amendment to Kaweah Project provision
The first section of Public Law 99–338 , as amended by Public Law 108–447 , is amended—
