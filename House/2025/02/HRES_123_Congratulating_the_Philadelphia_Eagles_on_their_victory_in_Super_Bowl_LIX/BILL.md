IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 123
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 10, 2025 Mr. Boyle of Pennsylvania (for himself, Mr. Evans of Pennsylvania , Mr. Norcross , Ms. Scanlon , Ms. Dean of Pennsylvania , Mr. Fitzpatrick , and Ms. Houlahan ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the Philadelphia Eagles on their victory in Super Bowl LIX.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
congratulates the Philadelphia Eagles on their hard-fought victory in Super Bowl LIX; and
**(2)**
recognizes the achievements of all the players, coaches, and staff who contributed to the victory.
