IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 129
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 13, 2025 Mr. Alford submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of February 9, 2025, as the first ever Gulf of America Day and celebrating the importance of changing the Gulf of Mexico to the Gulf of America.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of the first ever Gulf of America Day ; and
**(2)**
celebrates the importance of changing the Gulf of Mexico to the Gulf of America.
