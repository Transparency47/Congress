## Summary

This resolution condemns Azerbaijan for its treatment of Dr. Gubad Ibadoghlu, practice of wrongful detention, and suppression of academic freedom. The resolution also calls for Dr. Ibadoghlu's immediate and unconditional release and for the Department of State to prioritize his well-being and release in all engagements with Azerbaijan.

IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 120
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 6, 2025 Mr. Rouzer (for himself, Mr. Pallone , Mrs. Kim , Mr. Costa , Mr. Valadao , Mr. Davis of North Carolina , Mr. Lawler , Mr. Gottheimer , Ms. Malliotakis , Ms. Meng , Mr. McCormick , Mr. Moulton , Mr. Bilirakis , Mrs. Foushee , Mr. Smith of New Jersey , Ms. Norton , Mr. Doggett , Ms. Titus , Mr. Veasey , Mr. Cohen , Mr. Raskin , Mr. Schneider , and Mr. McGovern ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Condemning the treatment of Dr. Gubad Ibadoghlu by the Government of Azerbaijan and urging his immediate release, and for other purposes.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
condemns the treatment of Dr. Ibadoghlu by the Government of Azerbaijan, their practice of wrongful detention, and their suppression of academic freedom;
**(2)**
calls for Dr. Ibadoghlu’s immediate and unconditional release; and
**(3)**
urges the Secretary of State to continue prioritizing Dr. Ibadoghlu’s well-being and release in all engagements with the Government of Azerbaijan.
