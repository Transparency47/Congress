A BILL
To amend the Save Our Seas 2.0 Act to expand eligibility for certain wastewater infrastructure grants, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Wastewater infrastructure and trash-free waters grants
Section 302 of the Save Our Seas 2.0 Act ( 33 U.S.C. 4282 ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (c)(1)—
**(A)**
by inserting States, before municipalities ; and
**(B)**
by inserting a comma before or Indian Tribes ; and
**(2)**
in subsection (d)(1), by inserting States, before units of local government .
