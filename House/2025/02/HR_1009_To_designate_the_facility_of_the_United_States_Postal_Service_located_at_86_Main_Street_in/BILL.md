119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 1009
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 5, 2025 Mr. Lawler (for himself, Mr. Garbarino , Mr. Kennedy of New York , Ms. Malliotakis , Ms. Meng , Mr. Morelle , Mr. Ryan , Ms. Tenney , Mr. Tonko , Mr. Torres of New York , and Mr. Latimer ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 86 Main Street in Haverstraw, New York, as the Paul Piperato Post Office Building .
#### 1. Paul Piperato Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 86 Main Street in Haverstraw, New York, shall be known and designated as the Paul Piperato Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Paul Piperato Post Office Building .
