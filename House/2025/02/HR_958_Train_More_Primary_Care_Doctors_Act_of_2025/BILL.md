## Summary

**Train More Primary Care Doctors Act of 2025**

This bill reauthorizes through FY2030 the Health Resources and Services Administration’s Primary Care Training and Enhancement program, which provides various grants to entities such as hospitals and schools for enhancing training and education for the primary care workforce.

A BILL
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize funding for grants and contracts for primary care training and enhancement, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Train More Primary Care Doctors Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Primary care training and enhancement
Section 747(c)(1) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 293k(c)(1) ) is amended by striking $48,924,000 for each of fiscal years 2021 through 2025 and inserting $49,924,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2030 .
