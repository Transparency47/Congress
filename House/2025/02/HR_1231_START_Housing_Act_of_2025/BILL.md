A BILL
To reauthorize and expand the pilot program to help individuals in recovery from a substance use disorder become stably housed, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Supporting Transition And Recovery Through Housing Act of 2025 or the START Housing Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Reauthorization of Recovery Housing Program
