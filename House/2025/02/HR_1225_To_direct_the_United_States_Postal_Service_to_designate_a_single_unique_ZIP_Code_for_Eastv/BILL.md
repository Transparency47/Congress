February 12, 2025 Mr. Calvert (for himself and Mrs. Torres of California ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Eastvale, California.
#### 1. Single, unique ZIP code for Eastvale, California
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this section, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Eastvale, California.
