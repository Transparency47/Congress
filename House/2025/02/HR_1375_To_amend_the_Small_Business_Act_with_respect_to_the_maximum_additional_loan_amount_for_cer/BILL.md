A BILL
To amend the Small Business Act with respect to the maximum additional loan amount for certain disaster loans, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Maximum additional loan amount for certain disaster loans
Section 7(b)(1)(A) of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 636(b)(1)(A) ) is amended by striking 20 per centum and inserting 30 percent .
