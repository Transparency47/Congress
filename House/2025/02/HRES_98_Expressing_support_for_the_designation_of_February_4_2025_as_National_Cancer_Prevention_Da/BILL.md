119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 98
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 4, 2025 Mrs. Dingell (for herself and Mr. James ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of February 4, 2025, as National Cancer Prevention Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Cancer Prevention Day ;
**(2)**
recognizes all efforts to raise the awareness for the reduction of cancer risks; and
**(3)**
recognizes the devastating effect cancer has on families and wishes to expand knowledge, encourage early detection, and work with friends in the medical and scientific fields to put an end to this deadly disease.
