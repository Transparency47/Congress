IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 132
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
February 13, 2025 Ms. Mace submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics
RESOLUTION
Censuring Representative Robert Garcia of California for inciting violence against a special government employee.
That—
**(1)**
Representative Robert Garcia of California be censured;
**(2)**
Representative Robert Garcia forthwith present himself in the well of the House of Representatives for the pronouncement of censure; and
**(3)**
Representative Robert Garcia be censured with the public reading of this resolution by the Speaker.
