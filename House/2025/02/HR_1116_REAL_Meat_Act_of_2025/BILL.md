## Summary

**Right to Eat Authentic and Legitimate Meat Act of 2025 or the REAL Meat Act of 2025**

This bill prohibits the use of federal funds to support, directly or indirectly, the production, advancement, or enhancement of cell-cultured meat. Under the bill, *cell-cultured meat* means meat that is sourced from the cells of animals and artificially produced in a laboratory.

The bill includes an exemption for federal funds of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) that are used for activities related to cell-cultured meat that is intended for off-planet consumption.

A BILL
To prohibit the use of Federal funds to support cell-cultured meat, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Right to Eat Authentic and Legitimate Meat Act of 2025 or the REAL Meat Act of 2025 .
#### 2. No Federal funds supporting cell-cultured meat
##### (a) In general
Subject to subsection (b), no Federal funds may be used to support, directly or indirectly, the production, advancement, or enhancement of cell-cultured meat, including through—
**(1)**
conducting or supporting research on cell-cultured meat;
**(2)**
supporting meatpacking plants, organizations, or groups relating to cell-cultured meat;
**(3)**
allowing for the receipt of any assistance or other benefit under a program administered by the Secretary of Agriculture for any product that includes as an ingredient cell-cultured meat; and
**(4)**
the promotion and advertisement of cell-cultured meat.
##### (b) NASA exemption
The prohibition described in subsection (a) shall not apply to Federal funds of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration used to support, directly or indirectly, the production, advancement, or enhancement of cell-cultured meat that is intended for off-planet consumption.
##### (c) Cell-Cultured meat defined
In this section, the term cell-cultured meat means meat that is sourced from the cells of animals and artificially produced in a laboratory.
