(3) Reprocessing of materials A Community Reclaimer may reprocess materials recovered during the implementation of a remediation plan only if\u2014 (A) the applicable land management agency has signed a decision document approving reprocessing as part of the approved abandoned mine reclamation plan of the State; (B) the proceeds from the sale or use of the materials are used\u2014 (i) to defray the costs of the remediation; and (ii) to reimburse the Administrator or the head of a Federal land management agency for the purpose of carrying out this Act; and (C) the materials only include historic mine residue.
