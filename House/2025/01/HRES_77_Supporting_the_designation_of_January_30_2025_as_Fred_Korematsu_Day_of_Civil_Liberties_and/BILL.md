## Summary

This resolution supports the designation of Fred Korematsu Day of Civil Liberties and the Constitution.

It also encourages all people to reflect on the importance of political leadership and vigilance and on the values of justice and civil rights during times of uncertainty and emergency.

IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 77
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 28, 2025 Mr. Takano (for himself, Ms. Tokuda , and Ms. Matsui ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of January 30, 2025, as Fred Korematsu Day of Civil Liberties and the Constitution .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of a Fred Korematsu Day of Civil Liberties and the Constitution ;
**(2)**
recognizes Fred Korematsu’s bravery and resilience in the face of adversity; and
**(3)**
encourages all people to reflect on the importance of political leadership and vigilance and on the values of justice and civil rights during times of uncertainty and emergency.
