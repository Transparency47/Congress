119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 24
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 9, 2025 Ms. Greene of Georgia (for herself, Mrs. Miller of Illinois , Mr. Weber of Texas , Mr. Collins , Mr. Crane , Ms. Malliotakis , Mr. Moylan , Mr. Nehls , Mrs. Luna , and Mr. Van Drew ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Expunging the December 18, 2019, impeachment of President Donald John Trump.
That the December 18, 2019, impeachment of President Donald John Trump is expunged, as if such Articles had never passed the full House of Representatives, as the facts and circumstances upon which such Articles were based did not meet the burden of proving the commission of high Crimes and Misdemeanors , as set forth in section 4 of article II of the Constitution.
