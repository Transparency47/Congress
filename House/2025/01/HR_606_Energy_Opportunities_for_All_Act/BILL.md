119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 606
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 22, 2025 Mr. Crane introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To nullify Public Land Order No. 7923, withdrawing certain land in San Juan County, New Mexico, from mineral entry.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Energy Opportunities for All Act .
#### 2. Public Land Order No. 7923 nullified
Public Land Order No. 7923 (88 Fed. Reg. 37266, relating to the withdrawal of public lands surrounding Chaco Culture National Historical Park boundary, San Juan County, New Mexico) shall have no force or effect.
