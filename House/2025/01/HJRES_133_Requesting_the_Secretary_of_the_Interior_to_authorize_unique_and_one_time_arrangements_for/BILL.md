119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. J. RES. 133
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
November 4, 2025 Mr. Aderholt (for himself, Mrs. Watson Coleman , Ms. Salazar , and Mr. Evans of Pennsylvania ) submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
JOINT RESOLUTION
Requesting the Secretary of the Interior to authorize unique and one-time arrangements for displays on the National Mall and the Washington Monument during the period beginning on December 31, 2025, and ending on January 5, 2026.
That Congress—
**(1)**
requests the Secretary of the Interior to authorize unique and one-time arrangements for the display of United States semiquincentennial materials and other significant artifacts, digital content, film footage, and associated audio and imagery in and around the vicinity of the National Mall, including projected onto the surface of the Washington Monument for 5 nights of public display, during the period beginning on December 31, 2025, and ending on January 5, 2026; and
**(2)**
respectfully requests that the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit an enrolled copy of this resolution to—
**(A)**
the Secretary of the Interior; and
**(B)**
the Chair of the United States Semiquincentennial Commission.
