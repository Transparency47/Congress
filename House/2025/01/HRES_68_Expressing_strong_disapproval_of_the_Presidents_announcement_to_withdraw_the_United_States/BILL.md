RESOLUTION
Expressing strong disapproval of the President’s announcement to withdraw the United States from the Paris Agreement.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
strongly disapproves of the President’s announcement to withdraw the United States from the Paris Agreement;
**(2)**
commends the group of States, cities, colleges and universities, businesses, investors, and individuals who have publicly expressed their support for the Paris Agreement;
**(3)**
urges the President to reverse his decision and maintain United States participation in the Paris Agreement; and
**(4)**
urges Congress to prioritize the United States global leadership on addressing climate change.
