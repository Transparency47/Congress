A BILL
To amend title 5, United States Code, to establish Lunar New Year Day as a Federal holiday, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Lunar New Year Day Act .
#### 2. Lunar New Year Day
Section 6103(a) of title 5, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to the Birthday of Martin Luther King, Jr., the following:
Lunar New Year Day. .
