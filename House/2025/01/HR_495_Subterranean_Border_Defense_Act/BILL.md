## Summary

**Subterranean Border Defense Act**

This bill requires recurring annual reporting by U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) on the implementation of a strategic plan to counter illicit cross-border tunnel operations. Currently, CBP is only required to report once no later than one year after the plan's development.

January 16, 2025 Mr. Crane (for himself, Mr. Correa , Mr. Biggs of Arizona , Mr. Gosar , Mr. Higgins of Louisiana , Mr. Luttrell , and Mr. Ogles ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Homeland Security
A BILL
To require annual reports on counter illicit cross-border tunnel operations, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Subterranean Border Defense Act .
#### 2. Annual reports on counter illicit cross-border tunnel operations
Paragraph (2) of section 7134(a) of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ; 6 U.S.C. 257 note) is amended by inserting and annually thereafter after development of the strategic plan .
