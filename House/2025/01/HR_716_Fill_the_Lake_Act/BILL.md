H. R. 716
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 23, 2025 Mr. Zinke introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To direct the Secretary of the Interior to ensure full pool levels of Flathead Lake in Montana in accordance with certain requirements, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Fill the Lake Act .
#### 2. Regulation of Pool Level of Flathead Lake
From June 15 through September 15 of each calendar year, the Secretary of the Interior shall ensure—
**(1)**
a minimum lake level in Flathead Lake of 2892’ MSL by providing the water from Hungry Horse Reservoir; and
**(2)**
a maximum lake level in Flathead Lake of 2893’ MSL by releasing excess water downstream.
