## Summary

**Ending Presidential Overreach on Public Lands Act**

This bill removes the president's authority to designate or expand national monuments and gives that authority to Congress instead.

A BILL
To reserve to Congress the authority to establish or extend a national monument.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Ending Presidential Overreach on Public Lands Act .
#### 2. National monuments
Section 320301 of title 54, United States Code (commonly referred to as the Antiquities Act ), is amended to read as follows:
320301. National monuments The establishment or extension of a national monument may be undertaken only by express authorization of Congress. .
