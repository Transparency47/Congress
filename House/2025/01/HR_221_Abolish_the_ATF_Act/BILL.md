119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 221
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 7, 2025 Mr. Burlison (for himself, Ms. Boebert , Mr. Biggs of Arizona , Mr. Collins , Mr. Onder , Mr. Ogles , Mrs. Miller of Illinois , Mr. Self , and Mr. Gosar ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To abolish the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Abolish the ATF Act .
#### 2. Abolishment of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives
The Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives is hereby abolished.
