## Summary

This bill renames a trail and a park within Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park located in Paterson, New Jersey, in honor of Congressman Bill Pascrell, Jr. Specifically, it renames the Great Falls Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge as the Bill Pascrell, Jr. Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge. It also renames Overlook Park as Bill Pascrell, Jr. Overlook Park.

119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 249
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 9, 2025 Mr. Pallone (for himself, Ms. Pou , Mr. Norcross , Mr. Van Drew , Mr. Conaway , Mr. Smith of New Jersey , Mr. Gottheimer , Mr. Kean , Mr. Menendez , Mrs. McIver , Ms. Sherrill , and Mrs. Watson Coleman ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To redesignate certain facilities at Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park in honor of Congressman Bill Pascrell, Jr.
#### 1. Redesignation of Paterson Great Falls NHP facilities
##### (a) Great Falls Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge
Great Falls Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge at Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park shall hereafter be known and designated as Bill Pascrell, Jr. Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge .
##### (b) Overlook Park
Overlook Park at Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park shall hereafter be known and designated as Bill Pascrell, Jr. Overlook Park .
##### (c) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to—
**(1)**
Great Falls Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge shall be deemed to be a reference to Bill Pascrell, Jr. Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge; and
**(2)**
Overlook Park shall be deemed to be a reference to Bill Pascrell, Jr. Overlook Park.
