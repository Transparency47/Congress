January 15, 2025 Ms. Pettersen (for herself and Ms. Boebert ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Silver Cliff, Colorado, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Silver Cliff Community Act .
#### 2. Single, unique ZIP Code for Silver Cliff, Colorado
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code, to be numbered 81249, applicable to the area encompassing only Silver Cliff, Colorado.
