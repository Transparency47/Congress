## Summary

**No Foreign Persons Administering Our Elections Act**

This bill prohibits states and local jurisdictions from hiring noncitizens to administer federal elections.

A BILL
To prohibit the hiring of individuals who are not citizens of the United States to administer an election for Federal office in a State or local jurisdiction, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No Foreign Persons Administering Our Elections Act .
#### 2. Requirements with respect to hiring of election administrators for an election for Federal office
##### (a) In general
A State or local jurisdiction may not hire an individual who is not a citizen of the United States to administer an election for Federal office in the State or local jurisdiction.
##### (b) Effective date
This section shall apply with respect to any election for Federal office held on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
