119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6439
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 4, 2025 Mr. Tony Gonzales of Texas (for himself, Mr. Guest , Mr. Kelly of Pennsylvania , Mr. Alford , Mr. Stauber , and Mr. McGuire ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To expand eligibility for special overtime pay to U.S. Border Patrol agents classified above grade GS–12.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Border Patrol Supervisors Retention Act .
#### 2. Establishment of higher rates of regularly scheduled overtime pay for certain U.S. Border Patrol agents
Section 5550(h) of title 5, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in the subsection heading, by striking GS–12 Border Patrol agents and inserting Border Patrol agents classified from grade GS–12 through GS–15 ; and
**(2)**
in paragraph (1), by striking a position at grade GS–12 and inserting a position from grade GS–12 through GS–15 .
