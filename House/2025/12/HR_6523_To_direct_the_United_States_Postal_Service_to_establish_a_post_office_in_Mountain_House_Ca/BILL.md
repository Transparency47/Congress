IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 9, 2025 Mr. Harder of California introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to establish a post office in Mountain House, California.
#### 1. Establish post office in Mountain House, California
The United States Postal Service shall establish a post office in Mountain House, California, not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act.
