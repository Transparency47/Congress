IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 3, 2025 Mr. Kennedy of Utah introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, Utah.
#### 1. Single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, Utah
Not later than 270 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Highland City, Utah.
