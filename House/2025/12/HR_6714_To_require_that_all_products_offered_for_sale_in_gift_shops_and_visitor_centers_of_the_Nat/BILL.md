A BILL
To require that all products offered for sale in gift shops and visitor centers of the National Park System are produced in the United States, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the American Products in Parks Act .
#### 2. Requiring that items sold in National Park System Gift Shops are produced in the United States
##### (a) In general
Effective on the date that is 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, a product may only be offered for sale in a gift shop or visitor center located within a unit of the National Park System if—
**(1)**
the final assembly or processing of the product occurs in the United States;
**(2)**
all significant processing that goes into the product occurs in the United States; and
**(3)**
all or virtually all ingredients or components of the product are made and sourced in the United States.
##### (b) Enforcement procedures
Not later than the effective date described in subsection (a), the Secretary of the Interior shall establish certification, audit, and enforcement procedures to ensure compliance with the requirements of this Act.
