119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6755
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 16, 2025 Ms. Mace introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to relax restrictions on filling certain vacant positions at the Department of Veterans Affairs.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Accountable Leadership for Veterans Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Senior Executive Service positions in the Department filled by noncareer appointees
Section 709(a)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking 5 percent and inserting 10 percent .
#### 3. Under Secretary for Health
Section 305 of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
305. Under Secretary for Health There is in the Department an Under Secretary for Health, who is appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate. .
#### 4. Under Secretary for Benefits
Section 306 of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
306. Under Secretary for Benefits There is in the Department an Under Secretary for Benefits, who is appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate. .
