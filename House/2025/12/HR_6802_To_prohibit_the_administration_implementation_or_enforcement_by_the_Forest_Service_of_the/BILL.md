A BILL
To prohibit the administration, implementation, or enforcement by the Forest Service of the rule relating to Law Enforcement; Criminal Prohibitions .
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Community Law Enforcement Authority Restoration Act of 2025 or as the CLEAR Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Limitation on enforcement of rule relating to Law Enforcement; Criminal Prohibitions
The Forest Service of the Department of Agriculture may not administer, implement or enforce the rule relating to Law Enforcement; Criminal Prohibitions (89 Fed. Reg. 92808 (November 25, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
