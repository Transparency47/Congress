IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 918
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 1, 2025 Mrs. Sykes submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Honoring and commemorating 200 years of historic contributions to American culture made by the city of Akron.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
honors and commemorates 200 years of historic contributions to American culture made by the city of Akron;
**(2)**
recognizes the city’s longstanding history of fighting for economic, gender, and racial justice; and
**(3)**
encourages Akron to continue standing up to injustices and supporting workers’ rights for another 200 years.
