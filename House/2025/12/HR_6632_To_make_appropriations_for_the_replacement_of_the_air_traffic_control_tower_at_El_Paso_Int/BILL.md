This bill provides appropriations for the replacement of the El Paso Air Traffic Control Tower at the El Paso International Airport in El Paso, TX.

119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6632
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 11, 2025 Ms. Escobar introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Appropriations
A BILL
To make appropriations for the replacement of the air traffic control tower at El Paso International Airport in El Paso, Texas.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the El Paso Air Traffic Control Tower Modernization Act .
#### 2. Appropriation for air traffic control tower
There are hereby appropriated, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sums as may be necessary for the replacement of the El Paso Air Traffic Control Tower at the El Paso International Airport in El Paso, Texas.
