119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 309
[Report No. 119–52]
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 9, 2025 Mr. Steil , from the Committee on House Administration , reported the following resolution; which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be printed
RESOLUTION
Dismissing the election contest relating to the office of Representative from the Twenty-eighth Congressional District of Texas.
That the election contest relating to the office of Representative from the Twenty-eighth Congressional District of Texas is dismissed on the grounds of untimely filing with the House of Representatives.
