IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 960
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 18, 2025 Mr. Boyle of Pennsylvania (for himself and Mr. Lawler ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of November 2025 as National Lung Cancer Awareness Month and expressing support for early detection and treatment of lung cancer.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of National Lung Cancer Awareness Month ;
**(2)**
supports the designation of National Women's Lung Cancer Awareness Week ;
**(3)**
supports the designation of National Lung Cancer Screening Day ;
**(4)**
supports the purposes and ideals of National Lung Cancer Awareness Month;
**(5)**
promotes efforts to increase awareness of, education about, and research on—
**(A)**
mitigation of risk factors for lung cancer;
**(B)**
lung cancer screening;
**(C)**
treatment of lung cancer; and
**(D)**
lung cancer affecting minorities and individuals who have never smoked; and
**(6)**
encourages the people of the United States to observe National Lung Cancer Awareness Month with appropriate awareness and educational activities.
