and amplify antisemitism, mitigation efficacy, and significant model or policy changes affecting safety outcomes;\n**(8)**\nreaffirms that measures to address antisemitism on AI platforms must be consistent with the Constitution, protect civil liberties, due process, and privacy, and avoid discriminatory or overbroad enforcement; and\n**(9)**\ncalls upon all stakeholders to ensure the safety
