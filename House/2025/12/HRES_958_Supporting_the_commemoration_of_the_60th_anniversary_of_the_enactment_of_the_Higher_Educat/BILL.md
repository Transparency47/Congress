119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 958
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 17, 2025 Ms. Wilson of Florida (for herself, Ms. Adams , and Ms. Craig ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Supporting the commemoration of the 60th anniversary of the enactment of the Higher Education Act of 1965 and reaffirming the commitment of the House of Representatives to expanding access to higher education for all Americans.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the commemoration of the 60th anniversary of the enactment of the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1001 et seq. );
**(2)**
acknowledges those whose support and efforts led to passage and continued strengthening of the Act;
**(3)**
recognizes the significant investments the Act has made in the lives of American students, families, educators, and the Nation as a whole; and
**(4)**
reaffirms its commitment to ensuring all Americans, regardless of background or circumstance, can pursue and complete higher education for the benefit of the Nation’s democracy and economy.
