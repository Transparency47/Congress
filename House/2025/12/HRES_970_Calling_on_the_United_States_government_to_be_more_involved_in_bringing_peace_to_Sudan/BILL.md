IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 970
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 19, 2025 Mr. Green of Texas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Calling on the United States government to be more involved in bringing peace to Sudan.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
calls on all branches of government to hold the constituent states of the Quad accountable for their own commitments to ending external military support to Sudan, namely the UAE.
