119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 964
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 18, 2025 Ms. McClellan (for herself, Mr. Bacon , and Mr. Beyer ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the bicentennial of the historic DACOR Bacon House.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the historic importance of the DACOR Bacon House to the history of American diplomacy, the Supreme Court, and Congress;
**(2)**
applauds the Diplomatic and Consular Officers, Retired (DACOR) organization and DACOR Bacon House Foundation for their decades-long efforts to preserve this unique gem located in the heart of the Nation’s Capital;
**(3)**
congratulates all those who have found meaning and community in the DACOR Bacon House on its bicentennial; and
**(4)**
sincerely wishes to see the DACOR Bacon House continue to flourish over the next 200 years.
