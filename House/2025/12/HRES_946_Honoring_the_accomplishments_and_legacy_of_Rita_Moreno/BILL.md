119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 946
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 11, 2025 Mr. Hernández (for himself, Mr. Torres of New York , Ms. Ocasio-Cortez , Mr. Espaillat , Mr. Soto , Ms. Pou , and Ms. Velázquez ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Honoring the accomplishments and legacy of Rita Moreno.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the accomplishments and enduring legacy of Rita Moreno, a pioneering Latina artist, proud Puerto Rican, and cultural icon of the United States;
**(2)**
commends Rita Moreno for her lifelong contributions to the arts, civil rights, and Latino representation in American media; and
**(3)**
calls upon the people of the United States to celebrate and honor the life and work of Rita Moreno, whose remarkable journey continues to inspire and enrich the Nation.
