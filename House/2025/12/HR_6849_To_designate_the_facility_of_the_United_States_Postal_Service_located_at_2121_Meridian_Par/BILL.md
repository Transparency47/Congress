A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2121 Meridian Park Boulevard in Concord, California, as the Carl Jefferson Post Office Building .
#### 1. Carl Jefferson Post Office Building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2121 Meridian Park Boulevard in Concord, California, shall be known and designated as the Carl Jefferson Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Carl Jefferson Post Office Building .
