A BILL
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to adjust the eligibility for the rural emergency hospital designation under the Medicare program.
#### 1. Adjusting eligibility for rural emergency hospital designation under Medicare
Section 1861(kkk)(3) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395x(kkk)(3) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking as of and inserting , at any point during the period beginning on January 1, 2015, and ending on ;
**(2)**
in subparagraph (A), by striking or at the end;
**(3)**
in subparagraph (B), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; or ; and
**(4)**
by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(C) was an off-campus outpatient department of a hospital (as defined in section 1833(t)(21)(B)) that— (i) was a dedicated emergency department (as defined in section 489.24(b) of title 42, Code of Federal Regulations (or any successor regulation)); and (ii) was located in a county (or equivalent unit of local government) in a rural area (as defined in section 1886(d)(2)(D)). .
