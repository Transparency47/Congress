## Summary

**Northern Mariana Islands Medicaid Advancement Act**

This bill requires the Medicaid funding cap for the Northern Mariana Islands to be the same as that for American Samoa in FY2026.

A BILL
To amend title XI of the Social Security Act to increase the payment limit under Medicaid for the Northern Mariana Islands.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Northern Mariana Islands Medicaid Advancement Act .
#### 2. Increasing payment limit under Medicaid for the Northern Mariana Islands
Section 1108(g)(2)(D) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1308(g)(2)(D) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in clause (i), by striking clause (ii) and inserting clauses (ii) through (iv) ;
**(2)**
in clause (ii), by striking and at the end; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following new clause:
(iv) for fiscal year 2026, the amount determined under subparagraph (E) with respect to American Samoa for such fiscal year; and .
