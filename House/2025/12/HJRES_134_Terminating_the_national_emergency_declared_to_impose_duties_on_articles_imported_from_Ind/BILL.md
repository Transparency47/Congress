H. J. RES. 134
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 12, 2025 Ms. Ross (for herself, Mr. Veasey , and Mr. Krishnamoorthi ) submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Terminating the national emergency declared to impose duties on articles imported from India.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act ( 50 U.S.C. 1622 ), the national emergency declared on August 6, 2025, by the President in Executive Order 14329 (90 Fed. Reg. 38701) is terminated.
