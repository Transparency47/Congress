IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 5, 2025 Mr. Moulton introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To allow victims to sue Federal immigration enforcement officers for constitutional violations.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the National Oversight and Enforcement of Misconduct Act or the NOEM Act .
#### 2. Civil action against person acting under federal immigration enforcement authority
##### (a)
Section 1979 of the Revised Statutes ( 42 U.S.C. 1983 ) is amended—
**(1)**
in the first sentence, by inserting of any Federal immigration enforcement authority or before of any State .
