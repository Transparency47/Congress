119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 6413
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 3, 2025 Mr. Taylor introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
A BILL
To amend the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act to remove a requirement that 75 percent of certain funds be used to provide youth workforce investment activities for out-of-school youth.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Workforce Flexibility Act .
#### 2. Removal of out-of-school priority
Section 129(a) of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act ( 29 U.S.C. 3164 ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking paragraph (4); and
**(2)**
by redesignating paragraph (5) as paragraph (4).
