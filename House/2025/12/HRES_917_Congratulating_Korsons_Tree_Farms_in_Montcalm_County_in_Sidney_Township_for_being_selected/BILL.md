IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 917
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 1, 2025 Mr. Moolenaar (for himself, Mr. James , Mr. Bergman , Ms. Scholten , Mr. Huizenga , Mr. Walberg , Mrs. Dingell , Mr. Barrett , Ms. McDonald Rivet , Mrs. McClain , Ms. Stevens , Ms. Tlaib , and Mr. Thanedar ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Congratulating Korson’s Tree Farms in Montcalm County in Sidney Township for being selected to provide the 2025 White House Christmas Tree.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
congratulates Korson’s Tree Farms in Montcalm County in Sidney Township as being selected to provide the 2025 White House Christmas tree;
**(2)**
recognizes the agricultural and economic significance of the Christmas tree industry to the State of Michigan and the Nation;
**(3)**
invites Korson’s Tree Farms to the United States Capitol Building to be honored; and
**(4)**
directs the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make available enrolled copies of this resolution to President Donald J. Trump and First Lady Melania Trump for appropriate display.
