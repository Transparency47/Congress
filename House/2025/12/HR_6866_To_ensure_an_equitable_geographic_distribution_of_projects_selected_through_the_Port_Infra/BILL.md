IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 18, 2025 Mr. Joyce of Ohio (for himself, Mr. Kennedy of New York , Mr. Huizenga , Mr. Mrvan , Mr. Weber of Texas , Ms. Kaptur , Ms. Tenney , Mrs. Dingell , Mrs. Houchin , and Ms. Kelly of Illinois ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure
A BILL
To ensure an equitable geographic distribution of projects selected through the Port Infrastructure Development Program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Securing Smart Investments in our Ports Act .
#### 2. Port Infrastructure Development Program
##### (a) Port and intermodal improvement program
Section 54301(a)(6)(B) of title 46, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in clause (ii), by striking and after the semicolon;
**(2)**
in clause (iii), by striking the period and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following:
(iv) ensuring equitable geographic distribution among regions of the United States with regard to the projects selected. .
##### (b) Assistance for small inland river and coastal ports and terminals
Section 54301(b)(4) of title 46, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in subparagraph (B), by striking ; and ;
**(2)**
in subparagraph (C), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following:
(D) ensure equitable geographic distribution among regions of the United States with regard to the projects selected. .
