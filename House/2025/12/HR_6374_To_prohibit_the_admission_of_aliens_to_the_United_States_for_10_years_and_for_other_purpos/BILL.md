IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 3, 2025 Mr. Gosar (for himself and Mr. Crane ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To prohibit the admission of aliens to the United States for 10 years, and for other purposes.
#### 1. 10-year ban on admission of aliens to the United States
Beginning on the date of enactment of this Act, and ending on the date that is 10 years after such date of enactment, no alien may be admitted to the United States.
