119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 931
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 4, 2025 Mr. Shreve (for himself, Mr. Moylan , Mr. Mackenzie , Ms. Salazar , Ms. Fedorchak , Mr. Schmidt , Mr. Rulli , and Mr. McDowell ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform

Expressing support for the designation of December 4, 2025, as National Scam Prevention Day .
That the House of Representatives expresses support for the designation of National Scam Prevention Day .
