December 16, 2025 Ms. Hageman introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To provide a sunset for section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Sunset To Reform Section 230 Act .
#### 2. Sunset
Section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934 ( 47 U.S.C. 230 ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g) Sunset This section shall have no force or effect after December 31, 2026. .
