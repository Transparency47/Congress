Findings\nCongress finds the following:\n**(1)**\nAccording to the National Coalition Against Domestic Violence and the National Domestic Violence Hotline, between 10,000,000 to 12,000,000 women and men become victims of domestic violence each year.\n**(2)**\nA person can experience domestic violence though either intimate partner violence, such as with a current or former dating partner, or non-intimate partner violence, such as those with whom the person has a familial relationship.\n**(3)**\nIntimate partner violence has affected both women and men across the United States.
