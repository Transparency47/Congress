IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 941
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 10, 2025 Mrs. Foushee (for herself and Mr. Haridopolos ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology
RESOLUTION
Recognizing May 20 as National Women in Aerospace Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes the celebration of National Women in Aerospace Day as a time to reflect on the many notable contributions that women have made to the field of aerospace in the United States;
**(2)**
urges the people of the United States to observe National Women in Aerospace Day with appropriate programs and activities; and
**(3)**
affirms the commitment of the House of Representatives to ensuring that all women have equal access to opportunity in the aerospace field.
