IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 385
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 5, 2025 Mr. Vindman (for himself and Mr. Cline ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of May 2, 2025, as NKH Awareness Day .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
supports the designation of NKH Awareness Day ; and
**(2)**
recognizes the importance of raising awareness and improving education around nonketotic hyperglycinemia (NKH).
