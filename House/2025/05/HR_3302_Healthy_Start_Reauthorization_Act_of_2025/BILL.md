1st Session

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 8, 2025 Ms. Ocasio-Cortez (for herself and Ms. Malliotakis ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize the Healthy Start Initiative.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Healthy Start Reauthorization Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Reauthorization of Healthy Start Initiative
Section 330H(e)(1) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 254c–8(e)(1) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking appropriated and inserting the following:
appropriated— (A) ;
**(2)**
in subparagraph (A), as so inserted, by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and ; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following:
(B) $145,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030. .
