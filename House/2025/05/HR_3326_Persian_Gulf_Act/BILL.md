May 13, 2025 Ms. Ansari introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To prohibit the use of Federal funds to rename the Persian Gulf, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Persian Gulf Act .
#### 2. Prohibition on use of Federal funds to rename Persian Gulf
##### (a) In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds are authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available to rename the Persian Gulf.
##### (b) References
No law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States may refer to the Persian Gulf by a different name without express authorization of Congress.
