## Summary

**Facility for Runway Operations and Safe Transportation Act or the FROST Act**

This bill allows airport sponsors to use funds from Airport Improvement Program (AIP) grants for storage facilities for aircraft deicing equipment and fluids.

Under current law, airports may use AIP funds for structures and equipment that are used to deice aircraft, but not for the associated storage facilities.

A BILL
To amend title 49, United States Code, to include aircraft deicing storage facilities in the definition of airport development, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Facility for Runway Operations and Safe Transportation Act or the FROST Act .
#### 2. Deicing equipment protection
Section 47102 of title 49, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (3)(B)(v) by striking and storage facilities for the equipment and fluids ; and
**(2)**
in paragraph (24) by inserting storage facilities for aircraft deicing equipment and fluids, after sponsor, .
