## Summary

This resolution supports the designation of Tardive Dyskinesia Awareness Week. Tardive dyskinesia is a movement disorder characterized by involuntary and uncontrolled movements of muscles in the face, torso, and extremities.

IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 396
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 7, 2025 Mr. Peters (for himself, Mr. Bilirakis , Mr. Mullin , and Mr. Bean of Florida ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of the week of May 4, 2025, through May 10, 2025, as Tardive Dyskinesia Awareness Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of Tardive Dyskinesia Awareness Week ; and
**(2)**
encourages each individual in the United States to become better informed about and aware of tardive dyskinesia.
