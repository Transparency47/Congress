May 21, 2025 Mr. Amo (for himself and Mr. Olszewski ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To prohibit the Secretary of State from holding or otherwise carrying out the duties of any other Federal position.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Reaffirming Unified Boundaries for Integrity and Oversight Act or the RUBIO Act .
#### 2. Prohibition on Secretary of State holding or otherwise carrying out duties of other Federal positions
The Secretary of State may not hold or otherwise carry out the duties of any other Federal position, and no Federal funds may be obligated or expended for the salaries or expenses of the Secretary if the Secretary so holds or otherwise carries out duties of such other positions.
