A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 340 East 1st Street in Tustin, California, as the Ursula Ellen Kennedy Post Office Building .
#### 1. Ursula ellen kennedy post office building
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 340 East 1st Street in Tustin, California, shall be known and designated as the Ursula Ellen Kennedy Post Office Building .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Ursula Ellen Kennedy Post Office Building .
