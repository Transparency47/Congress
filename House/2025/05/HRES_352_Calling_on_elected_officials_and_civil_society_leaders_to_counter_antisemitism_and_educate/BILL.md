119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 352
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 24, 2025 Ms. Wasserman Schultz (for herself, Mrs. Miller-Meeks , Mr. Carter of Louisiana , and Mr. Fitzpatrick ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Calling on elected officials and civil society leaders to counter antisemitism and educate the public on the contributions of the Jewish American community.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
calls on elected officials, faith leaders, and civil society leaders to condemn and counter all acts of antisemitism;
**(2)**
calls on the executive branch and State and local leaders to educate the public on the contributions of the Jewish-American community and uplift Jewish stories and voices; and
**(3)**
takes all possible steps to ensure the safety, security, and dignity of American Jews in all aspects of their lives, including the workplace, college and university campuses, synagogues, and at home.
