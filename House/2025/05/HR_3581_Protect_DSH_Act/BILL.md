May 23, 2025 Mr. Crenshaw introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to delay certain disproportionate share hospital payment reductions under the Medicaid program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protect DSH Act .
#### 2. Delaying DSH Reductions
##### (a) In general
