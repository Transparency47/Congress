IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 428
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 19, 2025 Mr. Messmer submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of May 2025 as Moving Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
thanks the moving and storage industry of the United States; and
**(2)**
promotes the professional services of moving and storage companies by encouraging the public to recognize Moving Month .
