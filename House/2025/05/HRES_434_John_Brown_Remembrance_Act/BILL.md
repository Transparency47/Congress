IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 434
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 20, 2025 Mrs. Sykes submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
John Brown Remembrance Act.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
honors and commemorates the 225th anniversary of John Brown's birth; and
**(2)**
recognizes the generational impact that John Brown had on the abolitionist movement and his contributions to ending slavery in the United States.
