A BILL
To amend section 235 of the Immigration and Nationality Act to provide for an exception from expedited removal for certain countries concern.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Artemis Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Exception from expedited removal for certain countries of particular concern or special watch list countries
Section 235(b)(1) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ( 8 U.S.C. 1225(b)(1) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by striking subparagraph (F) each place it appears and inserting subparagraph (F) or (H) ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following:
(H) Exception for country of concern Subparagraph (A) shall not apply to an alien who is a native or citizen of a country of concern or would be removed to such a country. For the purposes of this subparagraph, the term country of concern means a country designated by the Secretary of State to be a country of particular concern or is a special watch list country under section 402(b)(1)(A) International Religious Freedom Act of 1998. .
