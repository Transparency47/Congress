119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3245
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 7, 2025 Mrs. Luna introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary , and in addition to the Committees on Intelligence (Permanent Select) , Financial Services , Foreign Affairs , Energy and Commerce , Education and Workforce , Transportation and Infrastructure , and Armed Services , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To repeal the USA PATRIOT Act.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the American Privacy Restoration Act .
#### 2. Repeal of USA PATRIOT Act
The USA PATRIOT Act ( Public Law 107–56 ; 115 Stat. 272) is repealed, and each provision of law amended by such Act is amended to read as such provision read on October 25, 2001.
