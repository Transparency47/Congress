May 7, 2025 Mrs. Sykes (for herself and Mr. Joyce of Pennsylvania ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize mental and behavioral health education and training grants.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Mental Health Improvement Act .
#### 2. Mental and behavioral health education and training grants
Section 756(f) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 294e–1(f) ) is amended by striking fiscal years 2023 through 2027 and inserting fiscal years 2026 through 2030 .
