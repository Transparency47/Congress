## Summary

**Veteran Jobs Training Act**

This bill increases by $15 million funding for homeless veterans reintegration programs for FY2024 and each fiscal year thereafter. Such programs, administered by the Department of Labor, provide job training, counseling, and job placement services for certain veterans, including veterans who are homeless or transitioning from being incarcerated.

May 21, 2025 Mr. Neguse introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to increase the authorization of appropriations for homeless veterans reintegration programs.
#### 1. Short Title
This Act may be cited as the Veteran Jobs Training Act .
#### 2. Increase of authorization of appropriations for homeless veterans reintegration programs
Section 2021(i)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
in subparagraph (G), by striking fiscal year 2024 and each fiscal year thereafter and inserting each of fiscal years 2024 and 2025 ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(H) $75,000,000 for fiscal year 2024 and each fiscal year thereafter. .
