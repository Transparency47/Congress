IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 14, 2025 Mr. Turner of Ohio (for himself, Mr. Carey , Ms. Leger Fernandez , and Ms. Elfreth ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources
A BILL
To amend title 54, United States Code, to extend funding for the Historic Preservation Fund.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Historic Preservation Fund Reauthorization Act .
#### 2. Extension of Historic Preservation Fund
Section 303102 of title 54, United States Code, is amended by striking 2023, $150,000,000 and inserting 2035, $250,000,000 .
