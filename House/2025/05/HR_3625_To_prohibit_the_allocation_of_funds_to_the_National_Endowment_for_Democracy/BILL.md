May 29, 2025 Mr. Crane introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To prohibit the allocation of funds to the National Endowment for Democracy.
#### 1. Prohibition on the allocation of funds to the National Endowment for Democracy
##### (a) Prohibition
The head of an agency may not allocate any funds to the National Endowment for Democracy.
##### (b) Agency defined
In this section the term agency has the meaning given that term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
