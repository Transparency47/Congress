119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 3393
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 14, 2025 Mr. Hamadeh of Arizona (for himself, Mr. Ciscomani , Mr. Stanton , Mr. Biggs of Arizona , Ms. Ansari , Mr. Gosar , Mr. Crane , and Mr. Schweikert ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12208 North 19th Avenue in Phoenix, Arizona, as the Officer Zane T. Coolidge Post Office .
#### 1. Officer Zane T. Coolidge Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 12208 North 19th Avenue in Phoenix, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Officer Zane T. Coolidge Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Officer Zane T. Coolidge Post Office .
