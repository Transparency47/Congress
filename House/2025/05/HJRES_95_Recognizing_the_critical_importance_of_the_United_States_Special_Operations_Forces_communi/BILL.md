IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 6, 2025 Mr. Jackson of Texas (for himself, Mr. Hudson , Mr. Peters , Ms. Castor of Florida , and Mr. Crow ) submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
JOINT RESOLUTION
Recognizing the critical importance of the United States Special Operations Forces community and expressing support for the designation of SOF Week.
That Congress—
**(1)**
recognizes the critical importance of the United States Special Operations Forces community to the defense and security of the United States;
**(2)**
commits to honoring the skill, dedication, bravery, and selfless service of SOF personnel and their families; and
**(3)**
supports the designation of SOF Week in appreciation of the courage, commitment, and contributions of the SOF community.
