119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 813
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 17, 2025 Ms. DeLauro (for herself, Mr. Rulli , Mr. Panetta , Mr. Garbarino , Mr. Amodei of Nevada , Mr. Suozzi , Ms. Salazar , Mr. Garamendi , Mr. Thompson of California , and Ms. Pou ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Urging the people of the United States to observe the month of October 2025 as Italian and Italian American Heritage Month.
That in order to recognize the enormous contributions Italian and Italian American people have made to this country and the world throughout our history, the House of Representatives urges the people of the United States to acknowledge Italian and Italian American Heritage Month and to observe the month with appropriate events and activities.
