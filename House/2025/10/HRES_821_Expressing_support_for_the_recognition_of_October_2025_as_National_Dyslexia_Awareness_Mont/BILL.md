IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 821
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 17, 2025 Mr. Westerman (for himself, Mr. Bean of Florida , Ms. DelBene , Ms. Brownley , Mrs. Houchin , and Mr. Lawler ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the recognition of October 2025 as National Dyslexia Awareness Month .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
calls on Congress, schools, and State and local educational agencies to recognize that dyslexia has significant educational implications that must be addressed; and
**(2)**
supports the designation of National Dyslexia Awareness Month .
