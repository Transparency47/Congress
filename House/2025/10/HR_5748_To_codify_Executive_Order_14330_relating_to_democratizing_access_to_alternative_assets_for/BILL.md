119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5748
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 14, 2025 Mr. Downing (for himself, Mr. Donalds , Mr. Davidson , Mr. Stutzman , Mr. Carter of Georgia , and Mr. Moore of Alabama ) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce , and in addition to the Committee on Financial Services , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
A BILL
To codify Executive Order 14330 (relating to democratizing access to alternative assets for 401(k) investors).
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Retirement Investment Choice Act .
#### 2. Codification of executive order 14330
Executive Order 14330 (90 Fed. Reg. 38921; relating to democratizing access to alternative assets for 401(k) investors) shall have the force and effect of law.
