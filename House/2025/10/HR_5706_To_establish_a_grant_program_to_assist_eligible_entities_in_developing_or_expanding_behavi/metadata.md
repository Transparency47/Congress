Grant program to expand behavioral health crisis response\n##### (a) Establishment\nThe Secretary of Health and Human Services, acting through the Assistant Secretary for Mental Health and Substance Use, shall establish a competitive grant program (in this section referred to as the program ) to assist eligible entities in developing or expanding behavioral health crisis response programs that do not rely primarily on law enforcement.\n##### (b) Eligible entities\nThe following entities shall be eligible to receive grants under the program\u2014\n**(1)**\na local or Tribal government;\n**(2)**\na regional emergency medical services agency or fire department;\n**(3)**\na certified community behavioral health clinic, as defined by section 1905(jj) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1396d(jj) );
