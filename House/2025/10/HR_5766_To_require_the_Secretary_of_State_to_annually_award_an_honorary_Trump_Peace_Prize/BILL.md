119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. R. 5766
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 17, 2025 Mr. Barr introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
A BILL
To require the Secretary of State to annually award an honorary Trump Peace Prize.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the President Donald J. Trump Peace Prize Act .
#### 2. Trump Peace Prize
##### (a) In general
The Secretary of State shall annually confer an honorary award, to be known as the Trump Peace Prize , on an individual who is a peacemaker.
##### (b) First awardee
The Secretary shall confer the first Trump Peace Prize on President Donald Trump.
