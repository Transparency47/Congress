IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 810
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 14, 2025 Mr. Whitesides submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology
RESOLUTION
Honoring José Moreno Hernández, who served as a NASA astronaut and continues to be an inspirational voice on the importance of perseverance and a defining example of Hispanic American contributions in STEM.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes José Moreno Hernández for his incredible career of service, his significant contributions to space exploration and scientific advancement, his advocacy for diverse representation in STEM, and his enduring story of perseverance.
