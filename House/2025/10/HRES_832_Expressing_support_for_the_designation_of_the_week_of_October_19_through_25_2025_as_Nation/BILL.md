IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 832
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 24, 2025 Mr. Moolenaar (for himself, Mr. Bishop , Mr. Carson , and Mr. Carter of Georgia ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of the week of October 19 through 25, 2025, as National Chemistry Week .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for the designation of National Chemistry Week ;
**(2)**
supports the goals and welcomes the participants of the 36th annual National Chemistry Week;
**(3)**
recognizes the need to promote the fields of science, including chemistry, technology, engineering, and mathematics, and to encourage youth, including from underrepresented groups, to pursue careers in these fields; and
**(4)**
commends the American Chemical Society and the partners of that society for seeking opportunities to engage with the public and for organizing and convening events and activities surrounding National Chemistry Week each year.
