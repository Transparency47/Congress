119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 805
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 14, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia (for himself and Mrs. Harshbarger ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Recognizing October 2025 as American Pharmacists Month in honor of the contribution of pharmacists to provide safe, accessible, affordable, and beneficial patient care services and products to all residents and protect the public health of our communities.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes “American Pharmacists Month” in honor of the contribution of pharmacists to provide safe, accessible, affordable, and beneficial patient care services and products to all residents and protect the public health of our communities;
**(2)**
pledges to celebrate contributions of pharmacists to the health and well-being of all people in each State;
**(3)**
recognizes pharmacists as one of the most accessible health care providers delivering critical services to patients and contributing to the health and well-being of all people; and
**(4)**
commits to working collaboratively with pharmacists and other health care providers to help patients overcome illness and live healthier lives through chronic disease management and the full, effective use of their medicines.
