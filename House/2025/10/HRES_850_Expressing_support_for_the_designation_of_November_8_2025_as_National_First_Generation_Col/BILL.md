IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 850
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 31, 2025 Ms. Moore of Wisconsin (for herself and Mr. Simpson ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Education and Workforce
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of November 8, 2025, as National First-Generation College Celebration Day .
That the House of Representatives urges all people in the United States to—
**(1)**
celebrate National First-Generation College Celebration Day throughout the United States;
**(2)**
recognize the important role that first-generation college students play in helping to develop the future workforce; and
**(3)**
celebrate the Higher Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 1001 et seq. ) and its programs that help historically excluded students access higher education.
