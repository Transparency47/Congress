IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 839
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 28, 2025 Mr. Gottheimer (for himself and Mr. Crawford ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs
RESOLUTION
Condemning Hamas for assassinating innocent Palestinians.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
condemns in the strongest terms the killings and acts of terror committed by Hamas against innocent Palestinians in Gaza; and
**(2)**
reaffirms the commitment of the United States to supporting the implementation of the ceasefire agreement and to advancing stability and peace for innocent civilians in Gaza.
