119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 788
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 6, 2025 Mr. Costa (for himself, Mr. Larson of Connecticut , Mr. Valadao , Mr. Tonko , Mr. Fitzpatrick , and Mr. Bilirakis ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of October 8, 2025, as National Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Day .
That the House of Representatives expresses support for the designation of National Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Day .
