A BILL
To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to reauthorize sewer overflow and stormwater reuse municipal grants.
#### 2. Sewer overflow and stormwater reuse municipal grants
Section 221 of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1301 ) is amended in subsection (f) by striking paragraph (1) and inserting—
(1) In General There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $350,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2031. .
