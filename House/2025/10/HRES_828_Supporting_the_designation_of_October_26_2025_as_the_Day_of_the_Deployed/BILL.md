IV
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 828
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 24, 2025 Mr. Bell (for himself and Mr. Bacon ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of October 26, 2025, as the Day of the Deployed .
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
expresses support for a Day of the Deployed ;
**(2)**
honors the deployed members of the Armed Forces of the United States and their families;
**(3)**
calls on the people of the United States to reflect on the service of those members of the Armed Forces, wherever the members serve, past, present, and future; and
**(4)**
encourages the people of the United States to observe the Day of the Deployed with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
