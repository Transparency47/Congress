October 28, 2025 Mr. Balderson introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce
A BILL
To amend title XXVII of the Public Health Service Act to include certain supplemental coverage provided to individual health insurance coverage as an excepted benefit.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Supplemental Benefits for Individuals Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Including certain supplemental coverage provided to individual health insurance coverage as an excepted benefit
Section 2791(c)(4) of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 300gg–91(c)(4) ) is amended by inserting or individual health insurance coverage before the period at the end.
