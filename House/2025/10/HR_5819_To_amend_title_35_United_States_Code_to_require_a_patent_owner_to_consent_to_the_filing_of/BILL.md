A BILL
To amend title 35, United States Code, to require a patent owner to consent to the filing of a petition for inter-partes review or post-grant review, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Balancing Incentives Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Requirement for patent owner to consent to the filing of petition for inter-partes review or post-grant review
Title 35, United States Code, is amended—
