119th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 814
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
October 17, 2025 Mr. Jackson of Texas submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government Reform , and in addition to the Committee on Armed Services , for a period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the committee concerned
RESOLUTION
Recognizing and honoring the White House Medical Unit for its service to the Commander in Chief.
That the House of Representatives—
**(1)**
recognizes and honors the White House Medical Unit for its exemplary service, professionalism, and unwavering commitment to the health and safety of the President, Vice President, their families, and White House staff;
**(2)**
extends its sincere gratitude to the military medical personnel who have served or are currently serving within the White House Medical Unit and recognize their vital contributions to the security and continuity of the United States Government;
**(3)**
commends the leadership of the White House Medical Unit for maintaining operational excellence in one of the most demanding and high-visibility assignments in Federal service; and
**(4)**
celebrates the 80th anniversary of the White House Medical Unit and honors its eight decades of dedicated service to the United States.
