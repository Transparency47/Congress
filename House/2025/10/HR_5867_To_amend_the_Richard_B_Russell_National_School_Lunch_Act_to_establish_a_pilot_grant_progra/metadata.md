(C) Report by Secretary With respect to each school year during which the Secretary receives the report required under subparagraph (B), the Secretary shall, not later than 1 year after the date that is the last day of such school year, submit to Congress a report that includes\u2014 (i) a summary of the reports received during such school year; and (ii) such information with respect to the pilot program as the Secretary determines to be relevant.
