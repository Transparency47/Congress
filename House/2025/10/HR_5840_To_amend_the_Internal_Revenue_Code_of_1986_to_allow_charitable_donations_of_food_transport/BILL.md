A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to allow charitable donations of food transportation vehicles and food storage equipment to receive the same tax treatment as charitable donations of food inventory in the case of donations to nonprofit organizations which provide food to communities in need.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Feed the Community Act .

(II) Food storage equipment The term food storage equipment means— (aa) an industrial or commercial grade refrigerator or freezer, (bb) industrial racking, palette racks, or other commercial shelving used by the donee for dry or temperature-controlled food storage, or (cc) inventory property or materials that aid in the receipt or storage of perishable foods, including freezer doors, insulated panels and other similar materials and equipment.
