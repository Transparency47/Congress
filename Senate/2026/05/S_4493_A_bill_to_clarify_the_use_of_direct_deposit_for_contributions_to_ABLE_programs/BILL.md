A BILL
To clarify the use of direct deposit for contributions to ABLE programs.
#### 1. Use of direct deposit for contributions to ABLE programs
No provision of law shall be construed to prevent the use of direct deposit for making contributions to qualified ABLE programs (as defined in section 529A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986).
