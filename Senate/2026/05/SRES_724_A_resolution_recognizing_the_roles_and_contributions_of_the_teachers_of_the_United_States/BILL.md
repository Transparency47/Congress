III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 724
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 11, 2026 Ms. Collins (for herself, Mr. Blumenthal , Mr. Van Hollen , Mrs. Capito , Mr. Cramer , Mr. Hagerty , Mr. Lankford , Mr. Booker , Ms. Cantwell , Mr. Durbin , Ms. Hassan , Mr. Hickenlooper , Ms. Hirono , Mr. Kaine , Mr. King , Ms. Klobuchar , Mr. Luján , Mr. Merkley , Mr. Padilla , Mr. Reed , Ms. Rosen , Mrs. Shaheen , and Mr. Welch ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the roles and contributions of the teachers of the United States in building and enhancing the civic, cultural, and economic well-being of the United States.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
thanks the teachers of the United States; and
**(2)**
promotes the profession of teaching and the contributions of educators by encouraging students, parents, school administrators, and public officials to recognize National Teacher Appreciation Week.
