119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 734
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 14, 2026 Mr. Wyden (for himself, Mrs. Hyde-Smith , Mr. Heinrich , Mrs. Capito , Mr. Justice , Mr. Daines , and Mr. Coons ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating May 16, 2026, as Kids to Parks Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates May 16, 2026, as Kids to Parks Day ;
**(2)**
recognizes the importance of outdoor recreation and the preservation of open spaces in promoting the health and education of the young people of the United States; and
**(3)**
encourages the people of the United States to observe Kids to Parks Day with safe family trips to parks.
