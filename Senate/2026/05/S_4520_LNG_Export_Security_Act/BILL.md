May 13, 2026 Mr. Cornyn (for himself and Mr. Fetterman ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources
A BILL
To amend the Natural Gas Act to include a definition of the term public interest , and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the LNG Export Security Act .
#### 2. Definition of the term public interest under the Natural Gas Act
Section 2 of the Natural Gas Act ( 15 U.S.C. 717a ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(12) Public interest means the consideration of— (A) the development of— (i) natural gas facilities in the United States; and (ii) the domestic natural gas supply; (B) domestic economic interests; and (C) national security interests. .
