119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 719
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 30, 2026 Mr. Risch (for himself, Mr. King , Ms. Lummis , Mr. Ricketts , Mr. Crapo , and Ms. Smith ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Celebrating the 154th anniversary of Arbor Day.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes April 24, 2026, as Arbor Day ;
**(2)**
celebrates the 154th anniversary of Arbor Day;
**(3)**
supports the goals and ideals of Arbor Day; and
**(4)**
encourages the people of the United States to participate in Arbor Day activities.
