A BILL
To reauthorize the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program Reauthorization Act of 2026 .
#### 2. 7-year extension of Terrorism Risk Insurance Program
##### (a) Termination date
Section 108(a) of the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 ( 15 U.S.C. 6701 note) is amended by striking 2027 and inserting 2034 .
