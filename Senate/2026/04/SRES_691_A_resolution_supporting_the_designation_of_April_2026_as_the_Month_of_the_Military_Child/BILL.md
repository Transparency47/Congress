III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 691
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 28, 2026 Mrs. Murray (for herself and Mr. Boozman ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of April 2026 as the Month of the Military Child .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the designation of April 2026 as the Month of the Military Child ; and
**(2)**
urges the people of the United States to observe the Month of the Military Child with appropriate ceremonies and activities that honor, support, and show appreciation for military children.
