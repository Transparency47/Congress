IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 16 (legislative day, April 14), 2026 Mr. Lee introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To amend the Pension Funding Equity Act of 2004 to repeal the antitrust exemption applicable to graduate medical resident matching programs.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring Rights of Medical Residents Act .
#### 2. Repealer
Section 207 of the Pension Funding Equity Act of 2004 ( 15 U.S.C. 37B ) is repealed.
#### 3. Effective date
This Act, and the amendment made by this Act, shall take effect on the March 18 that first occurs after the date of enactment of this Act.
