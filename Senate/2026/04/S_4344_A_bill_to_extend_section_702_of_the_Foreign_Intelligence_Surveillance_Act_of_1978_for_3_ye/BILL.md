A BILL
To extend section 702 of the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 for 3 years.
#### 1. Extension of section 702 authority for 3 years
##### (a) Effective dates
Section 403(b) of the FISA Amendments Act of 2008 ( Public Law 110–261 ; 122 Stat. 2474) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (1) ( 50 U.S.C. 1881 note), by striking two years after the date of enactment of the Reforming Intelligence and Securing America Act and inserting April 20, 2029 ; and
**(2)**
in paragraph (2) ( 18 U.S.C. 2511 note), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking two years after the date of enactment of the Reforming Intelligence and Securing America Act and inserting April 20, 2029 .
##### (b) Conforming amendment
Section 404(b)(1) of the FISA Amendments Act of 2008 ( Public Law 110–261 ; 122 Stat. 2476), is amended, in the heading, by striking two years after the date of enactment of the Reforming Intelligence and Securing America Act and inserting April 20, 2029 .
