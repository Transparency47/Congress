119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 694
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 28, 2026 Mr. Reed (for himself, Mr. Scott of South Carolina , Mr. Banks , Ms. Blunt Rochester , Mr. Boozman , Mrs. Britt , Mr. Budd , Ms. Cantwell , Mrs. Capito , Ms. Collins , Mr. Cramer , Mr. Crapo , Mr. Durbin , Mrs. Gillibrand , Mr. Hagerty , Ms. Hassan , Mrs. Hyde-Smith , Mr. Kelly , Mr. King , Ms. Lummis , Mr. Peters , Mr. Ricketts , Mr. Risch , Mr. Rounds , Mr. Tuberville , Mr. Whitehouse , Mr. Wicker , Mr. Moreno , and Mr. Daines ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating April 2026 as Financial Literacy Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates April 2026 as Financial Literacy Month to raise public awareness about—
**(A)**
the importance of personal financial education in the United States; and
**(B)**
the serious consequences that may result from a lack of understanding about personal finances; and
**(2)**
calls on the Federal Government, States, localities, schools, nonprofit organizations, businesses, and the people of the United States to observe Financial Literacy Month with appropriate programs and activities.
