IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 28, 2026 Mr. Grassley (for himself, Mr. Warner , and Mr. Coons ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to

Designating the week of April 20 through April 24, 2026, as National Home Visiting Week .
That the Senate supports—
**(1)**

**(2)**
the goals and ideals of National Home Visiting Week.
