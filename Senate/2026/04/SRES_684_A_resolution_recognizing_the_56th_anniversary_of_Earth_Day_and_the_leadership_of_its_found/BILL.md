119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 684
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 22, 2026 Ms. Baldwin submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 56th anniversary of Earth Day and the leadership of its founder, Senator Gaylord Nelson.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes April 22, 2026, as the 56th anniversary of Earth Day; and
**(2)**
commends the leadership and vision of the founder of Earth Day, Senator Gaylord Nelson.
