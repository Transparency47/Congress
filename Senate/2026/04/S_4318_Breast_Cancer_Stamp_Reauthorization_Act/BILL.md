April 16 (legislative day, April 14), 2026 Ms. Rosen (for herself, Mrs. Moody , Ms. Hirono , Mrs. Capito , Mrs. Shaheen , and Mrs. Hyde-Smith ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 39, United States Code, to extend the authority of the United States Postal Service to issue a semipostal stamp to raise funds for breast cancer research.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Breast Cancer Stamp Reauthorization Act .
#### 2. Extension of postage stamp for breast cancer research
Section 414(h) of title 39, United States Code, is amended by striking 2027 and inserting 2037 .
