119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 606
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 11, 2026 Mr. Lankford (for himself, Mrs. Shaheen , Mr. Booker , Mr. Boozman , Mrs. Britt , Mr. Budd , Mrs. Capito , Mr. Cramer , Mr. Cruz , Ms. Ernst , Mrs. Fischer , Mr. Grassley , Mr. Hoeven , Mr. Kaine , Mr. Merkley , Mr. Mullin , Mr. Ricketts , Ms. Rosen , Mr. Sullivan , Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. Schumer , Mr. McConnell , Mr. Cotton , Mr. Kim , and Mr. Durbin ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
RESOLUTION
Condemning the Government of Iran for its suppression of the right of Iranians to peacefully assemble.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
strongly condemns the Government of Iran’s continued killing of protestors and bystanders;
**(2)**
condemns the Government of Iran for its suppression of Iranians' basic human rights, including their right to peacefully assemble;
**(3)**
commends the courage of the Iranian people to protest amid increased and harsh persecution from the Government of Iran;
**(4)**
calls on the Government of Iran to hold free and fair elections and allow the people of Iran to determine their own future; and
**(5)**
supports the calls of the Iranian people to bring human rights violators to justice.
