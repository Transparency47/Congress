III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 610
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 12, 2026 Mrs. Murray (for herself and Ms. Cantwell ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the Seattle Seahawks for winning Super Bowl LX.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
congratulates the Seattle Seahawks, their staff, and 12s everywhere for the victory of the Seahawks in Super Bowl LX; and
**(2)**
respectfully directs the Secretary of the Senate to transmit an enrolled copy of this resolution to—
**(A)**
the chair of the Seattle Seahawks, Jody Allen;
**(B)**
the president of the Seattle Seahawks, Chuck Arnold, and the general manager of the Seattle Seahawks, John Schneider; and
**(C)**
the head coach of the Seattle Seahawks, Mike Macdonald.
