IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 5, 2026 Ms. Ernst introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
A BILL
To provide for a temporary adjustment to the prospectus thresholds for certain General Services Administration projects, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Prospectus Modernization Act of 2026 .
#### 2. GSA prospectus threshold
##### (a) In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, for fiscal years 2026 through 2028, section 3307(a) of title 40, United States Code, shall be applied—
**(1)**
by substituting $10,000,000 for $1,500,000 in each of paragraphs (1) and (2) of that section; and
**(2)**
by substituting $5,000,000 for $750,000 in paragraph (3) of that section.
##### (b) No adjustment
For purposes of fiscal years 2026 through 2028, section 3307(h) of title 40, United States Code, shall not apply to the amounts substituted under subsection (a).
