119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. 3818
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 10, 2026 Mr. King (for himself and Mr. Cramer ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to repeal a certain limitation on the receipt of assistance under both the Veteran Readiness and Employment program and the educational assistance programs of the Department of Veterans Affairs.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Ensuring Benefits for Disabled Veterans Act .
#### 2. Repeal of certain limitation on receipt of assistance under both the Veteran Readiness and Employment program and the educational assistance programs of the Department of Veterans Affairs
Section 3695 of title 38, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
by striking subsection (b); and
**(2)**
by redesignating subsection (c) as subsection (b).
