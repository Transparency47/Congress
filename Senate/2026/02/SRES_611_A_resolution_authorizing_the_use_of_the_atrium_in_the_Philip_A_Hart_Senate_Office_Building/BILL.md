Authorizing the use of the atrium in the Philip A. Hart Senate Office Building for a Bipawtisan Doggi Gras Pawrade on February 25, 2026, from 4:00p.m. to 6:00p.m.
#### 1. Use of the atrium in the Hart Senate Office building for a Bipawtisan Doggi Gras Pawrade
The atrium in the Philip A. Hart Senate Office Building is authorized to be used on February 25, 2026, from 4:00p.m. to 6:00p.m., for a Bipawtisan Doggi Gras Pawrade.
