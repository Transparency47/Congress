A BILL
To reauthorize the National Integrated Drought Information System Act of 2006, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the NIDIS Reauthorization Act of 2026 .

Section 4 of the National Integrated Drought Information System Act of 2006 ( Public Law 109–430 ; 15 U.S.C. 313d note) is amended to read as follows:
4. Authorization of appropriations There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this Act— (1) $15,000,000 for fiscal year 2026. (2) $15,500,000 for fiscal year 2027. (3) $16,000,000 for fiscal year 2028. (4) $16,500,000 for fiscal year 2029. (5) $17,000,000 for fiscal year 2030. .
