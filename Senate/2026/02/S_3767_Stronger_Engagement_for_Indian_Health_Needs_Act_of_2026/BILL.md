A BILL
To amend the Indian Health Care Improvement Act to elevate the position of Director of the Indian Health Service within the Department of Health and Human Services to Assistant Secretary for Indian Health, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Stronger Engagement for Indian Health Needs Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Assistant Secretary for Indian Health
##### (a) In general
Section 601 of the Indian Health Care Improvement Act ( 25 U.S.C. 1661 ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (a)—
**(A)**
in paragraph (2)—
**(i)**
in the paragraph heading, by striking Director and inserting Assistant Secretary for Indian Health ;
**(ii)**
in the first sentence, by striking a Director and inserting an Assistant Secretary for Indian Health (referred to in this section as the Assistant Secretary ) ;
**(iii)**
in the second sentence, by striking The Director shall report and inserting The Assistant Secretary shall report directly ;
**(iv)**
in the third sentence, by striking the Director and inserting the Assistant Secretary ; and
**(v)**

**(B)**

##### (b) References
Any reference to the Director of the Indian Health Service in any Federal law, Executive order, rule, regulation, or delegation of authority, or in any document of or relating to the Director of the Indian Health Service, shall be deemed to be a reference to the Assistant Secretary for Indian Health.
##### (c) Conforming amendments
**(1)**
Section 603(b) of the Indian Health Care Improvement Act ( 25 U.S.C. 1663(b) ) is amended by striking Office of the Director and inserting Office of the Assistant Secretary for Indian Health .
**(2)**
Section 604(b) of the Indian Health Care Improvement Act ( 25 U.S.C. 1663a(b) ) is amended, in each of paragraphs (2) and (3), by striking Office of the Director and inserting Office of the Assistant Secretary for Indian Health .
