119th CONGRESS

S. 3896
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 24, 2026 Mrs. Blackburn introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to modify the Department of Veterans Affairs procurement hierarchy of small business preferences, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Veterans Affairs Opportunity for Small Businesses Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Modification of Department of Veterans Affairs procurement hierarchy of small business preferences
Section 8127(h) of title 38, United States Code, is amended—
**(1)**
by striking paragraph (3);
**(2)**
by redesignating paragraph (4) as paragraph (3); and
**(3)**
in paragraph (3), as redesignated by paragraph (2), by inserting established under the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 631 et seq. ) before the period at the end.
