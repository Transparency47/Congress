III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 575
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 6, 2026 Mr. Lankford (for himself and Mr. Mullin ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the Oklahoma State University men’s cross country team for winning the 2025 National Collegiate Athletic Association Men’s Division I Cross Country National Championship.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
congratulates the 2025 Oklahoma State University men’s cross country team on its championship season; and
**(2)**
recognizes the 2025 Oklahoma State University men’s cross country team for its hard work and dedication to the sport and for the excitement it brings to Oklahoma State University, the State of Oklahoma, and to Cowboy fans everywhere.
