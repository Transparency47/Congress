119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 588
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 27, 2026 Mr. Van Hollen (for himself, Ms. Alsobrooks , Ms. Blunt Rochester , Mr. Heinrich , and Mr. Merkley ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
RESOLUTION
Celebrating the 40th anniversary of the International Coastal Cleanup.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
celebrates 2025 as the 40th anniversary of the International Coastal Cleanup;
**(2)**
supports the goals and ambitions of the International Coastal Cleanup;
**(3)**
encourages the people of the United States to participate in International Coastal Cleanup activities; and
**(4)**
highlights the importance of reducing plastic pollution and addressing the problem at its source by producing less plastics.
