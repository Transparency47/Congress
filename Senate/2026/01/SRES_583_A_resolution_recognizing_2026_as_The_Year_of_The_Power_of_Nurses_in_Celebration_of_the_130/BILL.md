III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 583
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 15, 2026 Mr. Merkley submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
RESOLUTION
Recognizing 2026 as The Year of The Power of Nurses in Celebration of the 130th Anniversary of the American Nurses Association.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes 2026 as The Year of The Power of Nurses in recognition of the 130th Anniversary of the American Nurses Association; and
**(2)**
honors the extraordinary contributions of nurses to the health, safety, and prosperity of the United States of America.
