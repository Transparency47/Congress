III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 576
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 8 (legislative day, January 7), 2026 Mr. Scott of Florida (for himself, Mr. Cotton , Mr. Budd , Mr. Tuberville , Mr. Curtis , Mr. Schmitt , Mrs. Britt , Mr. Rounds , Ms. Lummis , Mr. Cramer , Mrs. Blackburn , Mr. Mullin , and Mr. Cruz ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
RESOLUTION
Commending the United States military action in Venezuela.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
commends Operation Absolute Resolve for its precision, efficiency, and role in addressing longstanding regional instability in Latin America; and
**(2)**
emphasizes that this bold action will pave the way for democratic transitions in Venezuela and promote regional stability in the Western Hemisphere.
