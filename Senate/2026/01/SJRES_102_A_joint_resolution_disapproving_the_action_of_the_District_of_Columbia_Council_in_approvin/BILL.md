119th CONGRESS

S. J. RES. 102
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 27, 2026 Mr. Scott of Florida introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the action of the District of Columbia Council in approving the D.C. Income and Franchise Tax Conformity and Revision Temporary Amendment Act of 2025.
That Congress disapproves of the action of the District of Columbia Council described as follows: D.C. Income and Franchise Tax Conformity and Revision Temporary Amendment Act of 2025 (D.C. Act A26–0217), enacted by the District of Columbia Council on December 20, 2025, and transmitted to Congress pursuant to section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act on December 30, 2025.
