(B) Performing a home study on a prospective adoptive parent in an incoming case and reporting on such a study. At the time of initial or renewal of accreditation or limited accreditation of agencies, and approval of persons, the applying entity shall indicate whether the entity seeks accreditation as\u2014 (A) an accredited agency or approved person; or (B) an agency with a limited accreditation. .\n**(2) Exemption from paperwork reduction act**\nSection 503(c) of the Intercountry Adoption Act of 2000 ( 42 U.S.C. 14953(c) ) is amended by inserting 202(b)(1), after 104, .\n#### 4.
