IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 15, 2026 Mr. Murphy introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Scotland, Connecticut.
#### 1. Single, unique ZIP code for Scotland, Connecticut
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code, to be numbered 06264, applicable to the area encompassing solely Scotland, Connecticut.
