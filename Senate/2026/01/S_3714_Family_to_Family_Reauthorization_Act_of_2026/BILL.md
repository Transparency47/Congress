IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 28, 2026 Ms. Hassan (for herself and Mr. Scott of South Carolina ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend title V of the Social Security Act to extend funding for the family-to-family health information centers.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Family-to-Family Reauthorization Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Extension of funding for family-to-family health information centers
Section 501(c)(1)(A) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 701(c)(1)(A) ), as amended by section 6303 of division F of the Continuing Appropriations, Agriculture, Legislative Branch, Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, and Extensions Act, 2026 ( Public Law 119–37 ; 139 Stat. 636) is amended—
**(1)**
in clause (viii), by striking and at the end;
**(2)**
in clause (ix), by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon; and
**(3)**
by adding at the end the following:
(x) for the period beginning on January 31, 2026, and ending on September 30, 2026, an amount equal to the pro rata portion of the amount appropriated for fiscal year 2025; and (xi) $6,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2030. .
