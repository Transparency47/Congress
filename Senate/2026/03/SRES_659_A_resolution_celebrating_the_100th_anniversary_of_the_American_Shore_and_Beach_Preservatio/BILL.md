119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 659
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 23, 2026 Mr. Whitehouse (for himself and Mr. Cassidy ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Celebrating the 100th anniversary of the American Shore and Beach Preservation Association.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes and celebrates the 100th anniversary of the American Shore and Beach Preservation Association (in this resolution referred to as the Association );
**(2)**
commemorates the centennial of the Association, honoring a century of shore and beach preservation and progress; and
**(3)**
congratulates the leaders and members of the Association on a century of dedication to the shores and beaches of the United States and recognizes the enduring importance of its mission.
