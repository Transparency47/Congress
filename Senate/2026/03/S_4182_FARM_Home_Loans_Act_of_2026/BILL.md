March 24, 2026 Mr. Welch (for himself, Mr. Justice , Mr. Schiff , and Mrs. Hyde-Smith ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry
A BILL
To amend the Farm Credit Act of 1971 to modify rural housing financing under that Act.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Fostering the Availability in Rural Markets of Home Loans Act of 2026 or the FARM Home Loans Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Rural housing financing
Section 1.11(b) of the Farm Credit Act of 1971 ( 12 U.S.C. 2019(b) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (2), by striking appurtenances and inserting appurtenances, including accessory dwelling units, ; and
**(2)**
in paragraph (3), by striking 2,500 and inserting 10,000 .
