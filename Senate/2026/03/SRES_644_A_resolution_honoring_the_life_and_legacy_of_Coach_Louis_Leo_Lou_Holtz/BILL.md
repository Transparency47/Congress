119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 644
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 16, 2026 Mr. Young (for himself, Mr. Coons , and Mr. Banks ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Honoring the life and legacy of Coach Louis Leo Lou Holtz.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
honors the life and legacy of Louis Leo Lou Holtz, including his dedication to the game of college football and the promotion of excellence, character, and teamwork in student-athletes;
**(2)**
remembers the drive, faith, and character of Coach Holtz and all that he did to educate and mentor hundreds of players over his more than 3 decades as a college football head coach; and
**(3)**
extends a heartfelt thank you to Coach Holtz for his service to the game of college football and continues to honor his enduring legacy of faith, family, and the relentless pursuit of excellence.
