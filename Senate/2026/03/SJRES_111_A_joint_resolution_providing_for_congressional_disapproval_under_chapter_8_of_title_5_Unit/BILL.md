119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. J. RES. 111
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 4, 2026 Ms. Warren introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System relating to Revisions to the Large Financial Institution Rating System and Framework for the Supervision of Insurance Organizations .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of the Treasury relating to Revisions to the Large Financial Institution Rating System and Framework for the Supervision of Insurance Organizations (90 Fed. Reg. 51329 (November 17, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
