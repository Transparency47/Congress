S. J. RES. 139
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 19, 2026 Mr. Bennet (for himself, Mr. Whitehouse , and Mr. Hickenlooper ) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
March 19, 2026 Committee discharged, by petition, pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 802(c) , and placed on the calendar
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Disapproval; Colorado; Regional Haze Plan for the Second Implementation Period .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Disapproval; Colorado; Regional Haze Plan for the Second Implementation Period (91 Fed. Reg. 3048 (January 26, 2026)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
