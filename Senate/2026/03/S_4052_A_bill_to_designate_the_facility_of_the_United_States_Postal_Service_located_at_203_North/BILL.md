IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 11, 2026 Mr. Schmitt (for himself and Mr. Hawley ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 203 North Clay Street in Marshfield, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the Edwin P. Hubble Post Office .
#### 1. Edwin P. Hubble Post Office
##### (a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 203 North Clay Street in Marshfield, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the Edwin P. Hubble Post Office .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Edwin P. Hubble Post Office .
