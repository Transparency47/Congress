March 5, 2026 Ms. Warren (for herself, Mr. Kim , Mr. Wyden , Ms. Cortez Masto , Mr. Kaine , Mr. Merkley , and Mr. Coons ) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Industry and Security of the Department of Commerce relating to One Year Suspension of Expansion of End-User Controls for Affiliates of Certain Listed Entities .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Industry and Security of the Department of Commerce relating to One Year Suspension of Expansion of End-User Controls for Affiliates of Certain Listed Entities (90 Fed. Reg. 50857 (November 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
