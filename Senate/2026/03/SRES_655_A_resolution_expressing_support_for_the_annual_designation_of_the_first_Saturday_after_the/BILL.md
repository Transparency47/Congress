II
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 655
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 22, 2026 Mr. Ricketts (for himself, Mr. Murphy , Mr. Grassley , Mr. Cramer , Mr. Cornyn , Ms. Lummis , Mr. Daines , Mr. Risch , and Mrs. Britt ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the annual designation of the first Saturday after the Spring Equinox as National Day of Play .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates March 21, 2026, as National Day of Play ;
**(2)**
expresses support for the annual designation of the first Saturday after the Spring Equinox as National Day of Play ;
**(3)**
recognizes the importance of social connection for mental, physical, and social development; and
**(4)**
encourages people to put their electronics down and play.
