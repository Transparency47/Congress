119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. J. RES. 151
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 26, 2026 Ms. Hirono introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to the withdrawal of the rule relating to Bulletin 2023–01: Unfair Billing and Collection Practices After Bankruptcy Discharges of Certain Student Loan Debts .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to the withdrawal of the rule relating to Bulletin 2023–01: Unfair Billing and Collection Practices After Bankruptcy Discharges of Certain Student Loan Debts (88 Fed. Reg. 17366 (March 23, 2023)) (90 Fed. Reg. 20084 (May 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
