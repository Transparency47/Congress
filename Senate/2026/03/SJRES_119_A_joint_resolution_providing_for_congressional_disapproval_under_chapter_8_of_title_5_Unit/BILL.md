IIA
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. J. RES. 119
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 9, 2026 Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Approval; Montana; Regional Haze Plan for the Second Implementation Period .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Approval; Montana; Regional Haze Plan for the Second Implementation Period (90 Fed. Reg. 54586 (November 28, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
