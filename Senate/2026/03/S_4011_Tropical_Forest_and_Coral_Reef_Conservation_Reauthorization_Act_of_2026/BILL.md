A BILL
To reauthorize the Tropical Forest and Coral Reef Conservation Act of 1998.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Tropical Forest and Coral Reef Conservation Reauthorization Act of 2026 .
#### 2. Reauthorization
Section 806(d) of the Tropical Forest and Coral Reef Conservation Act of 1998 ( 22 U.S.C. 2431d(d) ) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraphs:
(14) $20,000,000 for fiscal year 2028. (15) $20,000,000 for fiscal year 2029. (16) $20,000,000 for fiscal year 2030. (17) $20,000,000 for fiscal year 2031. (18) $20,000,000 for fiscal year 2032. .
