119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. J. RES. 136
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 19, 2026 Mr. Sanders (for himself, Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. Merkley , and Mr. Welch ) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval of the proposed licensing of certain defense articles and services to Israel.
That the following proposed licensing of defense articles and services to Israel is prohibited:
**(1)**
The licensing of defense articles and services described in the arms sales notification submitted to Congress pursuant to section 36(c)(2) of the Arms Export Control Act ( 22 U.S.C. 2776(c)(2) ) and published in the Congressional Record on March 12, 2026, including 5,000 defense articles relating to the Small Diameter Bomb Weapon Systems.
