119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. J. RES. 121
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 9, 2026 Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Oil and Natural Gas Sector Climate Review: Extension of Deadlines in Standards of Performance for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources and Emissions Guidelines for Existing Sources .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Oil and Natural Gas Sector Climate Review: Extension of Deadlines in Standards of Performance for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources and Emissions Guidelines for Existing Sources (90 Fed. Reg. 55671 (December 3, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
