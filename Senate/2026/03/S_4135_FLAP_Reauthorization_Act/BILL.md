119th CONGRESS

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 18, 2026 Mr. Merkley (for himself, Mr. Sheehy , Mr. Wyden , Mr. Kelly , Mr. Heinrich , and Ms. Cortez Masto ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
A BILL
To reauthorize the Federal lands access program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the FLAP Reauthorization Act .
#### 2. Federal lands access program
There are authorized to be appropriated out of the Highway Trust Fund (other than the Mass Transit Account) for the Federal lands access program under section 204 of title 23, United States Code—
**(1)**
$313,975,000 for fiscal year 2027;
**(2)**
$318,975,000 for fiscal year 2028;
**(3)**
$323,975,000 for fiscal year 2029;
**(4)**
$328,975,000 for fiscal year 2030; and
**(5)**
$333,975,000 for fiscal year 2031.
