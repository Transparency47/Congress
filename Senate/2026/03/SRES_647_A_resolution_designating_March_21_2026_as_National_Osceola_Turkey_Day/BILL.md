IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 17, 2026 Mr. Scott of Florida (for himself and Mrs. Moody ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Designating March 21, 2026, as National Osceola Turkey Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**

**(2)**
