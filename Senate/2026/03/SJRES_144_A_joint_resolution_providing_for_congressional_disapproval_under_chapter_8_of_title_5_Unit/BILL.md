IIA
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. J. RES. 144
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 25, 2026 Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to Fair Credit Reporting Act; Preemption of State Laws .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to Fair Credit Reporting Act; Preemption of State Laws (90 Fed. Reg. 48710 (May 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
