119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 651
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 19, 2026 Ms. Warren (for herself and Mr. Markey ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
RESOLUTION
Honoring the USS Massachusetts (SSN–798) Virginia Class nuclear submarine and her crew on the historic occasion of her commissioning, on March 28, 2026.
That
—
**(1)**
the Senate—
**(A)**
again would like to recognize the profound historical significance of the commissioning of warship USS Massachusetts (SSN–798); and
**(B)**
extends its best wishes for good fortune for all those that sail aboard her, for fair winds and following seas during her service to the United States; and
**(2)**
service of the USS Massachusetts (SSN–798) shall commence with her commissioning in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts on March 28, 2026, in Boston Harbor.
