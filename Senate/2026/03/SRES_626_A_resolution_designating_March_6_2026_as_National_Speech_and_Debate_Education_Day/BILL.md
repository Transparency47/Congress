III
119th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 626
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 4, 2026 Mr. Grassley (for himself, Mr. Coons , Mr. Lankford , Mrs. Blackburn , Mr. Barrasso , Mr. Budd , Mrs. Hyde-Smith , Mrs. Britt , Mr. Cramer , Mr. Daines , Mr. Risch , Mr. Scott of Florida , Mr. King , Mr. Merkley , Mr. Durbin , Ms. Klobuchar , Ms. Hassan , Ms. Warren , and Ms. Blunt Rochester ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating March 6, 2026, as National Speech and Debate Education Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates March 6, 2026, as National Speech and Debate Education Day ;
**(2)**
strongly affirms the purposes of National Speech and Debate Education Day; and
**(3)**
encourages educational institutions, businesses, community and civic associations, and all people of the United States to celebrate and promote National Speech and Debate Education Day.
