119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. J. RES. 85
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
October 1, 2025 Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Commercial and Industrial Solid Waste Incineration Units: Temporary-Use Incinerators and Air Curtain Incinerators Used in Disaster Recovery .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Commercial and Industrial Solid Waste Incineration Units: Temporary-Use Incinerators and Air Curtain Incinerators Used in Disaster Recovery (90 Fed. Reg. 41508 (August 26, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
