III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 433
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
October 6, 2025 Mr. Moran (for himself and Ms. Hassan ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the contributions and achievements of student parents in seeking and completing a postsecondary education and designating September 2025 as National Student Parent Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
expresses support for the contributions and achievements of student parents in seeking and completing a postsecondary education; and
**(2)**
designates September 2025 as National Student Parent Month .
