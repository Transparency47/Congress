III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 431
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
October 3, 2025 Mr. Sullivan (for himself and Mr. Schiff ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and objectives of Choose Respect Day.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the goals and objectives of Choose Respect Day; and
**(2)**
encourages all private citizens, organizations, and Federal, State, and local governmental and legislative entities to recognize Choose Respect Day through proclamations, activities, and educational efforts in furtherance of changing the culture around the tolerance of domestic violence.
