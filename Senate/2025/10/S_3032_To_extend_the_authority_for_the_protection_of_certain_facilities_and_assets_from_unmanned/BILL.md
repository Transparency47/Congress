A BILL
To extend the authority for the protection of certain facilities and assets from unmanned aircraft.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Counter-UAS Authority Extension Act .
#### 2. Extension of Counter-UAS authorities of the Department of Homeland Security and the Department of Justice
Section 210G(i) of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 ( 6 U.S.C. 124n(i) ) is amended by striking September 30, 2025 and inserting September 30, 2028 .
