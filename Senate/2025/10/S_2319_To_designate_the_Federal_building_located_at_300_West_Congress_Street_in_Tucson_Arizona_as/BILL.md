119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 2319
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 17, 2025 Mr. Kelly (for himself and Mr. Gallego ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
A BILL
To designate the Federal building located at 300 West Congress Street in Tucson, Arizona, as the Raúl M. Grijalva Federal Building .
#### 1. Designation
The Federal building located at 300 West Congress Street in Tucson, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Raúl M. Grijalva Federal Building .
#### 2. References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the Raúl M. Grijalva Federal Building .
