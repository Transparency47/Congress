III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 442
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
October 8, 2025 Mr. Durbin (for himself, Mr. Barrasso , Mr. Wicker , Mr. Schumer , Mr. Thune , Mrs. Shaheen , Mr. Risch , Mr. Reed , Ms. Murkowski , and Mr. Sullivan ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
RESOLUTION
Condemning Russian incursions into NATO territory and reaffirming Article 5 of the North Atlantic Treaty.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
condemns Russian incursions into the territory and airspace of NATO member countries;
**(2)**
condemns Russia’s continued assault on Ukraine, kidnapping of Ukrainian children, and refusal to negotiate an end to the war it started; and
**(3)**
reaffirms NATO’s article 5 commitment to collective self-defense.
