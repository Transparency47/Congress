III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 474
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
October 29, 2025 Mrs. Blackburn (for herself and Mr. Kaine ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating October 2025 as National Country Music Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates October 2025 as National Country Music Month ;
**(2)**
honors the contributions of country music to the story and history of the United States; and
**(3)**
encourages the American people to observe National Country Music Month with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
