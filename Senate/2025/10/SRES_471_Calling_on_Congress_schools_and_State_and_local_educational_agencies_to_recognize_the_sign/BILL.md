III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 471
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
October 28, 2025 Mr. Cassidy (for himself, Mr. Hickenlooper , Mrs. Capito , Mr. King , Ms. Warren , and Mr. Boozman ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Calling on Congress, schools, and State and local educational agencies to recognize the significant educational implications of dyslexia that must be addressed, and designating October 2025 as National Dyslexia Awareness Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
calls on Congress, schools, and State and local educational agencies to recognize that dyslexia has significant educational implications that must be addressed; and
**(2)**
designates October 2025 as National Dyslexia Awareness Month .
