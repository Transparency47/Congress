III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 524
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 3, 2025 Mr. Tuberville submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 90th anniversary of the Alabama Highway Patrol and commemorating its history of excellence.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
commemorates the 90th Anniversary of the Alabama Highway Patrol; and
**(2)**
extends gratitude and commendation for the Alabama Highway Patrol's 9 decades of exceptional service and devotion to the State of Alabama.
