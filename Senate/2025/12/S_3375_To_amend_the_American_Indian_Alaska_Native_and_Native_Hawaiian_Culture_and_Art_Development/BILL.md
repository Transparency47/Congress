A BILL
To amend the American Indian, Alaska Native, and Native Hawaiian Culture and Art Development Act to modify the program for Native Hawaiian and Alaska Native culture and arts development, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Native Arts and Culture Promotion Act .
#### 2. Amendments to American Indian, Alaska Native, and Native Hawaiian Culture and Art Development Act
Section 1521 of the American Indian, Alaska Native, and Native Hawaiian Culture and Art Development Act ( 20 U.S.C. 4441 ) is amended—
