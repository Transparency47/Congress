119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 3408
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 9, 2025 Mr. Merkley (for himself, Ms. Cortez Masto , Mr. Wyden , Mr. Blumenthal , and Mr. Kim ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
A BILL
To prohibit a United States currency featuring the likeness of a living or sitting President.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Change Corruption Act .
#### 2. No United States currency featuring the likeness of a living or sitting President
No United States currency may feature the likeness of a living or sitting President.
