119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 3338
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 3, 2025 Mr. Curtis (for himself and Mr. Lee ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, Utah.
#### 1. Single, unique ZIP Code for Highland City, Utah
Not later than 270 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing solely Highland City, Utah.
