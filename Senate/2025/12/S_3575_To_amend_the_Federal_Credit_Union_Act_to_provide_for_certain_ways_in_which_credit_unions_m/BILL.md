IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 18, 2025 Mr. Padilla (for himself and Mr. Cramer ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
A BILL
To amend the Federal Credit Union Act to provide for certain ways in which credit unions may be Agent members of the National Credit Union Administration Central Liquidity Facility.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the NCUA Central Liquidity Facility Enhancements Act .
#### 2. Agent membership
Section 304(b)(2) of the Federal Credit Union Act ( 12 U.S.C. 1795c(b)(2) ) is amended by striking all those credit unions and inserting any such credit unions .
