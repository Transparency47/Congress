IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 3, 2025 Ms. Ernst (for herself and Ms. Hassan ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
A BILL
To amend the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993, to repeal certain limits on leave for married individuals employed by the same employer.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the FAIR Leave Act or the Fair Access for Individuals to Receive Leave Act .
#### 2. Repeal of certain time limitations on leave for spouses
Section 102(f) of the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993 ( 29 U.S.C. 2612(f) ) is repealed.
