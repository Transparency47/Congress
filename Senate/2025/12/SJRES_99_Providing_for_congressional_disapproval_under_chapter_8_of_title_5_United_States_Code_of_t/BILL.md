119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. J. RES. 99
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 10, 2025 Ms. Rosen (for herself, Mr. Coons , Mr. King , Mr. Schiff , Mr. Durbin , Mr. Van Hollen , Mrs. Shaheen , Mr. Padilla , Mr. Welch , Ms. Cortez Masto , and Mr. Bennet ) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services relating to Removal of the Automatic Extension of Employment Authorization Documents .
That Congress disapproves the interim final rule submitted by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services relating to Removal of the Automatic Extension of Employment Authorization Documents (90 Fed. Reg. 48799 (October 30, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
