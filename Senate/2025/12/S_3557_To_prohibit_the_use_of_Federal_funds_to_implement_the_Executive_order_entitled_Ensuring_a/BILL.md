119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 3557
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 17, 2025 Mr. Markey (for himself, Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. Welch , Mr. Wyden , Mr. Sanders , Mr. Schiff , Mr. Booker , Mr. Luján , Ms. Klobuchar , Mr. Padilla , and Mr. Durbin ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
A BILL
To prohibit the use of Federal funds to implement the Executive order entitled Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence .
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the States' Right to Regulate AI Act .
#### 2. No Federal funds for Executive order relating to State regulation of artificial intelligence
No Federal funds may be used to implement, administer, or enforce the Executive order entitled Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence , issued on December 11, 2025.
