119th CONGRESS

S. 3479
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
December 15, 2025 Mr. Scott of Florida (for himself and Mrs. Blackburn ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To specify that the Council on American-Islamic Relations shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No Tax Exemptions For Terror Act .
#### 2. Council on American-Islamic relations subject to taxation
##### (a) In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Council on American-Islamic Relations shall not be treated as described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.
##### (b) Effective date
This section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
