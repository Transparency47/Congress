III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 109
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 5, 2025 Mr. Sanders submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the Senate that Russian President Vladimir Putin should immediately withdraw Russian forces from Ukraine.
That it is the sense of the Senate that the Russian Federation must—
**(1)**
immediately, completely, and unconditionally withdraw all of its military forces from any territory within the internationally recognized borders of Ukraine; and
**(2)**
immediately cease its attacks against Ukraine.
