III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 134
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 24, 2025 Mr. Scott of Florida (for himself and Mrs. Moody ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating March 15, 2025, as National Osceola Turkey Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates March 15, 2025, as National Osceola Turkey Day ; and
**(2)**
encourages the people of the United States to observe National Osceola Turkey Day with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
