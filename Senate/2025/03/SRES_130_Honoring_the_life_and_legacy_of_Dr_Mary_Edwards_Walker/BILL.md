119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 130
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 14, 2025 Mrs. Blackburn (for herself, Mrs. Gillibrand , and Ms. Collins ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Honoring the life and legacy of Dr. Mary Edwards Walker.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
honors the life and legacy of Dr. Mary Edwards Walker, recognizing her as a trailblazer in medicine, a champion for women’s rights, and a true United States hero who exemplified the ideals of service, courage, and resilience; and
**(2)**
commits to ensuring the story of Dr. Mary Edwards Walker is remembered and celebrated for generations to come, inspiring others to follow in her footsteps in pursuit of justice and equality.
