119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 135
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 25, 2025 Mr. Banks (for himself and Mr. Young ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
RESOLUTION
A resolution recognizing a century of broadcasting excellence from WOWO and celebrating the radio station’s 100th anniversary.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
celebrates March 31, 2025, as WOWO’s 100th anniversary on air;
**(2)**
recognizes WOWO’s record of exemplary broadcasting, which has strengthened communities and educated countless families on the most important issues; and
**(3)**
supports WOWO’s efforts to continue informing and inspiring generations to come.
