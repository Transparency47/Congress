119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 125
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 12, 2025 Mr. Ossoff (for himself, Mr. Curtis , Mr. Warnock , and Mr. Lee ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Commemorating the centennial of Delta Air Lines.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes Delta Air Lines for 100 years of connecting people to the world and to each other; and
**(2)**
commemorates the centennial of Delta Air Lines.
