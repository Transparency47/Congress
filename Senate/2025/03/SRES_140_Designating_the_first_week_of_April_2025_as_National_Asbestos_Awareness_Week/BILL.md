119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 140
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 26, 2025 Mr. Merkley (for himself, Mr. Daines , Mr. Durbin , Mr. Sheehy , Mr. Padilla , Mr. Markey , Mr. Booker , and Mr. Schiff ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating the first week of April 2025 as National Asbestos Awareness Week .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates the first week of April 2025 as National Asbestos Awareness Week ;
**(2)**
urges the Surgeon General to warn and educate people about the public health issue of asbestos exposure, which may be hazardous to their health; and
**(3)**
respectfully requests that the Secretary of the Senate transmit a copy of this resolution to the Office of the Surgeon General.
