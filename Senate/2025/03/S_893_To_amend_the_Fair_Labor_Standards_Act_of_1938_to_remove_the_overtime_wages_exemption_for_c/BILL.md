IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 6, 2025 Mr. Padilla (for himself, Mr. Markey , Mr. Sanders , Mr. Blumenthal , Ms. Warren , and Mr. Wyden ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
A BILL
To amend the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 to remove the overtime wages exemption for certain employees, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Guaranteeing Overtime for Truckers Act .
#### 2. Amendment to the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938
Section 13(b)(1) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 213(b)(1) ) is repealed.
