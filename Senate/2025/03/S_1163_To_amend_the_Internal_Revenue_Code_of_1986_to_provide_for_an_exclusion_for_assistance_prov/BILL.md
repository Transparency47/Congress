March 27, 2025 Mr. Crapo (for himself, Ms. Smith , Mr. Boozman , Ms. Collins , Mr. Coons , Mrs. Gillibrand , Mrs. Hyde-Smith , Mr. King , Ms. Klobuchar , Ms. Lummis , Mr. Moran , Mr. Ossoff , Mr. Risch , and Mr. Marshall ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to provide for an exclusion for assistance provided to participants in certain veterinary student loan repayment or forgiveness programs.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Rural Veterinary Workforce Act .
#### 2. Exclusion for assistance provided to participants in certain veterinary student loan repayment or forgiveness programs
##### (a) In general
Paragraph (4) of section 108(f) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended—
**(1)**
by striking or after such Act, ,
**(2)**
by striking the period at the end and inserting , under section 1415A of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 ( 7 U.S.C. 3151a ), or under any other State loan repayment or loan forgiveness program that is intended to provide for increased access to veterinary services in such State. , and
**(3)**
by striking state in the heading and inserting other .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts received by an individual in taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
