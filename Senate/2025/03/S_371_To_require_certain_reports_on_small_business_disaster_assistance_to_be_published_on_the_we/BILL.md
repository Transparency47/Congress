119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 371
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 3, 2025 Mr. Scott of South Carolina (for himself, Mr. Schiff , Mr. Budd , Mrs. Blackburn , and Mr. Scott of Florida ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship
A BILL
To require certain reports on small business disaster assistance to be published on the website of the Small Business Administration, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the SBA Disaster Transparency Act .
#### 2. Publication of reports on disaster assistance
Section 12091 of the Small Business Disaster Response and Loan Improvements Act of 2008 ( 15 U.S.C. 636k ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subsection (a)—
**(A)**
in paragraph (1), by inserting and publish on the website of the Administration after Representatives ; and
**(B)**
in paragraph (2), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by inserting and published after submitted ;
**(2)**
in subsection (b)—
**(A)**
in paragraph (1), by inserting and publish on the website of the Administration after Representatives ; and
**(B)**
in paragraph (2), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by inserting and published after submitted ;
**(3)**
in subsection (c)—
**(A)**
in paragraph (1), by inserting and publish on the website of the Administration after Representatives ; and
**(B)**
in paragraph (2), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by inserting and published after submitted ; and
**(4)**
in subsection (e)—
**(A)**
in paragraph (1)—
**(i)**
by striking a report ; and
**(ii)**
by inserting and publish on the website of the Administration a report after Representatives ; and
**(B)**
in paragraph (2), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by inserting and published after submitted .
