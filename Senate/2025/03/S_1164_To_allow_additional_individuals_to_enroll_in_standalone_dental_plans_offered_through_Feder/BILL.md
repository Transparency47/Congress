## Summary

**Increasing Access to Dental Insurance Act**

This bill permits individuals to enroll in a dental benefits plan on a health insurance exchange without also enrolling in a qualified health plan.

March 27, 2025 Ms. Hassan (for herself, Mr. Marshall , Mr. Coons , Mr. Tillis , Mr. Curtis , Mrs. Shaheen , Mr. Grassley , Mr. Peters , and Mrs. Capito ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
A BILL
To allow additional individuals to enroll in standalone dental plans offered through Federal Exchanges.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Increasing Access to Dental Insurance Act .
#### 2. Standalone dental plans
Section 1321 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act ( 42 U.S.C. 18041 ) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(f) Availability of standalone dental plans The Secretary may not restrict any qualified individual from enrolling in a plan described in section 1311(d)(2)(B)(ii) offered through an Exchange established pursuant to subsection (c) on the basis of such qualified individual not being also enrolled in a qualified health plan offered through the Exchange. .
