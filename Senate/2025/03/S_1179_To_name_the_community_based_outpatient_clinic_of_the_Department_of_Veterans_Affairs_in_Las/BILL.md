IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 27, 2025 Mr. Heinrich introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To name the community-based outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Las Cruces, New Mexico, the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic .
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic Act .
#### 2. Name of Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in Las Cruces, New Mexico
##### (a) Name
The community-based outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Las Cruces, New Mexico, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic .
##### (b) Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic.
