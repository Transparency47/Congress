IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 11 (legislative day, March 10), 2025 Mr. Curtis (for himself and Mr. Lee ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources
A BILL
To prohibit natural asset companies from entering into any agreement with respect to land in the State of Utah or natural assets on or in land in the State of Utah.
#### 1. Prohibition of natural asset companies from entering into certain agreements
##### (a) Definition of natural asset company
In this section, the term natural asset company means—
**(1)**
a corporation that—
**(A)**
holds the rights to the ecological performance of a defined area; and
**(B)**
has the authority to manage the defined area for conservation, restoration, or sustainable management; or
**(2)**
a company or organization that is substantially similar to a corporation described in paragraph (1).
##### (b) Prohibition
A natural asset company may not enter into any agreement with respect to—
**(1)**
land in the State of Utah; or
**(2)**
natural assets on or in land in the State of Utah.
