119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 146
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 27, 2025 Mrs. Blackburn submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Condemning the recent acts of violence, arson, and domestic terrorism committed throughout the United States.
That the Senate condemns the horrific acts of violence, arson, and domestic terrorism committed against electric vehicles, car dealerships, and charging stations throughout the United States.
