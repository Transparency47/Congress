March 10, 2025 Ms. Ernst introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
A BILL
To repeal and rescind any unobligated balances under sections 70002 and 70003 of the Inflation Reduction Act, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Return to Sender Act .
#### 2. Repeal and rescission of funds
The unobligated balances of amounts appropriated or otherwise made available by sections 70002 and 70003 of Public Law 117–169 (commonly referred to as the Inflation Reduction Act ), as of the date of enactment of this Act are rescinded and those sections are hereby repealed.
