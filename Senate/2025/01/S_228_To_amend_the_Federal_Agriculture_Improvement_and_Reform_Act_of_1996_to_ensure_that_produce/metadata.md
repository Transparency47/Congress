Ensuring farmers and ranchers have access to drought and disaster protections\nSection 196(c)(2) of the Federal Agriculture Improvement and Reform Act of 1996 ( 7 U.S.C. 7333(c)(2) ) is amended by adding at the end the following:\n(C) Acequia systems On making a determination described in subsection (a)(3) for producers who rely on acequia systems, the Secretary shall provide assistance under this section to those producers from all losses suffered as a result of the cause described in subsection (a)(3). .\n#### 4. Special use permits not required for routine maintenance and minor improvements of acequias\n##### (a) Definitions\nIn this section:\n**(1) Acequia**\nThe term acequia has the meaning of the term community ditch as that term is construed under New Mexico Stat. 73\u20132\u201327.\n**(2) Community user**\nThe term community user means an employee, contractor, delegate, representative, volunteer, or other authorized personnel of the governing body of an acequia.\n**(3) Secretary concerned**\nThe term Secretary concerned means\u2014\n**(A)**\nthe Secretary of Agriculture, with respect to land under the jurisdiction of the Secretary of Agriculture; and\n**(B)**\nthe Secretary of the Interior, with respect to land under the jurisdiction of the Secretary of the Interior.\n##### (b) Prohibition\nA special use permit shall not be required for\u2014\n**(1)**\nthe presence or use of an acequia on Federal land or the use of the water therein; or\n**(2)**\nroutine maintenance and minor improvements described in subsection (c) conducted by a community user or governing body of an acequia on an acequia on Federal land.\n##### (c) Routine maintenance and minor improvements described\nRoutine maintenance and minor improvements referred to in subsection (b)(2) are\u2014\n**(1)**\ncleaning, maintenance, repair, or replacement-in-kind of infrastructure;\n**(2)**\nannual ditch cleaning, including removal of silt; and\n**(3)**\nany other traditional activity that preserves the state of the acequia, as agreed to in writing by the Secretary concerned and the governing body of the acequia.\n#### 5.
