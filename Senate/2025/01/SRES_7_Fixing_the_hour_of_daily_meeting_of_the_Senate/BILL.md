III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 7
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 3, 2025 Mr. Thune submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Fixing the hour of daily meeting of the Senate.
That the daily meeting of the Senate be 12 o'clock meridian unless otherwise ordered.
