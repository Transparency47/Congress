119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 203
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 23, 2025 Mr. Paul (for himself, Mr. Marshall , Mr. Tuberville , Mr. Mullin , Mr. Budd , Mr. Schmitt , Mr. Tillis , Mr. Cruz , and Mr. Banks ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
A BILL
To prohibit Federal funding of Planned Parenthood Federation of America.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Defund Planned Parenthood Act .
#### 2. Prohibition
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be made available to Planned Parenthood Federation of America, or to any of its affiliates.
