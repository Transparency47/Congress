III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 5
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 3, 2025 Mr. Thune submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Notifying the House of Representatives of the election of a President pro tempore.
That the House of Representatives be notified of the election of the Honorable Charles E. Grassley as President of the Senate pro tempore.
