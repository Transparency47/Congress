III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 3
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 3, 2025 Mr. Thune submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
To elect Charles E. Grassley, a Senator from the State of Iowa, to be President pro tempore of the Senate of the United States.
That Charles E. Grassley, a Senator from Iowa, be, and he is hereby, elected President of the Senate pro tempore.
