119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 303
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 29, 2025 Mr. Cruz (for himself, Mr. Barrasso , Mr. Scott of Florida , Mr. Daines , Mrs. Blackburn , Mr. Rounds, and Mr. Lee ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
A BILL
To amend the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 to limit to $0 the amount that the Director of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection may request to fund the activities of the Bureau.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Defund the CFPB Act .
#### 2. Limiting funding of bureau of consumer financial protection
Section 1017(a) of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 ( 12 U.S.C. 5497(a) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in paragraph (1), by striking taking into account and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting which shall be not more than $0. ;
**(2)**
by striking paragraphs (2) and (3); and
**(3)**
by redesignating paragraphs (4) and (5) as paragraphs (2) and (3), respectively.
