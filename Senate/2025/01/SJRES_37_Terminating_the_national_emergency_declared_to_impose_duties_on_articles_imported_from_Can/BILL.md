## Summary

This joint resolution terminates the national emergency declared by President Donald J. Trump on February 1, 2025, which imposed an additional 25% tariff on most imports from Canada (except for Canadian energy or energy resources, which have an additional 10% tariff).

119th CONGRESS
1st Session

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
March 11 (legislative day, March 10), 2025 Mr. Kaine (for himself, Ms. Klobuchar , Mr. Warner , Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. King , Mr. Whitehouse , and Mr. Coons ) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
JOINT RESOLUTION
Terminating the national emergency declared to impose duties on articles imported from Canada.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act ( 50 U.S.C. 1622 ), the national emergency declared on February 1, 2025, by the President in Executive Order 14193 (90 Fed. Reg. 9113) is terminated.
