119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 20
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 13, 2025 Mr. Kim (for himself, Ms. Hirono , Mr. Sullivan , Mr. Booker , Mr. Schumer , and Mr. Schatz ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of Korean American Day.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of Korean American Day;
**(2)**
urges all individuals in the United States to observe Korean American Day so as to have a greater appreciation of the invaluable contributions Korean Americans have made to the United States; and
**(3)**
honors and recognizes the 122nd anniversary of the arrival of the first Korean immigrants to the United States.
