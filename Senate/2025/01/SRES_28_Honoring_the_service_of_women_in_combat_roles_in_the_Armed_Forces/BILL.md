III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 28
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 16, 2025 Mrs. Murray (for herself, Ms. Duckworth , Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. Blumenthal , Mr. Schiff , Mr. Durbin , Mr. Coons , Mr. Booker , Ms. Smith , Mrs. Shaheen , Mr. Wyden , Ms. Hirono , Mrs. Gillibrand , Mr. Merkley , Ms. Baldwin , Mr. Warnock , Mr. Schatz , Mr. Bennet , Ms. Rosen , Mr. Padilla , Ms. Klobuchar , and Ms. Slotkin ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Armed Services
RESOLUTION
Honoring the service of women in combat roles in the Armed Forces.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes the contribution, bravery, and sacrifices of women serving in combat roles in the Armed Forces; and
**(2)**
encourages the continued celebration of the achievements of women in the Armed Forces to inspire future generations.
