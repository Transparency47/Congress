A BILL
To amend the Older Americans Act of 1965 to provide for food-based interventions.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Disease Intervention through Nutrition Education Act or the DINE Act .
#### 2. Food-based interventions
##### (a) Definition of disease prevention and health promotion services
Section 102(14) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( 42 U.S.C. 3002(14) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in subparagraph (B), by inserting screening for eligibility for Federal and non-Federal (including community-based) food is medicine programs and before routine ;
**(2)**
in subparagraph (C), by inserting , including referrals to Federal and non-Federal (including community-based) food is medicine programs, if appropriate after caregivers ; and
**(3)**
in subparagraph (D), by inserting (including from medically tailored meals and produce prescriptions) after improved nutrition .
##### (b) Administration of nutrition services
Section 205(a)(2)(A) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( 42 U.S.C. 3016(a)(2)(A) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in clause (vi), by inserting , including the use of innovative approaches, including food-based interventions, such as produce prescriptions after systems ; and
**(2)**
in clause (viii), by inserting and innovative approaches, including intervention programs, such as produce prescriptions, after including strategies .
##### (c) Nutrition education
Section 214(2)(C) of the Older Americans Act of 1965 ( 42 U.S.C. 3020e(2)(C) ) is amended—
**(1)**
by inserting interventions and after other ; and
**(2)**
by inserting and referral to food-based intervention programs after and counseling .
