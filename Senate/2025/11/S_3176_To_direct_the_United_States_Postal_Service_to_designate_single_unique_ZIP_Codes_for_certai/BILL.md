119th CONGRESS

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
November 18, 2025 Mr. Lankford (for himself and Mr. Mullin ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs
A BILL
To direct the United States Postal Service to designate single, unique ZIP Codes for certain Oklahoma communities, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Single, unique zip codes for certain Oklahoma communities
Not later than 270 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code for each of the following communities:
**(1)**
Hochatown, Oklahoma.
**(2)**
North Enid, Oklahoma.
