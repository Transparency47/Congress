III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 515
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
November 20, 2025 Mr. Padilla (for himself and Mr. Schiff ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the Los Angeles Dodgers for winning the 2025 Major League Baseball World Series.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
congratulates the Los Angeles Dodgers for winning the 2025 Major League Baseball World Series;
**(2)**
celebrates the dedication and teamwork of the players and staff who made this historic season possible; and
**(3)**
respectfully directs the Secretary of the Senate to transmit an enrolled copy of this resolution to—
**(A)**
the chairman and controlling owner of the Los Angeles Dodgers, Mark Walter;
**(B)**
the president of the Los Angeles Dodgers, Stan Kasten; and
**(C)**
the manager of the 2025 Los Angeles Dodgers, Dave Roberts.
