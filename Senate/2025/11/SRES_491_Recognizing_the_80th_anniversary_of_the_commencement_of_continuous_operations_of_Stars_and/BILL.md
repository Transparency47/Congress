119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 491
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
November 7, 2025 Mr. Gallego submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the 80th anniversary of the commencement of continuous operations of Stars and Stripes in the Pacific and the invaluable service of the Stars and Stripes as the hometown newspaper for members of the Armed Forces, civilian employees, and family members stationed around the world.
That the Senate, on the occasion of the 80th anniversary of the commencement of continuous operations of Stars and Stripes in the Pacific—
**(1)**
commemorates this important milestone in the history of an important institution of the United States; and
**(2)**
congratulates and honors the men and women of Stars and Stripes, past and present, who have so diligently served the United States military community.
