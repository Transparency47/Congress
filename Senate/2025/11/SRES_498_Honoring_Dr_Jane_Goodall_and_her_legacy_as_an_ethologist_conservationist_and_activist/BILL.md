III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 498
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
November 10, 2025 Mr. Coons (for himself, Mr. Tillis , Mrs. Shaheen , Mrs. Murray , Mr. Schatz , Mr. Heinrich , and Mr. Welch ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Honoring Dr. Jane Goodall and her legacy as an ethologist, conservationist, and activist.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
uses April 3, 2025, and April 3, 2026, to commemorate the birth of Dr. Jane Goodall and to celebrate the extraordinary contributions of Dr. Goodall and the impact she had on the world;
**(2)**
proclaims April 3, 2025, and April 3, 2026, as Jane Goodall Day across the country; and
**(3)**
expresses gratitude to Dr. Goodall for her unwavering dedication to the well-being of animals, conservation, and the planet as a whole.
