III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 499
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
November 18, 2025 Mr. Husted submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD) Awareness Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the goals and ideals of Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD) Awareness Day ; and
**(2)**
recognizes the importance of raising awareness of this rare brain disorder.
