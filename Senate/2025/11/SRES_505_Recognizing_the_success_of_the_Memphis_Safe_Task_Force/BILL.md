III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 505
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
November 19, 2025 Mrs. Blackburn (for herself and Mr. Hagerty ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the success of the Memphis Safe Task Force.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes the monumental success that has already been achieved because of the Memphis Safe Task Force and President Donald J. Trump’s decisive leadership; and
**(2)**
commends President Trump, the most pro-law enforcement President in the history of the United States, for his work to make America safe again.
