III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 83
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 19, 2025 Mr. Schatz (for himself and Ms. Hirono ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Designating February 2025 as Hawaiian Language Month or ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates February 2025 as Hawaiian Language Month or ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i Month ;
**(2)**
commits to preserving, protecting, and promoting the use, practice, and development of ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i in alignment with the Native American Languages Act ( 25 U.S.C. 2901 et seq. ); and
**(3)**
urges the people of the United States and interested groups to celebrate ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i Month with appropriate activities and programs to demonstrate support for ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i.
