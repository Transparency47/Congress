119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 470
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 6 (legislative day, February 5), 2025 Mrs. Hyde-Smith (for herself and Mr. Hagerty ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
A BILL
To amend the CARES Act to remove a requirement on lessors to provide notice to vacate, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Respect State Housing Laws Act .
#### 2. Clarification
Section 4024 of the CARES Act ( 15 U.S.C. 9058 ) is amended by striking subsection (c).
