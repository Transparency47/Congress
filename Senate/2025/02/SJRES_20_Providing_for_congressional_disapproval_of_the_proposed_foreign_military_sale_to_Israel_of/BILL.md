IIA
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. J. RES. 20
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 20, 2025 Mr. Sanders introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval of the proposed foreign military sale to Israel of certain defense articles and services.
That the following proposed foreign military sale to Israel is prohibited:
**(1)**
The sale of the following defense articles and services, described in Transmittal No. 24–16, submitted to Congress pursuant to section 36(b)(1) of the Arms Export Control Act ( 22 U.S.C. 2776(b)(1) ), and published in the Congressional Record on February 10, 2025: an additional 10,000 155mm ancillaries (fuzes, primers, and charges) that will be added to previously implemented cases.
