## Summary

**Ensuring Continuity in Veterans Health Act**

This bill requires the consideration of continuity of care when determining whether care through the Veterans Community Care Program is in the best medical interest of a veteran.

119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 540
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 12, 2025 Mr. Tuberville introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
A BILL
To amend title 38, United States Code, to require the consideration of continuity of health care in determining best medical interest under the Veterans Community Care Program, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Ensuring Continuity in Veterans Health Act .
#### 2. Consideration of continuity of health care under Veterans Community Care Program
Section 1703(d)(2) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(F) Continuity of care. .
