119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 623
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 18, 2025 Mr. Kennedy introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
A BILL
To prohibit the Environmental Protection Agency from using assessments generated by the Integrated Risk Information System as a tier 1 data source in rulemakings and other regulatory, enforcement, or permitting actions, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No IRIS Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Prohibition on use of assessments generated by the Integrated Risk Information System program
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency may not use any assessment generated by the Integrated Risk Information System program of the Environmental Protection Agency as a basis—
**(1)**
to develop, finalize, or issue a rule or regulation;
**(2)**
to carry out any other regulatory, enforcement, or permitting action; or
**(3)**
to inform air toxics assessments or other mapping or screening tools.
