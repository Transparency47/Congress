III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 103
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 27, 2025 Mr. Gallego (for himself, Mr. Durbin , Mr. Padilla , Mr. Bennet , and Mr. Schiff ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
RESOLUTION
Condemning the rejection by the United States of a United Nations resolution condemning the illegal invasion of Ukraine by the Russian Federation.
That the Senate condemns the rejection by the United States of United Nations General Assembly Resolution A/ES–11/L.10 (2025), titled Advancing a comprehensive, just and lasting peace in Ukraine , condemning the illegal invasion of Ukraine by the Russian Federation.
