119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 2279
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 15, 2025 Mr. Hawley introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To repeal the changes to Medicaid State provider tax authority and State directed payments made by the One Big Beautiful Bill Act and provide increased funding for the rural health transformation program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protect Medicaid and Rural Hospitals Act .
#### 2. Repeal of changes to Medicaid State provider tax authority
Section 71115 of the Act titled An Act to provide for reconciliation pursuant to title II of H. Con. Res. 14 ( Public Law 119–21 ) is repealed and title XIX of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1396 et seq. ) shall be applied as if such section and the amendments made by such section had not been enacted.
#### 3. Repeal of changes to Medicaid State directed payments
##### (a) Repeal
Section 71116 of the Act titled An Act to provide for reconciliation pursuant to title II of H. Con. Res. 14 ( Public Law 119–21 ) is repealed and the provisions of such section shall have no force and effect.
##### (b) Rescission
The amounts appropriated under section 71116(e) of the Act titled An Act to provide for reconciliation pursuant to title II of H. Con. Res. 14 ( Public Law 119–21 ) are hereby rescinded.
#### 4. Increased funding for the rural health transformation program
Section 2105(h)(1)(A) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1397ee(h)(1)(A) ), as added by section 71401 of the Act titled An Act to provide for reconciliation pursuant to title II of H. Con. Res. 14 ( Public Law 119–21 ), is amended—
