119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 276
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 12, 2025 Mrs. Blackburn (for herself, Mr. Booker , Mr. Boozman , Ms. Collins , Ms. Rosen , and Mrs. Shaheen ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Designating June 12, 2025, as “Women Veterans Appreciation Day”.
That the Senate designates June 12, 2025, as Women Veterans Appreciation Day to recognize the service and sacrifices of women veterans who have served valiantly on behalf of the United States.
