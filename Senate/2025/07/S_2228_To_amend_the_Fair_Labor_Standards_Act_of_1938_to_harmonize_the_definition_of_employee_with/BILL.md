A BILL
To amend the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 to harmonize the definition of employee with the common law.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Modern Worker Empowerment Act .
#### 2. Amendments to the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 to harmonize the definition of employee
##### (a) Definition of employee
Section 3(e)(1) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 203(e)(1) ) is amended by inserting before the period the following: , as determined under the usual common law rules .
##### (b) Definition of employ
Section 3(g) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 203(g) ) is amended by inserting an employee after permit .
