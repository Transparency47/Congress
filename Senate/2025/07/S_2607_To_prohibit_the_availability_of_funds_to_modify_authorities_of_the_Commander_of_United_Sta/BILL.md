A BILL
To prohibit the availability of funds to modify authorities of the Commander of United States Cyber Command, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Prohibition on availability of funds to modify authorities of the Commander of United States Cyber Command
No funds made available for fiscal year 2026 for the Department of Defense may be obligated or expended to modify, reorganize, or otherwise change the responsibilities, authorities, or command structure of the Commander of United States Cyber Command from those in effect on June 1, 2025.
