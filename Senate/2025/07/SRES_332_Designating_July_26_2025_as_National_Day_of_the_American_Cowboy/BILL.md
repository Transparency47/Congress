III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 332
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 22, 2025 Mr. Barrasso (for himself, Mr. Hickenlooper , Ms. Lummis , Ms. Cortez Masto , Mr. Risch , Mr. Cramer , Mr. Ricketts , Mr. Cruz , Mr. Cornyn , Mr. Hoeven , and Mr. Crapo ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating July 26, 2025, as National Day of the American Cowboy .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates July 26, 2025, as National Day of the American Cowboy ; and
**(2)**
encourages the people of the United States to observe the day with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
