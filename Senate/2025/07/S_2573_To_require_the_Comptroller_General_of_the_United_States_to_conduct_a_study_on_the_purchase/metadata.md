Findings\nCongress finds the following:\n**(1)**\nThere has been a marked increase in foreign purchases of residential real estate in the United States in recent years.\n**(2)**\nSome foreign purchases of residential real estate are made through shell companies or trusts, raising concerns about transparency and beneficial ownership.\n**(3)**\nForeign ownership of residential real estate may impact housing affordability, particularly in high-demand urban and coastal markets.\n**(4)**\nThe Federal Government currently lacks a comprehensive, coordinated understanding of the extent and impact of foreign residential property ownership.\n#### 3.
