IIA
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. J. RES. 64
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 22, 2025 Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Approval; West Virginia; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan for the Second Implementation Period .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to Air Plan Approval; West Virginia; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan for the Second Implementation Period (90 Fed. Reg. 29737 (July 7, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
