July 23, 2025 Mr. Tuberville introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry
A BILL
To amend the Federal Crop Insurance Act to require certain membership on the Board of Directors of the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Farm Board Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Board of Directors composition
Section 505(a)(2)(G) of the Federal Crop Insurance Act ( 7 U.S.C. 1505(a)(2)(G) ) is amended by striking at least one specialty crop producer. and inserting the following:
at least— (i) 1 specialty crop producer; and (ii) beginning with the Board of Directors serving on or after May 1, 2027, 1 producer that actively engages in livestock production and crop production of agricultural commodities. .
