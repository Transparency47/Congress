A BILL
To adjust certain ownership and other requirements for passenger vessels, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Open America's Ports Act .
#### 2. Repeal of PVSA and adjustment of Jones Act requirements for passenger vessels
##### (a) Repeal of PVSA domestic requirements
Section 55103 of title 46, United States Code (commonly referred to as the Passenger Vessel Services Act of 1886 ), is repealed.

#### 3. Rule of construction
Nothing in the amendments made by this Act shall be construed to exempt a vessel that transports passengers between ports or places in the United States to which the coastwise laws apply, either directly or via a foreign port, from any applicable law of the United States except as explicitly provided in such amendments.
