III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 361
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 31, 2025 Mr. Reed (for himself, Mr. Sullivan , Ms. Murkowski , Ms. Cortez Masto , Ms. Rosen , Mr. Warnock , Mr. Blumenthal , Mr. King , Mr. Kelly , Mr. Cotton , Mr. Tillis , Mr. Budd , Mr. Scott of Florida , Ms. Hirono , Mr. Tuberville , and Mr. Ossoff ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating August 16, 2025, as National Airborne Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates August 16, 2025, as National Airborne Day ; and
**(2)**
calls on the people of the United States to observe National Airborne Day with appropriate programs, ceremonies, and activities.
