IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 16, 2025 Mrs. Blackburn (for herself and Ms. Lummis ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To repeal the Federal charter for the National Education Association.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the National Education Association Charter Repeal Act .
#### 2. Repeal of Federal charter to the National Education Association
##### (a) In general
Chapter 1511 of title 36, United States Code (which granted a Federal charter to the National Education Association), is repealed.
##### (b) Clerical amendment
The table of chapters for title 36, United States Code, is amended by striking the items relating to chapter 1511.
