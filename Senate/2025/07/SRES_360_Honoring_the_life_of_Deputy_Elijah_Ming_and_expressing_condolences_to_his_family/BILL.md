III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 360
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
July 31, 2025 Mr. Marshall (for himself and Mr. Moran ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Honoring the life of Deputy Elijah Ming and expressing condolences to his family.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
highly respects and appreciates all that Deputy Elijah Ming did to protect and serve his Kansas community;
**(2)**
offers condolences to the family of Elijah Ming;
**(3)**
pays tribute to Mr. Ming's noble sacrifice in the line of duty; and
**(4)**
calls on all levels of government to support the family of this fallen officer.
