119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 386
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 15, 2025 Mr. Van Hollen (for himself, Ms. Hirono , Mr. Durbin , Mr. Sanders , and Mr. Heinrich ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Designating the week of September 14 through September 20, 2025, as Community School Coordinators Appreciation Week .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates the week of September 14 through September 20, 2025, as Community School Coordinators Appreciation Week ;
**(2)**
gives thanks to community school coordinators for the work they do to serve students, families, and communities; and
**(3)**
encourages students, parents, school administrators, and public officials to participate in events that celebrate Community School Coordinators Appreciation Week.
