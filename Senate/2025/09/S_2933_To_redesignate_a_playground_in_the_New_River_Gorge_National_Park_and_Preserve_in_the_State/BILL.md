IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 29, 2025 Mr. Justice (for himself and Mrs. Capito ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources
A BILL
To redesignate a playground in the New River Gorge National Park and Preserve in the State of West Virginia as the Hearts of Gold Playground: In Honor of West Virginia Children and Families Impacted by Childhood Cancer .
#### 1. Redesignation of the Hearts of Gold Playground: In Honor of West Virginia Children and Families Impacted by Childhood Cancer in the New River Gorge National Park and Preserve, West Virginia
##### (a) Redesignation
The primary playground located in the Grandview area south of the amphitheater at New River Gorge National Park and Preserve in the State of West Virginia shall be known and designated as the Hearts of Gold Playground: In Honor of West Virginia Children and Families Impacted by Childhood Cancer .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the playground referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Hearts of Gold Playground: In Honor of West Virginia Children and Families Impacted by Childhood Cancer .
