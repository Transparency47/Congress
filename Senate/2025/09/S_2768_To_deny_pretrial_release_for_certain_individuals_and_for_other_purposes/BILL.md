119th CONGRESS

S. 2768
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 11, 2025 Mr. Cotton introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
A BILL
To deny pretrial release for certain individuals, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No Bail Post-Jail Act .
#### 2. Denial for pretrial release for prior incarceration
Section 3124(e) of title 18, United States Code is amended by adding at the end the following:
(4) A person shall be considered to pose a danger to the safety of the community and be ineligible for release if the judicial officer finds that the person— (A) is charged with a felony offense; (B) is an adult or a juvenile charged as an adult with regard to the offense; and (C) has a prior felony conviction for a crime of violence that resulted in the person serving not less than 30 days in a State or Federal correctional facility, not including any period of pretrial detention without conviction. .
