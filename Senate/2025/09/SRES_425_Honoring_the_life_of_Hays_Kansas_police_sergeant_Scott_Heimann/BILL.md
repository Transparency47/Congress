III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 425
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 30, 2025 Mr. Moran (for himself and Mr. Marshall ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Honoring the life of Hays, Kansas police sergeant Scott Heimann.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
highly appreciates and respects all that Sergeant Scott Heimann did to protect and serve his Kansas community;
**(2)**
offers condolences to the family of Sergeant Scott Heimann;
**(3)**
pays tribute to Sergeant Scott Heimann’s noble sacrifice in the line of duty; and
**(4)**
calls on all levels of government to support the family of this fallen officer.
