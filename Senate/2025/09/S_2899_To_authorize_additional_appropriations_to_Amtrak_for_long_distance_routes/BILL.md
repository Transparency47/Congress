IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 18 (legislative day, September 16), 2025 Mr. Sheehy introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
A BILL
To authorize additional appropriations to Amtrak for long-distance routes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Rail Service Continuity and Stability Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Additional appropriations to Amtrak for long-distance routes
There are authorized to be appropriated to Amtrak such additional sums as may be necessary to support service for long-distance routes (as that term is defined in section 24102 of title 49, United States Code) existing on the date of the enactment of this Act.
