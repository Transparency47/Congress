III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 393
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 16, 2025 Mr. King (for himself, Mrs. Shaheen , Ms. Collins , Mr. Murphy , Mr. Whitehouse , Mr. Reed , Mr. Blumenthal , Mr. Scott of Florida , and Mr. Markey ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating September 25, 2025, as National Lobster Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates September 25, 2025, as National Lobster Day ; and
**(2)**
encourages the people of the United States to observe the day with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
