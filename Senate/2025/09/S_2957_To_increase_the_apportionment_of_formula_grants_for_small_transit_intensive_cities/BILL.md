119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 2957
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 30, 2025 Mr. Moran (for himself and Mr. Gallego ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
A BILL
To increase the apportionment of formula grants for small transit intensive cities.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Small Communities Transit Improvement Act .
#### 2. Increase percentage of formula for small transit intensive cities
Section 5336(h)(3) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking 3 percent and inserting 5 percent .
