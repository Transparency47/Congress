119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 400
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 17 (legislative day, September 16), 2025 Mr. Reed (for himself, Ms. Collins , Mr. King , Mr. Whitehouse , Mr. Blumenthal , and Mr. Durbin ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for designation of the week of September 14 through 20, 2025, as National Adult Education and Family Literacy Week .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the designation of the week of September 14 through 20, 2025, as National Adult Education and Family Literacy Week , to raise public awareness about the importance of adult education, workforce skills, and family literacy;
**(2)**
encourages people across the United States to support programs that assist individuals in need of adult education, workforce skills, and family literacy programs;
**(3)**
recognizes the importance of adult education, workforce skills, and family literacy programs; and
**(4)**
calls on public, private, and nonprofit entities to support increased access to adult education and family literacy programs to ensure a fully literate society.
