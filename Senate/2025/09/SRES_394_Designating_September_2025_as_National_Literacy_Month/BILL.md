III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 394
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 16, 2025 Mr. Reed (for himself, Mr. Cassidy , Mrs. Blackburn , Ms. Blunt Rochester , Mrs. Capito , Mr. Cornyn , Mr. Durbin , Mr. Heinrich , Ms. Hirono , Mr. Justice , Mrs. Gillibrand , Mrs. Britt , Mr. King , Mr. Lankford , Mr. Tuberville , Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. Whitehouse , Mr. Husted , Mr. Wicker , and Ms. Collins ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating September 2025 as National Literacy Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates September 2025 as National Literacy Month ; and
**(2)**
calls on the Federal Government, States, localities, schools, libraries, nonprofit organizations, businesses, and the people of the United States to observe National Literacy Month with appropriate programs and activities.
