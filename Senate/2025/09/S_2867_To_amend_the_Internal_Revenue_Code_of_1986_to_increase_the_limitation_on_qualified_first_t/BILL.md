September 18 (legislative day, September 16), 2025 Mr. Gallego (for himself and Mr. Young ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the limitation on qualified first-time homebuyer distributions, and for other purposes.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Uplifting First-Time Homebuyers Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Increasing the limitation on qualified first-time homebuyer distributions
##### (a) In general
Section 72(t)(8)(B)(i) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking $10,000 and inserting $50,000 .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by this Act shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2024.
