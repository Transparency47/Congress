State median income, the fee under this subsection shall not exceed 6 percent of the family income.\n**(G)**\nWith respect to a child who is in a family with a family income that is more than 150 percent of the State median income, the fee under this subsection shall not exceed 7 percent of the family income.\n**(3) Limitation**\nWith respect to a child who is in a family with a family income irrespective of the number of days of care provided per week.\n**(4) Fee percentage applicable regardless of number of children served**\nThe total fee for a family that is subject to the fee under than the fee allowed under paragraphs (2) and (3).\n##### (k) Parent boards\nThe prime sponsor shall require the establishment, at each child care and early learning center, of a board of parents, to
