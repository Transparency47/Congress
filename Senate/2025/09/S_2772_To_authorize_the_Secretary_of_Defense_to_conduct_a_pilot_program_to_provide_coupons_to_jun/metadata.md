or\n**(II)**\nto pay any deposit fee in excess of the amount of the State fee reimbursement (if any) required to purchase any food or food product contained in a returnable bottle or can, without regard to whether the fee is included in the shelf price posted for the food or food product.\n**(C) Supplement to other food assistance**\nA coupon provided to a member under the pilot program authorized by paragraph (1) shall be supplement and not supplant\u2014\n**(i)**\nthe basic allowance for subsistence under section 402 of title 37, United States Code;
