S. CON. RES. 21

September 3, 2025 Mr. Scott of Florida submitted the following concurrent resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary

Denouncing the horrors of socialism.
That Congress denounces socialism in all its forms, and opposes the implementation of socialist policies in the United States.
