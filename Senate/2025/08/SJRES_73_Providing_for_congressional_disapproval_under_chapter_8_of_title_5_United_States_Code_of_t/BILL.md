IIA
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. J. RES. 73
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
August 1, 2025 Mr. Whitehouse introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to National Volatile Organic Compound Emission Standards for Aerosol Coatings: Interim Final Rule .
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to National Volatile Organic Compound Emission Standards for Aerosol Coatings: Interim Final Rule (90 Fed. Reg. 28904 (July 2, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
