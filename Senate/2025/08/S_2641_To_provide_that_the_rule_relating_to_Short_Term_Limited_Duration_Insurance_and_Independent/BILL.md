119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 2641
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
August 1, 2025 Mr. Johnson introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
A BILL
To provide that the rule relating to Short-Term, Limited-Duration Insurance and Independent, Noncoordinated Excepted Benefits Coverage shall have no force or effect.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Health Care Freedom and Choice Act .
#### 2. Short-term, limited duration insurance rule nullification
The final interagency rule published by the Internal Revenue Service, the Employee Benefits Security Administration, and the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services relating to Short-Term, Limited-Duration Insurance and Independent, Noncoordinated Excepted Benefits Coverage (89 Fed. Reg. 23338 (April 3, 2024)) shall have no force or effect.
