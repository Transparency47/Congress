## Summary

**Protecting Access to Ground Ambulance Medical Services Act of 2025**

This bill extends a specialized rate increase for Medicare payment of ground ambulance services in rural areas until January 1, 2028, and provides for a higher rate increase for services furnished between October 1, 2025, and January 1, 2028.

A BILL
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to protect patient access to ground ambulance services under the Medicare program.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protecting Access to Ground Ambulance Medical Services Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Protecting patient access to Medicare ground ambulance services
##### (a) Super rural ambulance
Section 1834(l)(12)(A) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395m(l)(12)(A) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in the first sentence, by striking October 1, 2025 and inserting January 1, 2028 ; and
**(2)**
by adding at the end the following new sentence: In the case of services furnished on or after October 1, 2025, and before January 1, 2028, such percent increase shall be 26.7 percent. .
##### (b) Ground ambulance
Section 1834(l)(13)(A) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395m(l)(13)(A) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking October 1, 2025 and inserting January 1, 2028 ;
**(2)**
in clause (i), by inserting , or 4.3 percent if such service is furnished on or after October 1, 2025, and before January 1, 2028 after 2025 ; and
**(3)**
in clause (ii), by inserting , or 3.4 percent if such service is furnished on or after October 1, 2025, and before January 1, 2028 after 2025 .
