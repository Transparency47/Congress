IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 8, 2025 Mr. Kelly (for himself and Mr. Gallego ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions
A BILL
To amend the Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Act of 1981 to improve the formula for allotments to States.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the LIHEAP Parity Act .
#### 2. Allotments
Section 2604(a)(2) of the Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Act of 1981 ( 42 U.S.C. 8623(a)(2) ) is amended by striking , except that States and all that follows and inserting a period.
#### 3. Regulations
##### (a) Calculation
The Secretary of Health and Human Services shall issue a regulation that specifies the method to be used to calculate State allotments under section 2604(a)(2) of the Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Act of 1981, consistent with the amendment made by section 2, ensuring that the method will accurately allot the funds involved.
##### (b) Data sources
In issuing the regulation, the Secretary shall specify—
**(1)**
the data sources to be used by Administration for Children and Families to make the State allotments; and
**(2)**
how often the data sources shall be updated, and means of updating the data sources sufficiently, to ensure the accuracy of the State allotments.
