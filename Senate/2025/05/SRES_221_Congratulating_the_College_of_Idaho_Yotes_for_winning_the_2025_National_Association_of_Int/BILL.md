III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 221
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 13, 2025 Mr. Crapo (for himself and Mr. Risch ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the College of Idaho Yotes for winning the 2025 National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics Men’s Basketball National Championship.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
congratulates the College of Idaho Yotes for winning the 2025 National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics Men’s Basketball National Championship;
**(2)**
recognizes the achievements of the players, coaches, support staff, faculty, and fans who were instrumental in the College of Idaho’s victory; and
**(3)**
respectfully requests the Secretary of the Senate to transmit a copy of this resolution for appropriate display to—
**(A)**
the president of the College of Idaho, Doug Brigham;
**(B)**
the vice president of athletics of the College of Idaho, Reagan Rossi; and
**(C)**
the head coach of the College of Idaho men’s basketball team, Colby Blaine.
