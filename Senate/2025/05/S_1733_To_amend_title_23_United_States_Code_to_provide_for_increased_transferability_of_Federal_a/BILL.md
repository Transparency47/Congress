119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 1733
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 13, 2025 Mr. Cramer (for himself and Ms. Alsobrooks ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works
A BILL
To amend title 23, United States Code, to provide for increased transferability of Federal-aid highway funds.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Highway Funding Transferability Improvement Act .
#### 2. Transferability of Federal-aid highway funds
Section 126(a) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by striking 50 percent and inserting 75 percent .
