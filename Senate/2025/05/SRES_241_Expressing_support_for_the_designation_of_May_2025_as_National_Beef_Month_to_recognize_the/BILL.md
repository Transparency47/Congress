119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 241
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 21, 2025 Mr. Ricketts (for himself, Mr. Marshall , Mrs. Fischer , and Mr. Cornyn ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of May 2025 as National Beef Month to recognize the important role cattle play in the United States, and to consumers.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the designation of May 2025 as National Beef Month ; and
**(2)**
recognizes that—
**(A)**
historically, cattle production has contributed about 17 percent of the $520,000,000,000 in total cash receipts for agricultural commodities;
**(B)**
the United States is also the largest consumer of beef in the world, primarily high-value, grain-fed beef; and
**(C)**
beef is an excellent source of nutritious protein.
