119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 1800
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
May 19, 2025 Mrs. Hyde-Smith introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to modify the criteria for designation of rural emergency hospitals.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Rural Health Sustainability Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Modification of criteria for designation of rural emergency hospitals
Section 1861(kkk)(3) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1395x(kkk)(3) ) is amended—
**(1)**
in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking as of the date of the enactment of this subsection and inserting as of January 1, 2014 ;
**(2)**
in subparagraph (A), by striking or at the end;
**(3)**
in subparagraph (B), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; or ; and
**(4)**
by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(C) was designated as rural by the Office of Rural Health Policy of the Health Resources and Services Administration. .
