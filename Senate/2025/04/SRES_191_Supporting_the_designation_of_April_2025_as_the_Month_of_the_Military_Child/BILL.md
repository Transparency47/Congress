III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 191
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 30, 2025 Mrs. Murray (for herself, Mr. Boozman , Ms. Rosen , and Mr. Hoeven ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Supporting the designation of April 2025 as the Month of the Military Child .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
supports the designation of April 2025 as the Month of the Military Child ; and
**(2)**
urges the people of the United States to observe the Month of the Military Child with appropriate ceremonies and activities that honor, support, and show appreciation for military children.
