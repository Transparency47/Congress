Such notification shall include information about the circumstances surrounding the death, including the official time of death, the cause of death, and whether the death is under investigation, including the reason for opening the investigation.\n**(B) Notification of serious illness or serious injury**\nIn the event an individual becomes seriously ill or seriously injured while in the custody of a detention agency, the detention agency shall attempt to notify the emergency contact of the individual as soon as practicable after the serious injury or serious illness occurs.
