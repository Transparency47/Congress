April 10, 2025 Mr. Scott of South Carolina (for himself and Ms. Warren ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
A BILL
To extend certain authorities under the Defense Production Act of 1950.
#### 1. Extension of Defense Production Act of 1950
Section 717(a) of the Defense Production Act of 1950 ( 50 U.S.C. 4564(a) ) is amended by striking September 30, 2025 and inserting September 30, 2026 .
