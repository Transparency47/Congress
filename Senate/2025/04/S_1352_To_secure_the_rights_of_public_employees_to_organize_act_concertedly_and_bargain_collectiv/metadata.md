Short title\nThis Act may be cited as the Public Service Freedom to Negotiate Act of 2025 .\n#### 2. Definitions\n##### (a) In general\nIn this Act:\n**(1) Appropriate unit**\nThe term appropriate unit means a group of public employees or a group of supervisory employees appropriate for collective bargaining that share a community of interest, as demonstrated by factors including whether such group\u2014\n**(A)**\nhas a bargaining history or history of prior organization; or\n**(iii)**\neffectively recommend any action described in clause (i) or (ii); and\n**(B)**\ndevotes a majority of time at work to exercising the authority under subparagraph (A).\n##### (b) Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 terms\nThe terms commerce , employ , enterprise engaged in commerce , and State have the meanings given such terms in section 3 of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 203 ).\n##### (c) State law\nIf any term defined in this section has a substantially equivalent meaning to a term (or a substantially equivalent term) under applicable State law on the date of the enactment of this Act, such term (or substantially equivalent term) and meaning under such applicable State law shall apply with respect to the term defined under this Act with respect to such State.\n#### 3.
