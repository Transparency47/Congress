119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 163
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 9, 2025 Mr. Bennet (for himself and Mr. Hickenlooper ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Recognizing the contributions of Clela Rorex, a pioneering county clerk who, in 1975, advanced civil rights for all couples seeking to be married.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes—
**(A)**
the contributions of Clela Rorex as a pioneer for civil rights and same-sex marriage;
**(B)**
the respect and bravery Clela Rorex demonstrated when issuing the first same-sex marriage license in the United States; and
**(C)**
the courage Clela Rorex exhibited following the threats she received when she issued marriage licenses to same-sex couples; and
**(2)**
designates March 26, 2025, as Clela Rorex Day .
