April 9, 2025 Mr. Boozman introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the number of eligible shareholders of an S corporation.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the S-Corporation Additional Participation Act of 2025 or the S-CAP Act of 2025 .
#### 2. Increase in number of shareholders to qualify as an S corporation
##### (a) In general
Section 1361(b)(1)(A) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking 100 and inserting 250 .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
