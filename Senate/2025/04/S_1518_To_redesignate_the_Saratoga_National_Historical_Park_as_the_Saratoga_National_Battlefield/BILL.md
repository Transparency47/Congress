## Summary

**Strengthening America’s Turning Point Act**

This bill renames the Saratoga National Historical Park, located in Stillwater, New York, as Saratoga National Battlefield Park.

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 29, 2025 Mrs. Gillibrand introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources
A BILL
To redesignate the Saratoga National Historical Park as the Saratoga National Battlefield Park .
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Strengthening America’s Turning Point Act .
#### 2. Saratoga National Battlefield Park
##### (a) Redesignation
The Saratoga National Historical Park shall be known and designated as the Saratoga National Battlefield Park .
##### (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Saratoga National Historical Park shall be deemed to be a reference to the Saratoga National Battlefield Park .
