April 1 (legislative day, March 31), 2025 Mr. Paul introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the clean vehicle credit.
#### 1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the End Taxpayer Subsidies for Electric Vehicles Act .
#### 2. Repeal of clean vehicle credit
##### (a) In general
Subpart B of part IV of subchapter A of chapter 1 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking section 30D (and by striking the item relating to such section in the table of sections for such subpart).
