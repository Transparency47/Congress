III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 165
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 9, 2025 Mr. Sanders submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs
RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the Senate that the plan of President Trump and Elon Musk to fire 83,000 employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs is unacceptable and must be rescinded.
That it is the sense of the Senate that the Department of Veterans Affairs must immediately reject and rescind its Agency Reduction in Force and Reorganization Plan.
