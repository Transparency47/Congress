119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 154
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 3, 2025 Mr. Markey submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the Senate that Donald Trump is ineligible in any future elections to be elected Vice-President or President, or to serve as President beyond the conclusion of his current term.
That it is the sense of the Senate that Donald Trump is ineligible in any future elections to be elected Vice-President or President, or to serve as President beyond the conclusion of his current term.
