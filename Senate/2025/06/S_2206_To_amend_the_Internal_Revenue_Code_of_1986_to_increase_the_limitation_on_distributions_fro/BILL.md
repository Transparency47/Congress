IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 30, 2025 Mr. Schmitt introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance
A BILL
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the limitation on distributions from 529 accounts for qualified higher education expenses.
#### 1. Increase in limitation for qualified higher education expenses from 529 accounts
##### (a) In general
The last sentence of section 529(e)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking $10,000 and inserting $20,000 .
##### (b) Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
