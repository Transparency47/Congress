119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 293
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 18, 2025 Ms. Klobuchar (for herself and Ms. Smith ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Commending the Minnesota Frost for winning the 2025 Professional Women’s Hockey League Championship.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
commends the Minnesota Frost for winning the 2025 Professional Women’s Hockey League Championship, their second straight championship;
**(2)**
recognizes the dedication, perseverance, and hard work of the players, coaches, and staff of the Minnesota Frost in winning the championship;
**(3)**
recognizes the difficulty of winning 2 straight championship titles in any league or sport; and
**(4)**
congratulates and thanks the fans, players, coaches, and staff of the Minnesota Frost for a great 2024–2025 season.
