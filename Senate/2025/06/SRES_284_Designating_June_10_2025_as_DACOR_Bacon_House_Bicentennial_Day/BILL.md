III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 284
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 17, 2025 Mr. Whitehouse (for himself and Mr. Wicker ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating June 10, 2025, as DACOR Bacon House Bicentennial Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
recognizes the historic importance of the DACOR Bacon House to the history of the United States, including American diplomacy, the Supreme Court, and Congress;
**(2)**
applauds the DACOR Bacon House Foundation and the Diplomatic and Consular Officers, Retired organization for their decades-long efforts to preserve this historic landmark located in the heart of the capital of the United States; and
**(3)**
designates June 10, 2025, as DACOR Bacon House Bicentennial Day .
