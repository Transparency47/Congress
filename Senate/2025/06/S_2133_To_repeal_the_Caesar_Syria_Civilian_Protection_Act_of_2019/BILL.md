119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. 2133
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 18, 2025 Mrs. Shaheen (for herself and Mr. Paul ) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Foreign Relations
A BILL
To repeal the Caesar Syria Civilian Protection Act of 2019.
#### 1. Repeal
The Caesar Syria Civilian Protection Act of 2019 (title LXXIV of division F of Public Law 116–92 ; 22 U.S.C. 8791 note) is hereby repealed.
