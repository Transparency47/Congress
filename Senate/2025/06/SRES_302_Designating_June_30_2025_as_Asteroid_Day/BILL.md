III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 302
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 25 (legislative day, June 24), 2025 Mr. Kelly (for himself and Mr. Cornyn ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Designating June 30, 2025 as Asteroid Day .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates June 30, 2025 as Asteroid Day ; and
**(2)**
encourages increased public awareness about the risks posed by asteroids and promotes understanding of the importance of asteroid research and planetary defense.
