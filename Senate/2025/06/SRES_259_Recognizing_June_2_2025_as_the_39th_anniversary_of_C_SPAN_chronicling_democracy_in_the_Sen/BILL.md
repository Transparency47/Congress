119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 259
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 2, 2025 Mr. Grassley (for himself, Ms. Klobuchar , Mr. Wyden , and Mr. Young ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Recognizing June 2, 2025, as the 39th anniversary of C-SPAN chronicling democracy in the Senate.
That the Senate recognizes—
**(1)**
June 2, 2025, as the 39th anniversary of C-SPAN chronicling democracy in the Senate; and
**(2)**
the importance of continuous Senate coverage for all Americans and the need for live coverage to be accessible on all platforms.
