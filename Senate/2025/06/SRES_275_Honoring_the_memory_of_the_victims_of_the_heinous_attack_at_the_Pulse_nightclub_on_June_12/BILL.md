119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 275
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 12, 2025 Mr. Scott of Florida (for himself and Mrs. Moody ) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary
RESOLUTION
Honoring the memory of the victims of the heinous attack at the Pulse nightclub on June 12, 2016.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
commemorates the 49 innocent victims killed in the attack at the Pulse nightclub in Orlando, Florida, on June 12, 2016, and offers heartfelt condolences to the families, loved ones, and friends of the victims;
**(2)**
honors the dozens of survivors of the attack and pledges continued resolve to stand against terrorism and hate; and
**(3)**
expresses gratitude to the brave law enforcement and emergency medical personnel who responded to the attack.
