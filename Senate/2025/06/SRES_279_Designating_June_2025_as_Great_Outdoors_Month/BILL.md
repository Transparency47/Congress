III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 279
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 16, 2025 Mr. Daines (for himself, Mr. Peters , Mr. King , Mr. Hickenlooper , Mr. Welch , Mr. Warner , Mrs. Shaheen , Mr. Van Hollen , Mr. Wyden , and Mr. Marshall ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Designating June 2025 as Great Outdoors Month .
That the Senate—
**(1)**
designates June 2025 as Great Outdoors Month ; and
**(2)**
encourages all individuals in the United States to responsibly participate in recreation activities in the great outdoors during June 2025 and year-round.
