III
119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 298
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 24, 2025 Mr. Lankford (for himself and Mr. Mullin ) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Congratulating the Oklahoma State University men’s golf team for winning the 2025 National Collegiate Athletic Association Division I Men's Golf National Championship.
That the Senate—
**(1)**
commends the 2025 Oklahoma State University men’s golf team (referred to in this resolution as the Cowboys ) for winning the 2025 National Collegiate Athletic Association Division I Men's Golf National Championship;
**(2)**
recognizes the Cowboys for their hard work and dedication to the sport of golf and for the excitement they bring to Oklahoma State University, the State of Oklahoma, and to fans of the Cowboys everywhere; and
**(3)**
congratulates the Cowboys on a wonderful 2025 season.
